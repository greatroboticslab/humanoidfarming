Task: 3-Point Middle Buster
Please break it into step-by-step subtasks. Provide numbered subtasks with concise justifications.
------------------------------------------------------------
1) Stand in front of your opponent, positioning yourself slightly to their right.
2) Step back with your left foot while simultaneously jumping forward with your right foot.
3) As you land, pivot your body to face diagonally towards your opponent.
4) Extend your right hand forward, palm facing down, as if giving a handshake.
5) Immediately follow through with your left hand, extending it downward and inward.
6) Land on both feet, maintaining balance and preparing for an attack or defense.
