Task: How to Train a Houseplant to Climb a Wall! (It's so simple it's unbelievable!)
Please break it into step-by-step subtasks. Provide numbered subtasks with concise justifications.
------------------------------------------------------------
1) Choose the right climbing plant for your wall.
2) Select a sturdy, vertical support like a trellis or fence.
3) Tie the plant gently to the support using soft ties.
4) Place the plant near the top of the support and let it grow naturally.
5) Regularly prune the plant to encourage new growth and maintain shape.
6) Water the plant regularly and provide adequate sunlight.
7) Monitor the plant for signs of pests or diseases and address them promptly.
8) Continue to support the plant as it grows taller, adjusting ties as needed.
9) Once the plant reaches the desired height, trim back any excess foliage.
10) Enjoy watching your houseplant climb its way up the wall!
