Task: Ministry of Agriculture partners with TTM on okra cultivation techniques
Please break it into step-by-step subtasks. Focus on safety, measurement units, and necessary tools.
------------------------------------------------------------
1) Identify suitable okra varieties for local climate conditions.
2) Conduct soil analysis to determine nutrient levels and pH balance.
3) Purchase necessary tools including trowels, watering cans, and stakes.
4) Set up experimental plots measuring 10 meters by 10 meters each.
5) Apply recommended fertilizers based on soil test results in measured quantities.
6) Install irrigation systems using gallons per minute as the flow rate standard.
7) Train farmers on safe pesticide application techniques and proper disposal methods.
8) Monitor crop growth weekly, taking photos for documentation purposes.
9) Measure plant height and leaf area index at specific intervals for data collection.
10) Collect yield data at harvest time, noting any unusual occurrences for analysis.
