Task: Harvest Season 2023
Please break it into step-by-step subtasks. Focus on safety, measurement units, and necessary tools.
------------------------------------------------------------
1) Assess local weather forecasts for potential hazards during harvest season.
2) Ensure all machinery is serviced and in good working condition.
3) Gather necessary safety gear including helmets and protective clothing.
4) Measure fields accurately using GPS or traditional methods for planning.
5) Equip workers with appropriate personal protective equipment before starting.
6) Divide work areas to minimize exposure time and increase efficiency.
7) Use calibrated scales for precise weighing of harvested crops.
8) Prepare first aid kits and emergency contact information for immediate use.
9) Plan water supply routes to prevent machinery from running dry unexpectedly.
10) Train workers on proper handling techniques to avoid injury or damage to equipment.
