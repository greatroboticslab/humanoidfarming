Task: How To Introduce A New Hen To The Hen House
Please break it into step-by-step subtasks. Provide numbered subtasks with concise justifications.
------------------------------------------------------------
1) Assess the current hen house environment for safety and cleanliness.
2) Choose a calm day to introduce the new hen to prevent stress.
3) Place the new hen in a small, enclosed area within the main house.
4) Offer her food and water from a separate feeder and waterer.
5) Keep an eye on her for signs of distress or aggression.
6) Gradually increase her exposure to the main hen house over several days.
7) Introduce her to other hens one at a time, observing for any signs of conflict.
8) Ensure she has access to a quiet space where she can rest and adjust.
9) Monitor her behavior closely for the first week to ensure integration.
10) Provide regular health checks to address any issues promptly.
