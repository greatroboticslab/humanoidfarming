Task: Farm Tractor Tire Repair at Home
Please break it into step-by-step subtasks. Include validation steps and fallback procedures.
------------------------------------------------------------
1)  Gather necessary tools including a tire iron, lug wrench, spare tire, jack, and replacement tire.
2)  Ensure the tractor is parked on a flat surface away from any obstacles.
3)  Loosen the lug nuts but do not remove them to keep the wheel off the ground.
4)  Use the jack to lift the front or rear of the tractor until the tire is off the ground.
5)  Remove the lug nuts and carefully take off the damaged tire.
6)  Inspect the tire for punctures, cuts, or other damage that can be repaired.
7)  If the tire has a puncture, use a patch kit to repair it according to the instructions.
8)  If the tire is severely damaged, replace it with a new one.
9)  Clean the rim and apply tire sealant if the tire was punctured.
10)  Reinstall the new tire onto the wheel and tighten the lug nuts in a star pattern.
11)  Lower the tractor and recheck the lug nuts for tightness.
12)  Test drive the tractor to ensure the tire is properly seated and functioning correctly.
