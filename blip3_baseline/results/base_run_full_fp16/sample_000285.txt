Task: Commercial Maize Farming in Ghana for Beginners 2024
Please break it into step-by-step subtasks. Provide numbered subtasks with concise justifications.
------------------------------------------------------------
1)  Research local climate conditions suitable for maize cultivation.
2)  Identify local agricultural extension services for guidance.
3)  Purchase certified seed varieties from reputable suppliers.
4)  Plan crop rotation to maintain soil fertility.
5)  Prepare land by plowing and removing weeds.
6)  Apply organic fertilizers or compost to enrich the soil.
7)  Install drip irrigation systems for efficient water usage.
8)  Plant maize seeds at recommended intervals and depths.
9)  Monitor plant growth regularly for signs of pests or diseases.
10)  Harvest maize when ears turn brown and dry.
11)  Store harvested maize in cool, dry places to prevent spoilage.
