Task: 15. IRRI Agronomy Challenge: When to Harvest (19 April 2012)
Please break it into step-by-step subtasks. Focus on safety, measurement units, and necessary tools.
------------------------------------------------------------
1) Assess plant maturity visually for leaves turning yellow and stems hardening.
2) Measure plant height using a ruler or measuring tape.
3) Check soil moisture content with a soil moisture meter.
4) Evaluate yield potential based on plant density and growth stage.
5) Consider local weather forecasts for optimal harvest timing.
6) Use appropriate personal protective equipment before entering fields.
7) Wear gloves and sturdy footwear for field work safety.
8) Carry a water bottle and sunscreen for hydration and sun protection.
9) Have a first aid kit readily available in case of minor injuries.
10) Plan transportation routes to ensure timely delivery of harvested crops.
