Task: Harvesting Sweet Potatoes at Don's Produce
Please break it into step-by-step subtasks. Emphasize precision, sustainability, and error checks.
------------------------------------------------------------
1)  Wear appropriate protective gear including gloves and sturdy shoes.
2)  Inspect the sweet potato fields for ripe plants using a hand-held flashlight if necessary.
3)  Use a sharp spading fork to carefully dig up the sweet potatoes without damaging them.
4)  Sort the harvested sweet potatoes by size and condition, discarding any damaged or diseased ones.
5)  Place the sorted sweet potatoes in a clean, dry area to air-cure for about 24 hours before storage.
6)  Store sweet potatoes in a cool, dark place with good air circulation to prevent sprouting.
7)  Monitor the storage conditions regularly to ensure they remain optimal for consumption.
8)  After harvest, cover the soil around the plants to protect the roots from exposure and pests.
9)  Avoid over-harvesting to maintain soil fertility and plant health for future crops.
10)  Dispose of harvested sweet potatoes and plant debris responsibly, avoiding contamination of water sources.
