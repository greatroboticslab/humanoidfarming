Task: Conservation Practices Commonly Used in Animal Agriculture
Please break it into step-by-step subtasks. Focus on safety, measurement units, and necessary tools.
------------------------------------------------------------
1) Identify specific conservation practices relevant to animal agriculture.
2) Assess the safety of these practices for both animals and workers.
3) Determine appropriate measurement units for each practice.
4) Select necessary tools based on the chosen practices.
5) Implement the conservation practices following safety guidelines.
6) Regularly measure the effectiveness of implemented practices.
7) Adjust practices as needed based on measurements and feedback.
