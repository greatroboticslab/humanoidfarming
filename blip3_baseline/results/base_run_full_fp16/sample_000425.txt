1) Identify the specific crop or agricultural product needing harvest.
2) Check weather conditions for safety and efficiency during harvesting.
3) Gather necessary tools and machinery suitable for the crop type.
4) Plan the harvesting route to avoid damaging crops or equipment.
5) Train workers on proper use of machinery and safety protocols.
6) Start harvesting in sections, moving from one area to another systematically.
7) Monitor crop quality and adjust harvesting techniques as needed.
8) Regularly clean and maintain machinery to ensure optimal performance.

10) Document the harvesting process for future reference and improvement.
