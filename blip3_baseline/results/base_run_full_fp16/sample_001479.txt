Task: Extension program helps producers improve their operations
Please break it into step-by-step subtasks. Focus on safety, measurement units, and necessary tools.
------------------------------------------------------------
1) Identify specific areas needing improvement in producer's operations.
2) Develop safety guidelines tailored to identified areas.
3) Establish clear measurement units for all operations.
4) Gather necessary tools based on identified needs.
5) Train producers on safe use of new tools and equipment.
6) Implement monitoring systems to track progress and safety.
7) Regularly review and adjust programs based on feedback and outcomes.
