Task: Dryland Farming
Please break it into step-by-step subtasks. Focus on safety, measurement units, and necessary tools.
------------------------------------------------------------
1) Assess soil moisture levels using a soil moisture meter to ensure adequate dryness before planting.
2) Measure crop spacing in meters for even distribution of plants.
3) Use a tiller or tractor to prepare the land, ensuring proper soil preparation.
4) Apply organic fertilizers according to package instructions for balanced nutrition.
5) Install drip irrigation systems to conserve water efficiently.
6) Monitor weather forecasts daily to avoid planting during extreme conditions.
7) Wear protective gear such as gloves and goggles when handling chemicals.
8) Measure and mark rows accurately with stakes and string for straight planting lines.
9) Plant seeds at recommended depths and spacings based on seed packet guidelines.
10) Water crops gently with a watering can or sprinkler system immediately after planting.
11) Regularly check for pests and diseases using magnifying glasses or visual inspections.
12) Harvest crops when ripe, ensuring timely removal to prevent overgrowth.
