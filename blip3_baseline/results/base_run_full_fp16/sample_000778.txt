Task: Sustainable agriculture
Please break it into step-by-step subtasks. Focus on safety, measurement units, and necessary tools.
------------------------------------------------------------
1) Identify sustainable agriculture practices suitable for local climate and soil conditions.
2) Assess water availability using gallons or liters per acre.
3) Measure land area in acres or hectares for accurate resource allocation.
4) Determine crop yields in bushels, tons, or kilograms per hectare.
5) Calculate fertilizer needs based on soil tests in pounds per acre.
6) Use pH meters to test soil acidity in units of pH.
7) Implement crop rotation plans to enhance soil health over years.
8) Install drip irrigation systems to conserve water in gallons per minute.
9) Monitor pest populations through visual inspections or traps.
10) Apply organic pesticides measured in grams per liter of solution.
11) Set up solar-powered lighting systems for night-time monitoring.
12) Regularly collect data on energy consumption in kilowatt-hours.
