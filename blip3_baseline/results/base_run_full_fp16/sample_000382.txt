Task: Soil Amendment Myths | From the Ground Up
Please break it into step-by-step subtasks. Focus on safety, measurement units, and necessary tools.
------------------------------------------------------------
1) Identify the type of soil amendment needed based on plant needs.
2) Measure out the correct amount using a cup or scale for precision.
3) Wear gloves to protect hands from harmful chemicals during application.
4) Choose appropriate tools like a rake or trowel for spreading evenly.
5) Apply amendments at recommended depth according to product instructions.
6) Water the area thoroughly after applying amendments for even distribution.
7) Avoid over-application to prevent nutrient buildup and potential toxicity.
