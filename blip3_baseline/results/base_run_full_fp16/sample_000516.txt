Task: Collecting Soil Samples | From the Ground Up

1) Choose an appropriate location based on soil type and environmental factors.
2) Wear protective gear including gloves and sturdy shoes.
3) Use a trowel or spade to carefully dig a small hole about 10 inches deep.
4) Carefully remove soil from the hole without disturbing underlying layers.
5) Place collected soil in a clean container for analysis.
6) Mark the sample location with a flag or stake for reference.
7) Repeat process at multiple locations within the study area.
