Task: Young Farmer : planting corn.#HarmonyWithEarth#CultivatingWisdom#SustainableHarvest
Please break it into step-by-step subtasks. Focus on safety, measurement units, and necessary tools.
------------------------------------------------------------
1) Choose a safe location for planting corn, ensuring it receives adequate sunlight and has well-draining soil.
2) Measure the required space in square meters, using a measuring tape or ruler.
3) Gather necessary tools such as a shovel, rake, and watering can.
4) Prepare the soil by turning over the top layer with a shovel to improve aeration and remove weeds.
5) Use a rake to smooth out the soil surface after turning.
6) Calculate the number of seeds needed based on recommended spacing guidelines.
7) Plant the seeds at the appropriate depth, typically about one inch deep.
8) Water the planted seeds thoroughly to ensure they are moistened.
9) Monitor the growth regularly, applying more water as needed to maintain consistent moisture levels.
10) Apply organic fertilizer according to package instructions to promote healthy plant growth.
11) Harvest corn when the kernels are fully developed and turn golden yellow, usually around 2-3 months after planting.
12) Dispose of any leftover plants safely, following local regulations on composting or disposal.
