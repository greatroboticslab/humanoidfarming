Task: Mechanical and Machinery hazards | Contact With Moving Parts of Equipment
Please break it into step-by-step subtasks. Emphasize precision, sustainability, and error checks.
------------------------------------------------------------
1)  Identify all moving parts in the machinery or equipment.
2)  Assess the potential for contact with these moving parts.
3)  Apply appropriate safety measures to prevent contact.
4)  Regularly inspect equipment for any signs of wear or damage affecting moving parts.
5)  Train operators on safe operating procedures involving moving parts.
6)  Implement ergonomic designs to reduce the risk of accidental contact.
7)  Ensure all employees are aware of emergency stop mechanisms near moving parts.
8)  Maintain equipment according to manufacturer guidelines to prevent malfunctioning moving parts.
9)  Conduct regular maintenance checks focusing on moving parts.
10)  Educate workers about the importance of personal protective equipment (PPE) when handling moving parts.
