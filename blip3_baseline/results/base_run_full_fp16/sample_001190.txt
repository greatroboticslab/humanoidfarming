Task: Seeding Tomatoes
Please break it into step-by-step subtasks. Provide numbered subtasks with concise justifications.
------------------------------------------------------------
1) Choose suitable tomato seeds based on local climate and soil conditions.
2) Prepare seed starting trays or pots with well-draining potting mix.
3) Plant seeds at recommended depth according to seed packet instructions.
4) Water gently but thoroughly, ensuring soil is moist but not waterlogged.
5) Place trays in a warm location with indirect sunlight for optimal germination.
6) Monitor temperature and humidity levels to prevent mold growth.
7) Once seedlings emerge, thin them out to ensure proper spacing between plants.
8) Gradually acclimate seedlings to outdoor conditions over several days.
9) Transplant seedlings into garden beds or containers after all danger of frost has passed.
10) Water deeply and regularly, maintaining consistent moisture levels.
