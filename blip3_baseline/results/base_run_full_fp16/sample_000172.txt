Task: Growing Food in Urban Small Spaces - Urban Gardening

------------------------------------------------------------
1)  Assess available space for gardening.
2)  Choose appropriate plants suitable for small spaces.
3)  Select containers or vertical gardens if space is limited.
4)  Determine sunlight exposure of the chosen location.
5)  Plan irrigation system based on plant needs.
6)  Start seeds indoors or purchase seedlings from local nurseries.
7)  Prepare soil mix according to plant requirements.
8)  Plant seeds or seedlings following instructions.
9)  Water plants regularly according to their needs.
10)  Monitor for pests and diseases, implement natural remedies.
11)  Harvest plants as they mature.
12)  Rotate crops to maintain soil health and prevent pest buildup.
13)  Evaluate success and adjust gardening practices accordingly.
14)  Share gardening tips with neighbors or join urban gardening groups.
15)  Consider composting food waste for additional nutrient-rich soil.
