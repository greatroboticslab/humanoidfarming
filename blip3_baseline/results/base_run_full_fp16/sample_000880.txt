Task: Cleaning The Stable | Feeding Cows With Hay

------------------------------------------------------------
1) Open the stable doors to allow fresh air circulation.
2) Check for any sick or injured cows needing immediate attention.
3) Gather hay from storage area and distribute evenly across the stable floor.
4) Lead each cow individually to its designated feeding spot.
5) Place a portion of hay in front of each cow, ensuring they have enough space.
6) Observe cows as they eat to ensure they are consuming properly.
7) Remove uneaten hay after 30 minutes to prevent spoilage.
8) Clean up spilled hay and feces immediately to maintain cleanliness.
9) Close stable doors securely once all cows are fed and settled.
