Task: Automated shed covering process- Good tools and machinery can increase work efficiency
Please break it into step-by-step subtasks. Focus on safety, measurement units, and necessary tools.
------------------------------------------------------------
1) Assess the size of the shed to determine appropriate covering materials.
2) Measure dimensions accurately using a tape measure in feet and inches.
3) Ensure all workers wear protective gear before starting the task.
4) Gather necessary tools including ladders, scaffolding, and power tools.
5) Check machinery for proper functioning and safety features.
6) Lay out the covering material according to manufacturer's instructions.
7) Secure the covering with straps or clips at regular intervals.
8) Monitor weather conditions to avoid premature damage during installation.
9) Inspect the completed covering for any loose parts or gaps.
10) Document the process for future reference and quality control.
