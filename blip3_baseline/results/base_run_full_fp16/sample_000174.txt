Task: Grow Plant Based Protein in the Garden! | Vegetarian or Vegan Homestead
Please break it into step-by-step subtasks. Emphasize precision, sustainability, and error checks.
------------------------------------------------------------
1)  Choose a suitable location for your garden that receives at least six hours of sunlight daily.
2)  Prepare the soil by testing its pH level and adding compost to improve fertility.
3)  Select plant-based protein sources like beans, lentils, peas, and chickpeas based on local climate and availability.
4)  Start seeds indoors four to six weeks before the last expected frost date.
5)  Transplant seedlings outdoors when temperatures remain above 10°C (50°F).
6)  Rotate crops annually to prevent nutrient depletion and reduce pest and disease buildup.
7)  Water plants deeply but infrequently to encourage deep root growth and avoid waterlogging.
8)  Mulch around plants to retain moisture, suppress weeds, and regulate soil temperature.
9)  Practice companion planting to enhance growth and deter pests naturally.
10)  Harvest plants regularly to promote continuous production and prevent overgrowth.
11)  Monitor for signs of pests or diseases promptly and address them using organic methods.
12)  Compost plant waste and kitchen scraps to recycle nutrients back into the soil sustainably.
