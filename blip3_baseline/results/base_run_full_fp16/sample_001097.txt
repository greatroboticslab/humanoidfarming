Task: Charcoal retort in action
Please break it into step-by-step subtasks. Focus on safety, measurement units, and necessary tools.
------------------------------------------------------------
1) Gather all necessary tools including a charcoal retort, tongs, gloves, and a fire extinguisher.
2) Ensure the area is well-ventilated and free from flammable materials.
3) Light the charcoal retort using a long match or lighter.
4) Place the charcoal inside the retort, ensuring it's evenly distributed.
5) Close the lid of the retort and adjust the air vents for optimal combustion.
6) Monitor the temperature and adjust the vents as needed to maintain consistent heat.
7) Use tongs to handle hot objects near the retort, wearing appropriate protective gloves.
8) Keep a fire extinguisher nearby and ensure everyone knows how to use it.
9) Once finished, allow the retort to cool down before opening the lid.
10) Dispose of any remaining charcoal safely according to local regulations.
