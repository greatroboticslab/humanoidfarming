Develop and implement sustainable water management strategies for identified regions.
Educate communities about the importance of water conservation and efficient use.
Monitor progress and effectiveness of implemented strategies regularly.
Ensure that all interventions align with local environmental and socio-economic conditions.
Regularly update strategies based on new scientific findings and changing needs.
Implement policies and regulations to enforce water conservation measures effectively.
