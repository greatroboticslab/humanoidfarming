Task: Alabama Beef Systems Videos: How to Collect a Hay Sample
Please break it into step-by-step subtasks. Focus on safety, measurement units, and necessary tools.
------------------------------------------------------------
1) Wear protective gloves and goggles for handling hay samples.
2) Choose a clean, dry area away from dust and debris.
3) Use a clean, sharp knife or shears to cut the hay sample.
4) Measure the sample using a scale accurate to.
0) 1 kg.
5) Collect at least 1 kg of hay in a clean, labeled plastic bag.
6) Mix the sample thoroughly before taking the cuttings.
7) Label the bag clearly with date, location, and sample description.
8) Store the sample in a cool, dry place until testing.
9) Ensure all tools used are cleaned after sampling to avoid contamination.
