Task: Pesticidewise: minor pesticide spill
Please break it into step-by-step subtasks. Emphasize precision, sustainability, and error checks.
------------------------------------------------------------
1) Identify the type of minor pesticide spill for appropriate cleanup procedures.
2) Wear personal protective equipment including gloves, goggles, and a mask.
3) Close all windows and doors to prevent the spread of the pesticide.
4) Use absorbent materials like sand or sawdust to contain the spill.
5) Avoid using water as it can disperse the pesticide more widely.
6) If spill is on clothing, remove contaminated garments immediately.
7) Clean spill area with a solution of water and dish soap.
8) Dispose of absorbent materials and cleaning solutions according to local regulations.
9) Report the spill to your supervisor or designated safety officer.
10) Document the incident in the company's safety logbook for future reference.
