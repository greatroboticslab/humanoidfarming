Task: How to harvest and processing sunflower seeds | Agriculture Technology
Please break it into step-by-step subtasks. Provide numbered subtasks with concise justifications.
------------------------------------------------------------
1) Identify mature sunflowers for harvesting; these typically have brown or black seeds.
2) Cut down the sunflowers using a sharp tool to avoid damaging the seeds.
3) Remove the heads from the stalks and lay them out in a dry area to air dry.
4) Once dried, gently shake the heads to loosen the seeds from the petals.
5) Collect the seeds and separate them from the chaff using a screen or hand sorting.
6) Clean the seeds by removing any remaining debris or foreign material.
7) Store the cleaned seeds in a cool, dry place until ready for planting or consumption.
