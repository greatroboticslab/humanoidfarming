------------------------------------------------------------
1) Choose the right location based on sunlight needs.
2) Prepare soil by removing weeds and adding compost.
3) Dig holes according to plant size specifications.
4) Place plants in holes ensuring they stand at proper height.
5) Fill holes halfway with soil, then water lightly.
6) Add remaining soil, gently pressing down around roots.
7) Water thoroughly to settle soil and remove air pockets.
8) Mulch around plants for moisture retention and weed control.
9) Apply appropriate fertilizer as per plant type's needs.
