Please break it into step-by-step subtasks. Focus on safety, measurement units, and necessary tools.
------------------------------------------------------------
1) Wear gloves to protect your hands from thorns and irritants.
2) Use a hoe or hand trowel for precise weeding.
3) Identify common weeds like dandelions, clover, and bindweed.
4) Water the garden thoroughly before weeding to loosen soil.
5) Start weeding early in the morning or late in the evening.
6) Use a bucket to collect weeds for easy disposal.
7) Pull out weeds by their roots to prevent regrowth.
8) Apply mulch around plants to reduce weed growth.
9) Regularly check for new weeds and remove them promptly.
10) Consider using vinegar or boiling water as natural weed killers.
