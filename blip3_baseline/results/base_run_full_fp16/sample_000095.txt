Task: Tomato Seedling Potting: How to Repot for Thriving Growth!
Please break it into step-by-step subtasks. Emphasize precision, sustainability, and error checks.
------------------------------------------------------------
1)  Choose a suitable pot with adequate drainage holes.
2)  Fill the pot with well-draining potting mix.
3)  Gently remove the tomato seedling from its current container.
4)  Position the seedling in the new pot ensuring it is at the same depth as before.
5)  Fill around the seedling with fresh potting mix, pressing gently to secure.
6)  Water the newly potted tomato seedling thoroughly.
7)  Place the pot in a location receiving at least six hours of sunlight daily.
8)  Monitor soil moisture; avoid overwatering to prevent root rot.
9)  Apply balanced fertilizer every four weeks during growing season.
10)  Prune side shoots to encourage main stem growth and improve air circulation.
11)  Keep an eye out for pests and diseases, addressing them promptly.
12)  Gradually acclimate the plant to outdoor conditions before transplanting if necessary.
