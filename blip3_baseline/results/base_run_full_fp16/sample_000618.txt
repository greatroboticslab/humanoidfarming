Task: Training Video (Safe use of Pesticides)
Please break it into step-by-step subtasks. Focus on safety, measurement units, and necessary tools.
------------------------------------------------------------
1) Wear protective clothing including gloves, goggles, and a mask.
2) Read and understand all pesticide labels carefully.
3) Choose the appropriate pesticide based on crop type and pest.
4) Measure pesticides accurately using calibrated measuring cups or spoons.
5) Mix pesticides according to recommended ratios in a clean container.
6) Apply pesticides evenly following manufacturer instructions and weather conditions.
7) Dispose of unused pesticides safely as per local regulations.
8) Keep records of pesticide applications for future reference and compliance.
