1) Identify the end of the lay flat header pipe that needs plugging.
2) Choose an appropriate plug material like a rubber washer or a plastic cap.
3) Clean the end of the pipe to ensure a tight seal.
4) Apply a small amount of lubricant on the plug for easier insertion.
5) Insert the plug firmly into the open end of the pipe.
6) Ensure the plug is seated properly without any gaps.
7) Tighten any clamps or fittings around the plug if necessary for secure closure.
