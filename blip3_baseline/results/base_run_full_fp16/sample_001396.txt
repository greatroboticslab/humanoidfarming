7)  Prune plants regularly to promote air circulation and reduce disease risk.
8)  Harvest peppers at peak ripeness to maximize yield and flavor.
9)  Store harvested peppers properly to maintain quality until ready for market.
10)  Regularly assess farm performance against set goals and adjust practices accordingly.
11)  Seek feedback from experienced farmers and agricultural experts for continuous improvement.
12)  Document all farming practices and outcomes for future reference and learning.
