Task: Sustainable Agriculture - science challenge
Please break it into step-by-step subtasks. Provide numbered subtasks with concise justifications.
------------------------------------------------------------
1) Identify key sustainability challenges in current agricultural practices.
2) Research innovative technologies that can enhance soil health.
3) Analyze methods to reduce water usage in crop irrigation systems.
4) Develop strategies for increasing crop yields without chemical fertilizers.
5) Study genetic modifications to improve plant resistance to pests.
6) Implement precision farming techniques to optimize resource use.
7) Evaluate renewable energy sources for powering farm operations.
8) Assess organic waste recycling as a method for nutrient management.
9) Collaborate with local communities to promote sustainable farming practices.
10) Create educational programs to train farmers on sustainable agriculture methods.
