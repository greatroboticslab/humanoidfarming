Task: Winter Livestock Feeding
Please break it into step-by-step subtasks. Emphasize precision, sustainability, and error checks.
------------------------------------------------------------
1)  Assess current livestock health and nutritional needs.
2)  Plan feeding schedule based on seasonal changes.
3)  Source sustainable feed sources from local farms.
4)  Implement waste reduction strategies in feeding practices.
5)  Monitor livestock weight and adjust feeding as necessary.
6)  Conduct regular health checks to prevent diseases.
7)  Rotate pastures to maintain soil fertility and reduce erosion.
8)  Use natural pest control methods instead of chemical pesticides.
9)  Install water conservation systems to minimize water usage.
10)  Educate farmers about the importance of sustainable livestock management.
