Task: Cattle Feeding Scenarios 2021
Please break it into step-by-step subtasks. Focus on safety, measurement units, and necessary tools.
------------------------------------------------------------
1) Identify all cattle in the feeding area for accurate measurements.
2) Measure each cattle's weight using a digital scale in kilograms.
3) Record the current feed inventory in tons.
4) Determine the daily feed requirement per cattle based on age and size.
5) Calculate the total daily feed needed for all cattle in kilograms.
6) Convert the total daily feed requirement to tons for inventory management.
7) Prepare a feeding schedule considering weather conditions and health status.
8) Use a measuring scoop or bucket to distribute feed evenly among the cattle.
9) Monitor cattle's body condition weekly to adjust feeding as necessary.
10) Store leftover feed securely to prevent spoilage and waste.
