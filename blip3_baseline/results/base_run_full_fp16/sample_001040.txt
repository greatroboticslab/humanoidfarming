Task: Pest risk analysis: Why we do it and why it is important
Please break it into step-by-step subtasks. Include validation steps and fallback procedures.
------------------------------------------------------------
1)  Identify potential pests that could affect crops or stored products.
2)  Research historical pest occurrences in the area.
3)  Assess current pest management practices of the region.
4)  Determine the economic impact of each identified pest.
5)  Evaluate the environmental impact of the pests.
6)  Develop strategies to prevent pest infestations.
7)  Implement monitoring systems for early detection of pests.
8)  Train personnel on proper pest control techniques.
9)  Regularly review and update pest risk analysis reports.
10)  Share findings with stakeholders for collaborative decision-making.
