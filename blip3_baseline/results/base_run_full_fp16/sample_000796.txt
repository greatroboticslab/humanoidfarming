Task: HOW TO SMOOTH AND LEVEL A BUMPY FIELD OR LAWN
Please break it into step-by-step subtasks. Emphasize precision, sustainability, and error checks.
------------------------------------------------------------
1)  Assess the terrain for areas needing leveling or smoothing.
2)  Identify high spots and low spots on the field.
3)  Determine the appropriate tools and equipment needed.
4)  Clear any debris or obstacles from the area.
5)  Mark the high points with stakes or spray paint.
6)  Use a rake to gently level the ground around marked areas.
7)  Apply a layer of topsoil or compost to fill depressions.
8)  Smooth the soil using a lawn roller or tampers.
9)  Water the leveled area thoroughly to settle the soil.
10)  Check the levelness again before planting grass.
11)  Plant grass seeds or sod according to package instructions.
12)  Water regularly and maintain the lawn as needed.
