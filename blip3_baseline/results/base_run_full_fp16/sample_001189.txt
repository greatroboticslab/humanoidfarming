Task: HOW TO FIX FLAT TIRES FOR 10 YEARS
Please break it into step-by-step subtasks. Provide numbered subtasks with concise justifications.
------------------------------------------------------------
1) Check your vehicle's tire pressure according to the manufacturer's guidelines.
2) Inflate tires to the recommended pressure for optimal performance.
3) Inspect all tires for cuts, punctures, or bulges that could cause flats.
4) Rotate tires every 5,000 to 7,500 miles to ensure even wear.
5) Replace tires if they show signs of excessive wear or age beyond the recommended lifespan.
6) Keep spare tire in good condition and know how to change a flat tire safely.
7) Store spare tire properly to avoid damage from exposure to extreme temperatures.
8) Regularly check tire tread depth using a tread depth gauge.
9) Avoid driving on underinflated tires as it can lead to increased risk of flats.
10) If a flat tire occurs, pull over to a safe location, turn on hazard lights, and change the tire if equipped.
