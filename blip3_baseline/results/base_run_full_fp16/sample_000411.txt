Task: Shiro Plum  My First Fruit & How You Can Grow One Too #shorts
Please break it into step-by-step subtasks. Provide numbered subtasks with concise justifications.
------------------------------------------------------------
1) Identify Shiro Plum trees in local nurseries or online.
2) Choose a suitable planting location with full sun exposure.
3) Dig holes twice as wide but same depth as tree roots.
4) Plant trees at proper height, ensuring good root-to-soil contact.
5) Water thoroughly after planting to settle soil around roots.
6) Mulch around trees for moisture retention and weed control.
7) Apply organic fertilizer once a month during growing season.
8) Prune branches annually to promote healthy growth and fruiting.
9) Monitor for pests and diseases regularly, treating promptly.
10) Harvest plums when they turn dark purple and slightly soft.
