Task: GUIDE TO TRACTOR PALLET FORKS.  WHAT SIZE PALLET FORKS?
Please break it into step-by-step subtasks. Focus on safety, measurement units, and necessary tools.
------------------------------------------------------------
1) Determine the size of your tractor by measuring its width and height.
2) Select pallet forks that match or slightly exceed the tractor's width.
3) Measure the height difference between the tractor's floor and the pallet's top.
4) Choose forks that can safely lift the required height without exceeding the tractor's capacity.
5) Ensure forks are made for industrial use to maintain safety standards.
6) Verify that the fork length is appropriate for the load you intend to transport.
7) Use a tape measure to ensure all dimensions fit within the tractor's specifications.
8) Check the weight capacity of both the tractor and the forks to avoid overload.
9) Inspect the forks for any damage or wear before using them on the tractor.
10) If necessary, consult a professional to ensure proper installation and operation of the forks.
