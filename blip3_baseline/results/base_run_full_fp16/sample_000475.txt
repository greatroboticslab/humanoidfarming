Task: Transforming cassava peels into animal feed
Please break it into step-by-step subtasks. Focus on safety, measurement units, and necessary tools.
------------------------------------------------------------
1) Wash cassava peels thoroughly under running water to remove dirt and debris.
2) Cut the washed cassava peels into small pieces for easier processing.
3) Measure 1 kg of cassava peels for each batch of feed preparation.
4) Use a food processor or blender to grind the peeled cassava into a fine powder.
5) Ensure the ground cassava is free from lumps before proceeding to next step.
6) Mix the ground cassava with an equal amount of rice bran in a large container.
7) Add
0) 5% of a premixed vitamin and mineral supplement to the mixture.
8) Mix well until all ingredients are evenly distributed throughout the cassava powder.
9) Transfer the prepared cassava feed mixture into a clean plastic bag for storage.
10) Store the bagged cassava feed in a cool, dry place away from direct sunlight.
11) Before feeding, measure out 1 kg of the prepared cassava feed for each animal.
12) Distribute the measured feed evenly among the animals according to their weight and nutritional needs.
