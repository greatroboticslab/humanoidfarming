Task: Excavator job: Fence remove
Please break it into step-by-step subtasks. Focus on safety, measurement units, and necessary tools.
------------------------------------------------------------
1) Wear appropriate personal protective equipment including hard hat, safety glasses, gloves, and steel-toed boots.
2) Assess the fence type and materials to determine the removal method.
3) Measure the fence length and width accurately using a tape measure for precise cutting.
4) Identify any underground utilities or cables near the fence area.
5) Turn off power to any electrical fences or systems before proceeding.
6) Use a shovel or excavator bucket to carefully dig out the fence posts and anchor points.
7) Cut the fence panels and posts with a reciprocating saw or chainsaw, ensuring safe handling of sharp edges.
8) Remove debris and dispose of it properly in accordance with local regulations.
9) Clean up the work area thoroughly to ensure compliance with safety standards.
10) Document the removal process for future reference and safety audits.
