Task: The #1 Key For Year-Round Grazing
Please break it into step-by-step subtasks. Focus on safety, measurement units, and necessary tools.
------------------------------------------------------------
1) Assess local grazing regulations to ensure compliance.
2) Identify suitable pasture areas based on soil quality.
3) Measure pasture sizes in acres or hectares for planning.
4) Determine livestock numbers per acre using stocking rates.
5) Install electric fencing to delineate grazing zones safely.
6) Prepare necessary tools like measuring tapes and stakes.
7) Schedule regular rotations to prevent overgrazing.
8) Monitor weather forecasts to avoid extreme conditions.
9) Provide adequate water sources for livestock daily.
10) Conduct health checks on livestock monthly for disease prevention.
