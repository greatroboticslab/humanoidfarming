Task: "Maintain Your Combine: Essential Maintenance for John Deere S Series"
Please break it into step-by-step subtasks. Emphasize precision, sustainability, and error checks.
------------------------------------------------------------
1)  Inspect the combine's hydraulic system for leaks or damage.
2)  Check belts and pulleys for wear and replace if necessary.
3)  Lubricate moving parts according to the manufacturer's guidelines.
4)  Clean the cab interior and exterior for better visibility and comfort.
5)  Verify that all electrical connections are secure and not damaged.
6)  Ensure the fuel filter is clean and change it if needed.
7)  Inspect the grain tank for any signs of rust or corrosion.
8)  Check the bearings in the header for proper lubrication and tightness.
9)  Test the engine oil level and change it if it's dirty or old.
10)  Inspect the hydraulic fluid levels and quality, replacing as required.
11)  Check the air filter for blockages and replace if necessary.
12)  Tighten all fasteners on the machine to prevent loosening during operation.
13)  Perform a final check of all safety features and ensure they are operational.
14)  Document all maintenance tasks performed for future reference and compliance.
15)  Schedule regular maintenance intervals based on the manufacturer's recommendations.
