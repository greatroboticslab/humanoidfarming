Task: Autumn Awakening (and Garden Graveyard!): Preparing for Cool-Season Planting
Please break it into step-by-step subtasks. Include validation steps and fallback procedures.
------------------------------------------------------------
1)  Assess local climate conditions suitable for cool-season planting.
2)  Research specific plant species compatible with the chosen climate.
3)  Purchase seeds or seedlings from reputable nurseries.
4)  Prepare garden beds by removing weeds and adding compost.
5)  Ensure beds have adequate drainage and soil fertility.
6)  Plan planting schedule based on plant hardiness zones.
7)  Water newly planted areas thoroughly to settle soil.
8)  Apply mulch around plants to retain moisture and suppress weeds.
9)  Monitor plants for signs of pests or diseases regularly.
10)  Adjust watering frequency as needed based on weather conditions.
11)  Consider using row covers if necessary for protection against frost.
12)  Prepare for potential late frosts by covering tender plants at night.
