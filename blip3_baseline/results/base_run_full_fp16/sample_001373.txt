Task: How To Start A Farm In Five Minutes
Please break it into step-by-step subtasks. Emphasize precision, sustainability, and error checks.
------------------------------------------------------------
1)  Choose a suitable location for your farm that receives at least six hours of sunlight daily.
2)  Conduct a soil test to determine nutrient levels and pH balance for optimal plant growth.
3)  Purchase seeds or seedlings from a reputable supplier based on local climate conditions.
4)  Install necessary irrigation systems to ensure consistent water supply without waste.
5)  Select crops that are sustainable and require minimal pesticides or fertilizers.
6)  Implement crop rotation practices to maintain soil health and reduce pest populations.
7)  Monitor weather forecasts regularly to prepare for potential extreme weather events.
8)  Set up composting facilities to recycle organic waste and create natural fertilizer.
9)  Educate yourself about local regulations regarding farming activities and permits.
10)  Plan for future expansion by considering additional land or resources needed.
