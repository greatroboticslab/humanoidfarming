Task: Exploring Sustainable Farming Practices and Community Values
Please break it into step-by-step subtasks. Focus on safety, measurement units, and necessary tools.
------------------------------------------------------------
1) Identify sustainable farming practices through research.
2) Assess local climate conditions for optimal crop selection.
3) Determine soil health needs using pH testing kits.
4) Measure water usage in liters per day for efficiency.
5) Calculate energy consumption in kilowatt-hours monthly.
6) Select appropriate tools based on task requirements.
7) Ensure all equipment is safe to use before beginning work.
8) Plan community meetings to discuss values and goals.
9) Establish clear communication channels for feedback.
10) Develop metrics to measure progress towards sustainability.
