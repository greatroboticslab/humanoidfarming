Task: 10. IRRI Agronomy Challenge: Are Insects a Problem? (2 & 6 February 2012)
Please break it into step-by-step subtasks. Provide numbered subtasks with concise justifications.
------------------------------------------------------------
1) Review historical data on insect populations in agricultural settings.
2) Analyze current pest management practices for effectiveness.
3) Examine economic impacts of insect damage on crops.
4) Assess environmental effects of insecticides used.
5) Investigate resistance patterns among insect pests.
6) Evaluate alternative control methods' efficacy.
7) Consult with agronomists and entomologists for insights.
8) Gather feedback from farmers regarding insect issues.
9) Compile findings to determine if insects are indeed problematic.
10) Prepare a comprehensive report summarizing conclusions.
