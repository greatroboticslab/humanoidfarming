Task: Burning straw @Shorts
Please break it into step-by-step subtasks. Focus on safety, measurement units, and necessary tools.
------------------------------------------------------------
1) Choose a safe outdoor area away from flammable materials.
2) Gather dry straw in manageable quantities for burning.
3) Measure out the straw using a yardstick or measuring tape.
4) Prepare a fire pit or use an existing one to contain the burning.
5) Use tongs or a long-handled shovel to handle hot embers safely.
6) Light the straw using a match or lighter, ensuring proper ventilation.
7) Monitor the fire closely to prevent it from spreading beyond the designated area.
8) Extinguish the fire completely by pouring water over it until it is fully out.
9) Dispose of any ashes properly, allowing them to cool before disposing.
