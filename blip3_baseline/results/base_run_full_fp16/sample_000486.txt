Task: How to grow a TROPICAL JUNGLE in a small garden
Please break it into step-by-step subtasks. Emphasize precision, sustainability, and error checks.
------------------------------------------------------------
1)  Choose a suitable location with ample sunlight and partial shade for tropical jungle plants.
2)  Prepare the soil by adding organic matter like compost or well-rotted manure.
3)  Select native tropical plants that thrive in your climate zone.
4)  Plant the chosen plants at the appropriate depth and spacing, ensuring they have room to grow.
5)  Water the plants regularly, checking the soil moisture level weekly.
6)  Apply a balanced fertilizer every two months to support plant growth.
7)  Mulch around the plants to retain moisture and suppress weeds.
8)  Monitor for pests and diseases, using natural remedies if necessary.
9)  Prune plants as needed to maintain shape and encourage healthy growth.
10)  Encourage biodiversity by introducing various species of plants and animals.
11)  Regularly check for signs of environmental stress such as wilting or yellowing leaves.
12)  Adjust watering and fertilizing based on seasonal changes and weather conditions.
