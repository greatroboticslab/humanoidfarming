1)  Start by finding a calm, healthy cow suitable for milking.
2)  Ensure the cow is in a comfortable position on its side.
3)  Use a clean, sanitized milking pail or bucket.
4)  Gently pull the udder to separate the teats from the rest of the udder.
5)  Hold each teat with your thumb and forefinger.
6)  Squeeze the base of the teat to release air bubbles.
7)  Pull the teat out slowly while applying gentle pressure.
8)  Pour the milk directly into the sanitized container.
9)  Repeat the process for all four teats.
10)  Clean and sanitize the milking equipment after each use.
11)  Dispose of the used equipment properly.
12)  Offer the cow fresh water immediately after milking.
