Task: Growing Strawberries Treating Pests in your Strawberries
Please break it into step-by-step subtasks. Emphasize precision, sustainability, and error checks.
------------------------------------------------------------
1)  Identify common pests affecting strawberries such as aphids, slugs, and spider mites.
2)  Apply organic pest control methods like introducing beneficial insects or using neem oil.
3)  Regularly inspect plants for signs of pests and diseases weekly.
4)  Water plants deeply but infrequently to encourage strong root growth and discourage pests.
5)  Maintain clean garden areas free from debris where pests can hide.
6)  Rotate strawberry crops annually to prevent soil-borne diseases and pests.
7)  Use mulch around plants to suppress weeds and retain moisture.
8)  Monitor weather forecasts for potential outbreaks of fungal diseases.
9)  Prune plants regularly to improve air circulation and reduce humidity.
10)  Harvest ripe berries promptly to avoid attracting pests.
11)  Store harvested berries properly to extend shelf life and prevent spoilage.
12)  Consider companion planting with herbs like mint or basil to deter pests naturally.
