Task: STOP Buying FREE RANGE CHICKEN Until You Watch This
Please break it into step-by-step subtasks. Focus on safety, measurement units, and necessary tools.
------------------------------------------------------------
1) Research free range chicken farming practices thoroughly.
2) Identify farms that meet your criteria for ethical treatment.
3) Contact these farms directly to inquire about their methods.
4) Request detailed documentation of animal welfare standards.
5) Check if farms comply with local and international regulations.
6) Verify certifications such as USDA Organic or similar.
7) Consider visiting farms in person to assess conditions firsthand.
8) Calculate the cost per pound of certified free range chicken.
9) Compare this price with conventional and organic options.
10) Make an informed decision based on research and calculations.
11) Share findings with friends and family to raise awareness.
