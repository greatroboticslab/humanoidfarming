Task: Preparing Your Garden for the Growing Season
Please break it into step-by-step subtasks. Include validation steps and fallback procedures.
------------------------------------------------------------
1)  Assess the soil condition by testing pH levels and nutrient content.
2)  Amend the soil with compost or organic matter as needed.
3)  Remove any weeds or debris from the garden area.
4)  Clear out old plant material and prepare beds for planting.
5)  Water the garden thoroughly to settle the soil and remove any remaining debris.
6)  Apply a layer of mulch to retain moisture and suppress weeds.
7)  Check for pests and diseases in the garden area.
8)  Prepare tools and equipment for gardening tasks.
9)  Plan the layout of plants based on sunlight needs and space availability.
10)  Purchase seeds or seedlings according to your garden plan.
11)  Start seeds indoors if necessary, following instructions on seed packets.
12)  Harden off seedlings gradually before transplanting them outdoors.
