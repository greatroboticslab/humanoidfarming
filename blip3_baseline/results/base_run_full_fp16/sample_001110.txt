Task: Ford 8N Tractor  Changing Transmission Hydraulic Fluid
Please break it into step-by-step subtasks. Focus on safety, measurement units, and necessary tools.
------------------------------------------------------------
1)  Ensure the tractor is parked on a flat surface and turned off.
2)  Open the hood and locate the transmission fluid drain plug.
3)  Use an appropriate wrench to loosen and remove the drain plug.
4)  Allow the old fluid to drain into a suitable container.
5)  Replace the drain plug securely after draining.
6)  Locate the transmission fill port and connect a hydraulic fluid hose.
7)  Turn on the engine and allow it to reach operating temperature.
8)  Start adding new transmission fluid through the fill port.
9)  Monitor the level of fluid as you add until it reaches the recommended mark.
10)  Close the fill port and remove the hose.
11)  Check for leaks around the drain plug and fill port.
12)  Start the engine and check for proper operation and fluid level.
