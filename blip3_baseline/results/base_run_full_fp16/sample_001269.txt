Task: Grain Farming Process
Please break it into step-by-step subtasks. Provide numbered subtasks with concise justifications.
------------------------------------------------------------
1)  Clear the field of weeds and debris.
2)  Prepare the soil by plowing and harrowing.
3)  Apply fertilizers and organic matter to enrich the soil.
4)  Level the land using a plow or roller.
5)  Plant seeds in rows at recommended intervals.
6)  Cover seeds with soil for planting.
7)  Water the newly planted seeds gently.
8)  Monitor soil moisture levels regularly.
9)  Thin seedlings as they grow to ensure proper spacing.
10)  Weed the field manually or mechanically.
11)  Protect crops from pests and diseases with natural methods or pesticides.
12)  Harvest grains when fully matured.
13)  Remove chaff and stalks from harvested grains.
14)  Store grains in dry, cool conditions.
