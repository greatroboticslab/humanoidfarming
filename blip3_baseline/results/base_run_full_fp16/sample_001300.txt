Task: Gardening Tips for Beginner
Please break it into step-by-step subtasks. Focus on safety, measurement units, and necessary tools.
------------------------------------------------------------
1) Start by choosing beginner-friendly plants suitable for your climate.
2) Invest in basic gardening tools like a trowel, watering can, and gloves.
3) Ensure you have a good quality potting mix for planting.
4) Research the specific needs of your chosen plants regarding sunlight and water.
5) Learn how to properly measure soil depth for planting seeds or seedlings.
6) Always wear protective clothing when handling chemicals or working outdoors.
7) Before digging, mark where you plan to plant to avoid accidental damage to existing plants.
8) Use a level to ensure raised beds or containers are at the right height for easy access.
9) Water plants early in the morning or late afternoon to reduce evaporation and prevent leaf burn.
10) Apply mulch around plants to retain moisture and suppress weeds.
11) Regularly inspect plants for signs of pests or diseases and treat accordingly.
12) Keep a journal to track plant growth and care practices for future reference.
