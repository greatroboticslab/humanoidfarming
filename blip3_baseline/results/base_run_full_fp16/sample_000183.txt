Task: BREAKING GROUND AND STARTING A NEW GARDEN-PREPARING #garden
Please break it into step-by-step subtasks. Emphasize precision, sustainability, and error checks.
------------------------------------------------------------
1)  Choose a suitable location for your garden that receives adequate sunlight.
2)  Conduct a soil test to determine nutrient levels and pH balance.
3)  Amend the soil with organic matter like compost or well-rotted manure.
4)  Install a drip irrigation system to conserve water and reduce waste.
5)  Build raised beds if needed to improve drainage and prevent pests.
6)  Plant native species to support local wildlife and promote biodiversity.
7)  Mulch around plants to retain moisture and suppress weeds.
8)  Install birdhouses and nesting boxes to encourage beneficial birds.
9)  Avoid using chemical fertilizers and pesticides to maintain soil health.
10)  Regularly monitor plant health and remove any diseased or infested parts.
11)  Water plants deeply but less frequently to encourage deep root growth.
12)  Rotate crops annually to prevent soil depletion and pest buildup.
