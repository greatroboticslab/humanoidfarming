Task: Tips for Checking Planter Depth
Please break it into step-by-step subtasks. Provide numbered subtasks with concise justifications.
------------------------------------------------------------
1) Choose a suitable measuring tool like a ruler or tape measure.
2) Locate the planting hole where you intend to place the plant.
3) Insert the measuring tool vertically into the planting hole.
4) Read the measurement at the bottom of the tool to determine depth.
5) Ensure the depth matches the recommended planting depth for the specific plant species.
6) Adjust the planting depth if necessary, using additional soil or removing excess soil as needed.
7) Cover the planting hole securely with soil after placing the plant.
