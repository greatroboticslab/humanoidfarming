Task: How to Present the Value of Telematics to Customers
Please break it into step-by-step subtasks. Focus on safety, measurement units, and necessary tools.
------------------------------------------------------------
1) Identify key safety benefits telematics offers like reduced accidents and lower insurance premiums.
2) Explain how telematics measures driving behavior in real-time using GPS and sensors.
3) Show customers how telematics data can predict potential risks through analytics.
4) Demonstrate the importance of choosing reliable telematics providers for accurate data.
5) Highlight the use of various measurement units such as miles per gallon or kilometers per liter for fuel efficiency.
6) Emphasize the need for specific tools like smartphones or dashcams to collect telematics data effectively.
7) Illustrate the process of integrating telematics data into vehicle maintenance schedules for better performance.
