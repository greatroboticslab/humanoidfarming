Task: Sowing Garlic 28th September 2024
Please break it into step-by-step subtasks. Focus on safety, measurement units, and necessary tools.
------------------------------------------------------------
1)  Choose a suitable location with well-draining soil for planting garlic.
2)  Prepare the soil by removing weeds and adding compost or organic matter.
3)  Water the area thoroughly to settle the soil and prepare for planting.
4)  Purchase garlic cloves from a trusted source, ensuring they are disease-free.
5)  Select healthy, large-sized garlic cloves without sprouts.
6)  Plant garlic cloves about 4 inches apart in rows spaced 12-18 inches apart.
7)  Cover the cloves with 2-3 inches of soil, pressing gently to secure them.
8)  Water the planted garlic deeply to help establish roots.
9)  Mulch around the garlic plants to retain moisture and suppress weeds.
10)  Monitor for pests and diseases, applying appropriate control measures if needed.
11)  Harvest garlic when the leaves begin to yellow and die back naturally.
12)  Store harvested garlic in a cool, dry place until ready for use.
