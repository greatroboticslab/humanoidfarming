Task: WHAT IS A TRACTOR PTO? ONLY THE MOST USEFUL FEATURE ON ANY TRACTOR!
Please break it into step-by-step subtasks. Include validation steps and fallback procedures.
------------------------------------------------------------
1)  Identify the acronym PTO which stands for Power Take-Off.
2)  Recognize that PTO is a rotating shaft on a tractor's power take-off clutch.
3)  Understand that the most useful feature of PTO is its ability to transmit power to implements like mowers or balers.
4)  Confirm that PTO allows for easy attachment and detachment of equipment without disconnecting the engine.
5)  Validate this functionality by attempting to connect and disconnect an implement using the PTO on a tractor.
