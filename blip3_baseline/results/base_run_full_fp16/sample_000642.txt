Task: Learn how to grow rice
Please break it into step-by-step subtasks. Provide numbered subtasks with concise justifications.
------------------------------------------------------------
1)  Choose an appropriate climate and soil type suitable for rice cultivation.
2)  Prepare the land by plowing, harrowing, and leveling.
3)  Plant seeds or seedlings at the right time according to local agricultural practices.
4)  Ensure proper irrigation to maintain consistent water levels in the paddies.
5)  Apply fertilizers as recommended to support healthy plant growth.
6)  Monitor for pests and diseases regularly and take necessary control measures.
7)  Harvest the rice when the grains have reached their full maturity.
8)  Dry the harvested rice in the sun or using mechanical equipment.
9)  Store the dried rice in a cool, dry place to prevent spoilage.
10)  Maintain records of the farming process for future reference and improvement.
