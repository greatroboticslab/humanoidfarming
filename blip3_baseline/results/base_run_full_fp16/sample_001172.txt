Task: Grass Roots in the Soil part 1
Please break it into step-by-step subtasks. Focus on safety, measurement units, and necessary tools.
------------------------------------------------------------
1) Use gloves to protect hands from soil particles.
2) Choose a safe area away from traffic or hazards.
3) Measure soil depth using a ruler or tape measure.
4) Select appropriate tools like trowel or spade for digging.
5) Ensure all tools are clean and sharp for better results.
6) Prepare the area by removing any debris or rocks.
7) Start digging at least 10 centimeters deep in the chosen spot.
8) Record measurements and observations about the soil texture.
9) Continue digging until reaching the desired depth of analysis.
10) Handle soil samples carefully to avoid contamination.
