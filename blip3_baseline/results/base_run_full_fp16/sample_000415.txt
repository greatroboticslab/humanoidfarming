Task: Transform Your Grass: Drying Techniques | Farming Simulator 2022
Please break it into step-by-step subtasks. Focus on safety, measurement units, and necessary tools.
------------------------------------------------------------
1) Assess weather conditions for drying grass safely.
2) Gather necessary tools including pitchforks or rakes.
3) Measure grass patches to determine drying area size.
4) Use pitchforks to collect grass in measured areas.
5) Spread collected grass evenly across drying areas.
6) Ensure grass is not too wet to prevent mold growth.
7) Monitor temperature and humidity levels daily.
8) Rotate grass patches to ensure even drying.
9) Use fans if available to increase air circulation.
10) Harvest dried grass when fully dry and ready for storage.
