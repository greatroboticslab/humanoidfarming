Task: How to Make a Powerful JADAM Natural Pesticide by mixing JWA+JS+JHS for Gardeners. Homemade
Please break it into step-by-step subtasks. Focus on safety, measurement units, and necessary tools.
------------------------------------------------------------
1)  Gather all ingredients: JWA (Jinsheng Wuyao), JS (Jinsheng Shuang), and JHS (Jinsheng Huashan).
2)  Measure 1 tablespoon of JWA, 1 teaspoon of JS, and 1/2 teaspoon of JHS.
3)  Mix these ingredients in a small bowl or container.
4)  Add 1 cup of water to the mixture.
5)  Stir well until all ingredients are fully dissolved.
6)  Transfer the solution to a spray bottle.
7)  Shake the spray bottle before each use to ensure even distribution.
8)  Wear gloves and protective eyewear when handling the pesticide.
9)  Apply the solution to plants using a spray nozzle.
10)  Ensure thorough coverage of leaves and stems.
11)  Avoid spraying during windy conditions.
12)  Store the remaining solution in a cool, dry place away from direct sunlight.
13)  Dispose of any unused solution according to local regulations.
