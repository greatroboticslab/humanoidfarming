Task: Ghazas maize planting technique.#MaizePlantingTips #ExpertGardening #GrowBigCorn
Please break it into step-by-step subtasks. Focus on safety, measurement units, and necessary tools.
------------------------------------------------------------
1) Start by selecting a sunny spot in your garden for planting maize.
2) Measure out at least 10 feet between rows to allow for growth.
3) Use a hoe or tiller to loosen the soil to a depth of about 6 inches.
4) Mix in compost or well-rotted manure to improve soil fertility.
5) Plant maize seeds about 2 inches deep and 36 inches apart in each row.
6) Water the planted area thoroughly to settle the soil around the seeds.
7) Cover the planted area with a thin layer of straw mulch for weed control.
8) Monitor for pests and diseases regularly, using organic methods if possible.
9) Harvest maize when the ears are fully colored and the silk has dried.
10) Store harvested corn in a cool, dry place until ready to use.
