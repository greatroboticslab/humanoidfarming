Task: NO POTTING UP METHOD FOR TOMATO SEEDLINGS  - One Cup Method for Huge Tomatoes
Please break it into step-by-step subtasks. Provide numbered subtasks with concise justifications.
------------------------------------------------------------
1) Start by selecting healthy tomato seedlings from your nursery.
2) Fill one cup with a mixture of equal parts soil and compost.
3) Gently remove seedlings from their current pots without disturbing roots.
4) Place each seedling into the prepared cup, ensuring they stand upright.
5) Cover the base of each seedling with a thin layer of soil.
6) Water the cups thoroughly to settle the soil around the roots.
7) Place the cups in a warm, sunny spot indoors or outdoors.
8) Keep the soil moist but not waterlogged.
9) After about 2 weeks, transplant seedlings into larger pots or directly into the garden.
10) Monitor for pests and diseases, providing support as needed.
