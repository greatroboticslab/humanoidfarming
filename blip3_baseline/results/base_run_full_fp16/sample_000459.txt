Task: Irrigation check list plan
Please break it into step-by-step subtasks. Focus on safety, measurement units, and necessary tools.
------------------------------------------------------------
1) Inspect all irrigation system components for damage or wear.
2) Check water pressure to ensure it meets system requirements.
3) Measure soil moisture levels using a moisture meter.
4) Adjust sprinkler heads to cover all areas evenly.
5) Verify that all valves are properly sealed and functioning.
6) Prepare necessary tools such as a wrench, screwdriver, and measuring tape.
7) Ensure all electrical connections are secure and insulated.
8) Record current weather conditions and expected rainfall.
9) Plan watering schedule based on plant needs and local climate.
10) Set up a safety protocol including wearing protective gear.
