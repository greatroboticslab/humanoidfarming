Task: How to braid/cornrow FOR BEGINNERS!
Please break it into step-by-step subtasks. Provide numbered subtasks with concise justifications.
------------------------------------------------------------
1) Gather necessary tools including hair ties, comb, and styling products like gel or wax.
2) Wash and dry your hair thoroughly before starting the braiding process.
3) Divide your hair into three equal sections from top to bottom.
4) Take the middle section and begin twisting it around the other two sections.
5) Secure each twist with a hair tie as you go along.
6) Continue until you reach the ends of your hair, securing the last section tightly.
7) Apply a finishing product to smooth out any frizz and enhance the look.
