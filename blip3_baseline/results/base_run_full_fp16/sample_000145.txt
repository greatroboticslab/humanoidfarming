Task: Pt 1 - 3 tips to make your homesteading self-sufficient lifestyle successful while staying debt free
Please break it into step-by-step subtasks. Include validation steps and fallback procedures.
------------------------------------------------------------
1)  Create a detailed budget plan including all expenses and income sources.
2)  Prioritize essential needs over non-essential spending to avoid debt.
3)  Set up a savings account for unexpected expenses and emergencies.
4)  Start small with manageable projects that can generate income or reduce costs.
5)  Research local resources like community gardens or co-op markets for supplies.
6)  Develop skills in gardening, cooking, and other self-sufficiency areas through online courses or workshops.
7)  Regularly review and adjust your budget and financial goals as needed.
8)  Consider bartering or swapping goods with neighbors or local businesses for additional support.
9)  Establish a diversified income stream through multiple homesteading ventures.
10)  Keep detailed records of all financial transactions for accurate tracking and analysis.
