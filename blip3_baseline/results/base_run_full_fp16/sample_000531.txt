Task: Grow Your Own Grains Part 1: Best Seeding Methods -- Low Tech Video No. 24
Please break it into step-by-step subtasks. Include validation steps and fallback procedures.
------------------------------------------------------------
1)  Choose a suitable location for planting grains based on sunlight and soil conditions.
2)  Prepare the soil by removing weeds and adding compost or organic matter.
3)  Decide on the grain variety to plant according to local climate and storage needs.
4)  Purchase seeds from a reliable source ensuring they are fresh and disease-free.
5)  Soak seeds overnight in clean water to soften the outer shell and promote germination.
6)  Plant seeds at recommended depth and spacing as per the grain variety's instructions.
7)  Water the seeds gently but thoroughly after planting to ensure good seed-to-soil contact.
8)  Cover the planted area with a thin layer of mulch to retain moisture and suppress weeds.
9)  Monitor the plants regularly for signs of pests or diseases and take appropriate action if necessary.
10)  Harvest the grains when they have reached their full maturity and color, usually indicated by the head turning brown.
11)  Clean the harvested grains by threshing and winnowing to remove chaff and debris.
12)  Store the cleaned grains in a cool, dry place away from direct sunlight to prevent spoilage.
