Task: How to rid Weeds from your Garden | 6 Tips from a Farmer

------------------------------------------------------------
1)  Identify the type of weeds in your garden for targeted treatment.
2)  Remove visible weeds manually or use a hoe to cut them at the base.
3)  Apply herbicides according to the product instructions, avoiding sensitive plants.
4)  Mulch around plants to prevent weed seeds from germinating.
5)  Regularly check and remove new weeds as they appear.
6)  Consider using natural methods like vinegar or boiling water for selective weed control.
7)  Rotate crops annually to disrupt weed growth patterns.
