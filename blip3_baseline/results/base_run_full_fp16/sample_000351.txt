Task: Milking a cow at Lake Metroparks Farmpark.
Please break it into step-by-step subtasks. Focus on safety, measurement units, and necessary tools.
------------------------------------------------------------
1) Wear appropriate protective gear including gloves, sturdy boots, and a mask to prevent injuries and exposure to dust.
2) Approach the cow calmly, speaking softly to ensure the animal remains calm during the milking process.
3) Use a milking machine or hand milking techniques, ensuring you have clean, sanitized equipment.
4) Position the cow correctly, securing her in a comfortable stance for efficient milking.
5) Lubricate the teats gently with a mild soap solution to facilitate easier milking.
6) Connect the milking equipment to the cow's udder, ensuring a secure fit without causing discomfort.
7) Turn on the milking machine, starting with the first teat and moving systematically to the last.
8) Monitor the cow closely for any signs of distress or discomfort, stopping immediately if needed.
9) After milking, thoroughly clean and sanitize all equipment used to avoid contamination.
10) Dispose of the collected milk safely, following local regulations regarding waste disposal.
