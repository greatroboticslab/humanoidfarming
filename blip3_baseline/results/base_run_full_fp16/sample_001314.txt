Task: Spring Planter Tips and Planting Considerations
Please break it into step-by-step subtasks. Include validation steps and fallback procedures.
------------------------------------------------------------
1)  Assess sunlight exposure for optimal plant growth.
2)  Choose appropriate spring-planted plants suitable for local climate.
3)  Prepare soil by adding compost or organic matter to improve fertility.
4)  Ensure containers have adequate drainage holes to prevent waterlogging.
5)  Fill containers with potting soil, leaving space for root growth.
6)  Plant seeds or seedlings at recommended depths based on species.
7)  Water thoroughly after planting to settle soil around roots.
8)  Monitor soil moisture levels and adjust watering as needed.
9)  Apply a balanced fertilizer every few weeks during growing season.
10)  Provide support structures like stakes or cages for climbing plants.
11)  Regularly inspect plants for pests and diseases, treating promptly if found.
12)  Prune dead or overgrown branches to maintain plant health and shape.
13)  Move containers indoors before first frost to protect from cold temperatures.
14)  Harvest vegetables and flowers regularly to encourage continued growth.
15)  Rotate plants within containers or move them to different locations periodically.
