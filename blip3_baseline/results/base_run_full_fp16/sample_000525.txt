Task: Tractor Tire Set up
Please break it into step-by-step subtasks. Focus on safety, measurement units, and necessary tools.
------------------------------------------------------------
1)  Ensure all tractor tires are properly inflated to the manufacturer's recommended pressure.
2)  Check that all lug nuts are securely tightened according to the vehicle manual.
3)  Verify that the tire tread depth meets or exceeds the minimum legal requirement.
4)  Measure the distance between the front and rear axles to ensure proper wheelbase alignment.
5)  Align the tractor tires so they are parallel to each other and perpendicular to the ground.
6)  Use a torque wrench to tighten lug nuts in a star pattern for optimal stability.
7)  Secure the tractor to a stable surface using jack stands or a trailer hitch.
8)  Position the tractor on level ground to avoid uneven tire wear.
9)  Inspect the tractor tires for any signs of damage or wear before mounting them.
10)  Mount the tractor tires onto the wheels, ensuring they are seated correctly without excessive play.
11)  Tighten lug nuts to the specified torque values using a torque wrench.
12)  Double-check all connections and securements for safety before operating the tractor.
