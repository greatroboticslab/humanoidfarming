Task: Plant These 8 Perennial Vegetables to Get a Harvest Each Year #shorts
Please break it into step-by-step subtasks. Emphasize precision, sustainability, and error checks.
------------------------------------------------------------
1)  Choose suitable perennial vegetables for your climate zone.
2)  Select a location with full sun and well-draining soil.
3)  Prepare the soil by adding compost or organic matter.
4)  Plant seeds or seedlings according to recommended spacing.
5)  Water deeply once a week during dry periods.
6)  Mulch around plants to retain moisture and suppress weeds.
7)  Monitor for pests and diseases regularly.
8)  Harvest vegetables as needed without damaging plants.
9)  Rotate crops annually to prevent soil depletion.
10)  Consider companion planting for pest control and nutrient uptake.
11)  Compost plant waste to recycle nutrients back into the soil.
12)  Evaluate plant health and adjust care as necessary each year.
