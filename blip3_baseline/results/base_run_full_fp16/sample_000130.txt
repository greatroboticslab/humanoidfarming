Task: Milking Cows with Hand
Please break it into step-by-step subtasks. Provide numbered subtasks with concise justifications.
------------------------------------------------------------
1) Approach the cow from behind to avoid startling it.
2) Gently grasp the udder between thumb and forefinger.
3) Use your other hand to support the cow's hindquarters.
4) Squeeze the teat gently to release milk.
5) Rotate the hand to target different teats for milking.
6) Continue milking until all teats have been emptied.
7) Release the cow's udder and support its back.
8) Clean the equipment and sanitize hands before moving on.
