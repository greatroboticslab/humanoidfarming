Task: Growing Potatoes The Cheap and Easy Way!
Please break it into step-by-step subtasks. Focus on safety, measurement units, and necessary tools.
------------------------------------------------------------
1) Choose a suitable location with plenty of sunlight and well-draining soil.
2) Purchase certified seed potatoes from a trusted source.
3) Clean the seed potatoes thoroughly under running water.
4) Cut the seed potatoes into pieces, ensuring each has at least one eye.
5) Plant the cut seed potatoes about 6 inches deep in rows spaced 2 feet apart.
6) Water the planted areas deeply to settle the soil around the potatoes.
7) Mulch the planting beds to retain moisture and suppress weeds.
8) Keep the soil consistently moist but not waterlogged.
9) Monitor for signs of pests or diseases regularly.
10) Harvest when the tops begin to yellow and die back naturally.
