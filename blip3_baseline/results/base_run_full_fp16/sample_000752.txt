Task: Extreme Weather: Livestock Preparation Before the Storm
Please break it into step-by-step subtasks. Focus on safety, measurement units, and necessary tools.
------------------------------------------------------------
1) Assess local weather forecasts for extreme weather events.
2) Identify potential shelters or safe areas for livestock.
3) Prepare sturdy shelter structures if not already built.
4) Ensure all livestock have access to clean water.
5) Provide adequate food storage for at least three days.
6) Secure loose items around livestock pens to prevent damage.
7) Check and reinforce fences to prevent livestock escape.
8) Prepare emergency contact information for livestock owners.
9) Educate livestock handlers on evacuation procedures.
10) Gather necessary tools such as ropes, tarps, and first aid kits.
11) Monitor weather conditions closely during the storm.
12) Evacuate livestock to prepared shelters immediately upon warning.
