Task: Unlock the Future of Rice Farming: Modern Transplanting Techniques!
Please break it into step-by-step subtasks. Focus on safety, measurement units, and necessary tools.
------------------------------------------------------------
1) Assess local climate conditions for optimal transplant timing.
2) Measure soil moisture levels using a moisture meter before planting.
3) Select healthy rice seedlings from a controlled environment nursery.
4) Prepare the field by plowing and harrowing to loosen the soil.
5) Apply recommended fertilizers according to soil analysis results.
6) Use a tractor with a transplanter attachment for efficient planting.
7) Ensure proper spacing between seedlings at 15-20 cm apart in rows.
8) Water the field immediately after transplanting to settle the soil.
9) Monitor seedling growth weekly for signs of disease or pest infestation.
10) Adjust irrigation as needed based on weather forecasts and plant needs.
