Task: Rice cultivation//growing rice plants
Please break it into step-by-step subtasks. Emphasize precision, sustainability, and error checks.
------------------------------------------------------------
1)  Choose suitable land for planting rice, ensuring it has good drainage and soil fertility.
2)  Prepare the land by plowing and harrowing to create a smooth, level surface.
3)  Apply organic fertilizers or compost to enrich the soil, promoting healthy plant growth.
4)  Plant rice seeds in rows using a seedling tray or directly in the field, spacing them appropriately.
5)  Monitor water levels regularly, maintaining optimal moisture conditions for germination and early growth.
6)  Transplant seedlings when they reach a certain size, ensuring they are sturdy and disease-free.
7)  Provide adequate shade during the hottest part of the day to protect young plants from sunburn.
8)  Irrigate the fields as needed, using methods that minimize water waste and promote sustainable farming practices.
9)  Apply natural pest control measures, such as introducing beneficial insects or using neem oil, to keep pests under control.
10)  Harvest rice when the grains are fully matured but still green, ensuring minimal damage to the plants.
11)  Dry the harvested rice in the sun or using a mechanical dryer, avoiding excessive heat to preserve its quality.
12)  Store the dried rice in a cool, dry place to prevent spoilage and maintain freshness.
