Task: What is Spate Irrigation?
Please break it into step-by-step subtasks. Provide numbered subtasks with concise justifications.
------------------------------------------------------------
1) Spate irrigation involves the use of rainwater and floodwater to irrigate crops over a short period.
2) This method is particularly effective in regions with erratic rainfall patterns and high floodwaters.
3) It typically relies on natural water flows from rivers or streams during monsoon seasons.
4) The technique allows for efficient water usage by utilizing the full extent of available water resources.
5) Spate irrigation can significantly increase agricultural productivity in areas prone to drought and flooding.
