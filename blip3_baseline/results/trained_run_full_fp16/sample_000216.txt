Task: Pond Building
Please break it into step-by-step subtasks. Emphasize precision, sustainability, and error checks.
------------------------------------------------------------
Create a plan for pond construction.
Choose a suitable location for the pond.
Measure and mark the area where the pond will be built.
Excavate the soil to form the pond's shape.
Lay down a layer of gravel or sand at the bottom of the pond.
Install a waterproof liner over the gravel or sand.
Fill the pond with water from a nearby source.
Add aquatic plants and fish to the pond ecosystem.
Maintain the pond by cleaning debris and adjusting pH levels periodically.
Monitor the health of the pond's inhabitants regularly.
Educate others about proper pond care and maintenance practices.
Celebrate the completion of the pond project.
End.
