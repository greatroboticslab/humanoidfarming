Please break it into step-by-step subtasks. Provide numbered subtasks with concise justifications.
------------------------------------------------------------
1) Choose the right spot for your plants based on sunlight needs.
2) Prepare the soil by adding compost or organic matter.
3) Water the area thoroughly before planting to settle the soil.
4) Dig holes slightly larger than the plant's roots.
5) Place the plant in the hole and fill it with soil, pressing down gently.
6) Water the newly planted plant deeply to settle the soil around the roots.
7) Apply a layer of mulch around the base of the plant to retain moisture.
8) Monitor the plant regularly for signs of stress or disease.
9) Apply appropriate fertilizer according to the plant's needs.
10) Keep an eye on pests and treat them as needed.
11) Prune if necessary to encourage healthy growth and shape.
12) Enjoy watching your plants grow and thrive in their new home.
