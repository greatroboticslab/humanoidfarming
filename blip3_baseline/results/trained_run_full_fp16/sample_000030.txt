Task: Lesson 21  Concept of Sustainable Agriculture
Please break it into step-by-step subtasks. Focus on safety, measurement units, and necessary tools.
------------------------------------------------------------
1) Define sustainable agriculture as practices that meet current needs without compromising future generations' ability to meet their own needs.
2) Identify key principles of sustainable agriculture including biodiversity, soil health, water conservation, and fair labor practices.
3) Explain how sustainable agriculture reduces environmental impact while increasing food security.
4) Discuss examples of sustainable farming techniques such as crop rotation, organic fertilizers, and integrated pest management.
5) Highlight the economic benefits of sustainable agriculture through reduced input costs and increased market value.
6) Explore challenges in implementing sustainable agriculture methods globally and locally.
7) Outline strategies for promoting sustainable agriculture among farmers and consumers.
8) Emphasize the importance of policy support and education in advancing sustainable agricultural practices.
9) Conclude by summarizing the significance of sustainable agriculture in addressing global food production challenges.
