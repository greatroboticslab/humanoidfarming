Task: Farm Machine Safety: Loader and Skid Steer Safety
Please break it into step-by-step subtasks. Include validation steps and fallback procedures.
------------------------------------------------------------
1) Always wear appropriate personal protective equipment including a helmet, gloves, and goggles when operating farm machinery.
2) Inspect the loader or skid steer for any visible damage or malfunction before starting work.
3) Ensure all controls are in safe positions and the machine is properly grounded before turning on.
4) Operate at a steady speed and maintain control of the machine at all times.
5) Keep a safe distance from other vehicles and pedestrians while working.
6) Use caution when loading or unloading materials to prevent tipping or overturning.
7) Never exceed the maximum load capacity of the machine to avoid overloading.
8) Stop the machine immediately if you notice any unusual sounds or smells.
9) Turn off the engine and engage the parking brake before leaving the machine unattended.
10) Regularly check tire pressure and perform routine maintenance checks as per manufacturer guidelines.
11) Store the machine in a secure location away from potential hazards after use.
12) Familiarize yourself with emergency procedures and have them readily available for quick reference.
