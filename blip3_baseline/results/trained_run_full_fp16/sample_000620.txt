Task: How One Country Sought to Combat the Harm of Burning Sugar Cane
Please break it into step-by-step subtasks. Focus on safety, measurement units, and necessary tools.
------------------------------------------------------------
1) The country identified burning sugar cane as a significant source of air pollution and environmental degradation.
2) They initiated research to understand the extent of harm caused by this practice.
3) Government agencies developed policies aimed at reducing sugar cane burning.
4) Educational campaigns were launched to inform farmers about alternative methods.
5) Financial incentives were offered to encourage the adoption of sustainable farming practices.
6) Infrastructure improvements were made to facilitate the transportation of harvested sugar cane.
7) Collaboration between government, private sector, and local communities was strengthened for implementation.
8) Monitoring systems were established to track progress and effectiveness of implemented measures.
9) Regular assessments were conducted to evaluate the impact on air quality and public health.
10) International partnerships were formed to share best practices and technologies for combating the issue.
