Task: Preparing Your Garden for the Growing Season
Please break it into step-by-step subtasks. Include validation steps and fallback procedures.
------------------------------------------------------------
Prepare garden tools and equipment.
Assess soil health and prepare beds accordingly.
Remove weeds and debris from garden area.
Test soil pH levels and adjust if necessary.
Apply compost or organic matter to enrich soil.
Water garden thoroughly to settle new amendments.
Plant seeds or seedlings according to planting guides.
Mulch around plants to retain moisture and suppress weeds.
Monitor for pests and diseases regularly.
Prune overgrown plants and remove deadwood.
Fertilize plants as needed based on their specific needs.
Install irrigation system if not already in place.
Cover plants during cold nights using row covers.
Rotate crops annually to prevent depletion of nutrients.
Introduce beneficial insects to control pests naturally.
Harvest vegetables and flowers as they mature.
Clean up fallen leaves and plant debris after harvest.
Inspect garden for signs of winter damage and repair.
Store garden tools properly for winter storage.
Plan next year's garden layout based on this season's experiences.
