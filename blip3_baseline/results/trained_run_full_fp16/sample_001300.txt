Task: Gardening Tips for Beginner
Please break it into step-by-step subtasks. Focus on safety, measurement units, and necessary tools.
------------------------------------------------------------
1) Start by choosing a suitable location that receives at least six hours of sunlight daily.
2) Select plants appropriate for your climate zone to ensure they thrive.
3) Prepare the soil by adding compost or organic matter to improve fertility and drainage.
4) Water plants deeply but infrequently to encourage strong root growth.
5) Mulch around plants to retain moisture and suppress weeds.
6) Apply balanced fertilizer according to package instructions during growing seasons.
7) Regularly check for pests and diseases, treating them promptly to prevent damage.
8) Harvest vegetables and fruits when they reach maturity for best flavor and nutrition.
9) Rotate crops annually to avoid depleting soil nutrients and reduce pest buildup.
10) Practice companion planting to enhance plant health and deter pests naturally.
11) Learn about pruning techniques specific to your garden's plants to promote healthy growth.
12) Keep records of what works well in your garden to refine your gardening skills over time.
