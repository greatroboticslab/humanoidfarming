Task: CAM-Smart irrigation system - CAM-Science
Please break it into step-by-step subtasks. Focus on safety, measurement units, and necessary tools.
------------------------------------------------------------
1) Identify the specific needs of plants in the smart irrigation system.
2) Connect sensors to monitor soil moisture levels and weather conditions.
3) Program the system to adjust watering based on sensor data and environmental factors.
4) Install a water tank or reservoir for storing water efficiently.
5) Choose an appropriate pump to deliver water to the plants' roots.
6) Select a control panel or app for remote monitoring and management of the system.
7) Set up a timer to automate watering schedule according to plant requirements.
8) Test the system under various conditions to ensure accuracy and efficiency.
9) Regularly maintain the system by cleaning sensors and checking for leaks.
10) Update software and firmware as needed to improve performance and add new features.
