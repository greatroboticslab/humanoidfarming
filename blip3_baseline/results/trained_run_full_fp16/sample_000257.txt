Task: Small Farm Irrigation - Video 4 - How to Patch a Lay Flat Header Pipe
Please break it into step-by-step subtasks. Focus on safety, measurement units, and necessary tools.
------------------------------------------------------------
1) Locate the damaged section of the lay flat header pipe.
2) Cut out the damaged portion using appropriate cutting tools.
3) Clean the cut edges thoroughly to remove any debris.
4) Apply a layer of primer to both cut edges for better adhesion.
5) Apply a suitable sealant or adhesive to one of the cut edges.
6) Align the cut edge of the damaged section with the intact part of the pipe.
7) Press the cut edge firmly against the intact part of the pipe.
8) Use clamps or straps to secure the connection in place.
9) Ensure all connections are tight and secure.
10) Test the patched area for leaks before use.
11) If necessary, apply additional layers of sealant or adhesive for extra strength.
12) Mark the patched area clearly for future reference.
End.
