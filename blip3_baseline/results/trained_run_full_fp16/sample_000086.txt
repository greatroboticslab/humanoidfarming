1) Inspect all tractor components for wear or damage before each use.
2) Always wear protective gear including helmets and goggles when operating the tractor.
3) Keep the tractor in good working condition by regularly servicing it according to manufacturer guidelines.
4) Ensure all safety features such as seat belts and roll-over protection systems are functioning properly.
5) Never operate the tractor while fatigued or under the influence of alcohol or drugs.
6) Keep the area around the tractor clear of obstacles and debris to prevent accidents.
7) Follow all local regulations and farm safety guidelines strictly at all times.
8) Train all operators on proper tractor operation and emergency procedures.
9) Store the tractor securely when not in use to avoid accidental start-ups.
10) Regularly review and update safety protocols based on new information and changes in equipment.
