Task: Crop Rotation in Farming
Please break it into step-by-step subtasks. Focus on safety, measurement units, and necessary tools.
------------------------------------------------------------
1) Identify the type of crop to be rotated.
2) Determine the planting season for each crop.
3) Plan the rotation schedule based on soil health and pest management needs.
4) Prepare the land according to the new crop's requirements.
5) Plant the first crop as per the rotation plan.
6) Monitor the growth and health of the first crop regularly.
7) Prepare the land for the second crop after the first one is harvested.
8) Plant the second crop when the conditions are right.
9) Repeat the process for subsequent crops in the rotation cycle.
10) Adjust the rotation plan if necessary based on crop performance and environmental changes.
