Task: Operator Daily Maintenance of Crawler Tractors
Please break it into step-by-step subtasks. Provide numbered subtasks with concise justifications.
------------------------------------------------------------
1) Inspect all hydraulic lines for leaks or damage.
2) Check tire pressure and condition on all crawler tractors.
3) Lubricate moving parts according to manufacturer's guidelines.
4) Clean filters to ensure proper airflow and function.
5) Tighten loose bolts and screws on various components.
6) Verify that all safety devices are functioning correctly.
7) Test the operation of the steering system for smooth movement.
8) Inspect the engine oil level and quality for cleanliness.
9) Ensure that all lights and indicators are working properly.
10) Check the coolant level and check for any signs of corrosion.
11) Inspect the brakes for wear and adjust as necessary.
12) Perform a thorough visual inspection of the entire machine exterior.
13) Prepare a maintenance log documenting all performed tasks.
14) Schedule the next scheduled maintenance based on usage and manufacturer recommendations.
15) Report any issues found during the daily inspection to the appropriate personnel.
