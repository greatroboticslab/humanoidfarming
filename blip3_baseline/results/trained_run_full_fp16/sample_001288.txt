Task: 6 Gardening Tips and Trick for Beginners
Please break it into step-by-step subtasks. Focus on safety, measurement units, and necessary tools.
------------------------------------------------------------
1) Start by choosing the right plants suitable for your climate and soil type.
2) Prepare the soil by adding compost or organic matter to improve fertility.
3) Water plants deeply but less frequently to encourage deep root growth.
4) Use mulch around plants to retain moisture and suppress weeds.
5) Regularly prune dead or diseased parts of plants to promote healthy growth.
6) Rotate crops annually to prevent depletion of soil nutrients and reduce pest problems.
