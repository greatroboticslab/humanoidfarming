Task: Grow Your Own Grains Part 1: Best Seeding Methods -- Low Tech Video No. 24

1) Choose seeds suitable for local climate and soil conditions.
2) Prepare soil by removing weeds and adding compost.
3) Plant seeds at recommended depth and spacing for optimal growth.
4) Water regularly to keep soil moist but not waterlogged.
5) Monitor plants for pests and diseases, using natural remedies if possible.
6) Harvest grains when they turn from green to golden brown.
7) Store harvested grains in a cool, dry place to prevent spoilage.
8) Rotate crops annually to maintain soil fertility and health.
9) Consider companion planting techniques to enhance plant health naturally.
10) Use organic fertilizers to support plant growth without chemical inputs.
11) Practice crop rotation to reduce pest and disease buildup over time.
12) Enjoy the satisfaction of growing your own grains from seed to harvest.
