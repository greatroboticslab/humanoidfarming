3) By doing so, they can identify areas needing specific care like water or fertilizer.
4) Precision farming techniques then apply these resources more efficiently.
5) As a result, crop yields increase while reducing waste and environmental impact.

7) This automation leads to faster operations and less human error in planting.

10) Overall, GPS technology enhances agricultural productivity and sustainability.
