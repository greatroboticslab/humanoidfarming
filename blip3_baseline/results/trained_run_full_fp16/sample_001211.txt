Task: Ghazas maize planting technique.#MaizePlantingTips #ExpertGardening #GrowBigCorn
Please break it into step-by-step subtasks. Focus on safety, measurement units, and necessary tools.
------------------------------------------------------------
1) Identify suitable soil type for maize planting.
2) Choose an appropriate time of year for planting.
3) Prepare the land by removing weeds and adding compost.
4) Plant maize seeds at recommended depth and spacing.
5) Water the planted areas thoroughly to settle the soil.
6) Apply fertilizer according to crop needs and instructions.
7) Monitor plants for signs of pests or diseases regularly.
8) Support taller plants with stakes if necessary.
9) Harvest maize when ears turn golden brown.
10) Store harvested corn in a cool dry place.
11) Rotate crops annually to prevent soil depletion.
12) Practice natural pest control methods whenever possible.
