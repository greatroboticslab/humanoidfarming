Task: Regenerative Agriculture: A Solution to Climate Change
Please break it into step-by-step subtasks. Focus on safety, measurement units, and necessary tools.

2) Implement practices such as crop rotation and cover cropping in agricultural fields.
3) Encourage farmers to use natural fertilizers like compost and manure instead of synthetic ones.
4) Promote the use of permaculture designs to enhance biodiversity and soil health.
5) Support policies that incentivize regenerative farming methods and reduce carbon emissions.
6) Educate communities about the benefits of regenerative agriculture for both environment and economy.
7) Monitor and evaluate the effectiveness of regenerative agriculture practices over time.
8) Collaborate with local governments, NGOs, and international organizations to scale up regenerative agriculture initiatives.
9) Advocate for global agreements that prioritize sustainable land management and climate resilience.
10) Foster innovation in technology to support regenerative agriculture practices more efficiently.
