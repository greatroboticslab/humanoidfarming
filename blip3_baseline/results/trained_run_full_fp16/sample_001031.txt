Task: Farm Fresh Lunch | Cooking for Orange Harvest Workers
Please break it into step-by-step subtasks. Include validation steps and fallback procedures.
------------------------------------------------------------
1) Identify ingredients needed for lunch including fresh vegetables from the orange harvest.
2) Prepare a simple recipe that highlights local produce like carrots, tomatoes, and cucumbers.
3) Ensure all utensils are clean and ready before starting cooking.
4) Start boiling water for rice or other staple dishes to accompany the salad.
5) Chop vegetables into bite-sized pieces for easy eating during labor.
6) Mix together a dressing of olive oil, lemon juice, salt, and pepper for the salad.
7) Arrange chopped vegetables on plates alongside the prepared rice or main dish.
8) Serve immediately while workers are busy harvesting oranges to avoid waste.
9) Encourage workers to take breaks to enjoy their meal and stay hydrated.
10) Clean up kitchen area promptly after serving to prepare for next day's tasks.
