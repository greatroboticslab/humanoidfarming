Task: IMPROVE your raised bed soil the EASY Way! (With cover crops)
Please break it into step-by-step subtasks. Provide numbered subtasks with concise justifications.
------------------------------------------------------------
1) Choose cover crops suitable for your climate and soil type.
2) Plant cover crops in early spring or fall to improve soil health.
3) Mulch cover crops with straw or leaves after planting.
4) Allow cover crops to grow until they start to die back naturally.
5) Till the dead cover crop into the soil during its last weeks of life.
6) Water thoroughly after tilling to help incorporate organic matter.
7) Add compost or well-rotted manure if needed for additional nutrients.
8) Mix in any desired amendments like lime or fertilizer as per soil test results.
9) Level the soil surface and prepare for planting seeds or seedlings.
10) Cover the prepared soil with mulch to retain moisture and suppress weeds.
11) Monitor soil moisture levels and adjust watering as necessary throughout the growing season.
12) Harvest cover crops annually to maintain healthy soil structure and fertility.
