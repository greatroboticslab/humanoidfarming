Task: Potatoes - Grading and Packing
Please break it into step-by-step subtasks. Provide numbered subtasks with concise justifications.
------------------------------------------------------------
1) Inspect potatoes for size, shape, and overall quality.
2) Sort potatoes into different grades based on size and condition.
3) Clean potatoes thoroughly under running water to remove dirt.
4) Dry potatoes gently to prevent moisture damage during packing.
5) Pack graded potatoes carefully in appropriate containers or bags.
6) Label each package clearly with grade, quantity, and date of packaging.
7) Store packed potatoes in cool, dry place until ready for distribution.
