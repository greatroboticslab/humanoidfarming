------------------------------------------------------------
1) Identify the type of weeds in your garden to choose the right treatment method.
2) Remove visible weeds manually before they spread seeds.
3) Apply herbicides according to label instructions for effective control.
4) Mulch around plants to prevent weed growth and retain moisture.
5) Introduce beneficial insects that naturally eat weeds and pests.
6) Rotate crops annually to disrupt weed life cycles and soil health.
