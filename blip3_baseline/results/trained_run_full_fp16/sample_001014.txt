Task: Harvesting Sunflowers seed
Please break it into step-by-step subtasks. Include validation steps and fallback procedures.
------------------------------------------------------------
1) Identify mature sunflowers in your garden or field.
2) Use pruning shears to cut down the plants at ground level.
3) Spread out the cut stems on a flat surface to allow them to dry.
4) Once dry, separate the seeds from the remaining plant material.
5) Clean the seeds by removing any remaining chaff or debris.
6) Store the cleaned seeds in an airtight container away from moisture.
7) Plant the seeds in spring when temperatures warm up.
8) Water regularly and provide adequate sunlight for growth.
9) Monitor for pests and diseases, taking action if necessary.
10) Harvest again next year using the same process.
