Task: A day in farmer's life
Please break it into step-by-step subtasks. Focus on safety, measurement units, and necessary tools.
------------------------------------------------------------
Wake up early.
Feed livestock and check for any injuries.
Milk cows if applicable.
Sow seeds in prepared fields.
Water crops using hand pumps or hoses.
Harvest vegetables from garden plots.
Pick fruits from trees.
Breed animals as needed.
Sell produce at local markets.
Repair farm equipment when necessary.
Rest during midday heat.
Prepare meals using fresh ingredients.
Clean barns and stables thoroughly.
Inspect crops for pests or diseases.
Plan next day's tasks.
Sleep early to prepare for another day.
