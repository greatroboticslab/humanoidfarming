Task: How to grow a TROPICAL JUNGLE in a small garden
Please break it into step-by-step subtasks. Emphasize precision, sustainability, and error checks.
------------------------------------------------------------
1) Choose a sunny spot with well-draining soil for your tropical jungle garden.
2) Prepare the soil by adding compost or organic matter to improve fertility and drainage.
3) Select appropriate tropical plants suitable for your climate zone.
4) Plant larger trees first, then add shrubs and smaller plants gradually.
5) Water deeply but infrequently to encourage deep root growth.
6) Mulch around plants to retain moisture and suppress weeds.
7) Provide support structures like stakes or trellises for climbing plants.
8) Regularly prune plants to maintain shape and promote healthy growth.
9) Apply balanced fertilizer during the growing season to support plant health.
10) Monitor for pests and diseases, treating them promptly if necessary.
11) Encourage biodiversity by including various types of plants and animals.
12) Enjoy watching your tropical jungle garden flourish over time.
