Task: 10 Essential GARDENING Tips for Beginners
Please break it into step-by-step subtasks. Provide numbered subtasks with concise justifications.
------------------------------------------------------------
1) Start by choosing a suitable location that gets plenty of sunlight.
2) Select easy-to-grow plants like tomatoes or lettuce for beginners.
3) Prepare the soil by adding compost to enrich its nutrients.
4) Water plants regularly but avoid over-saturating the roots.
5) Mulch around plants to retain moisture and suppress weeds.
6) Use organic fertilizers to promote healthy growth without chemicals.
7) Prune plants periodically to encourage bushier growth and prevent disease.
8) Harvest vegetables and flowers at their peak maturity for best flavor.
9) Rotate crops annually to prevent soil depletion and pest buildup.
10) Join local gardening groups for tips and support from experienced gardeners.
