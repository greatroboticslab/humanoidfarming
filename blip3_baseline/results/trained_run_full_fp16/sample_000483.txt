Task: How to Milk a Cow By Hands | Evening Milking Of Cow By Hand
Please break it into step-by-step subtasks. Focus on safety, measurement units, and necessary tools.
------------------------------------------------------------
1) Obtain permission from the farmer or owner before attempting to milk a cow.
2) Ensure the cow is comfortable and in a quiet area for evening milking.
3) Gently approach the cow from behind and place one hand under its hindquarters.
4) Use your other hand to support the cow's head and neck.
5) Position your hands around the udder, applying gentle pressure.
6) Slowly squeeze each quarter of the udder to release the milk.
7) Collect the milk in a clean container or bucket.
8) Repeat the process for each quarter of the udder until all milk is extracted.
9) Clean your hands thoroughly after milking to prevent contamination.
10) Dispose of the used container safely and wash any equipment that came into contact with the milk.
10) If possible, thank the cow for its milk and provide some treats as a reward.
