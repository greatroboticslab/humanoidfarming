Task: Harvest & count a Turing's sunflower
Please break it into step-by-step subtasks. Focus on safety, measurement units, and necessary tools.
------------------------------------------------------------
1) Identify a mature sunflower plant to ensure it has reached the stage suitable for harvesting.
2) Locate the center of the flower head where the seeds are densely packed.
3) Use pruning shears to cut the stem just above the flower head.
4) Gently remove the flower head from the stem.
5) Lay the flower head flat on a clean surface.
6) Count each individual seed in the flower head.
7) Record the total number of seeds counted.
8) Repeat the process if multiple flower heads are available for analysis.
9) Ensure all harvested flower heads are properly disposed of after counting to avoid contamination.
