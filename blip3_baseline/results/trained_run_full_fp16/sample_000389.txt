Task: Simple Guide on to How to Grow Capsicums (Hoho) | Capsicums Farming in Kenya
Please break it into step-by-step subtasks. Provide numbered subtasks with concise justifications.
------------------------------------------------------------
1) Choose a sunny spot for your capsicum plants as they need at least six hours of direct sunlight daily.
2) Prepare the soil by adding compost or well-rotted manure to enrich it with nutrients.
3) Plant seeds indoors about four weeks before the last expected frost date.
4) Transplant seedlings outdoors when nighttime temperatures stay above 50°F (10°C).
5) Space plants about 18 inches apart to allow for growth and air circulation.
6) Water regularly, keeping the soil moist but not waterlogged.
7) Apply a balanced fertilizer every few weeks during the growing season.
8) Monitor for pests and diseases, treating them promptly if necessary.
9) Harvest capsicums when they turn fully colored and feel firm to the touch.
10) Continue harvesting throughout the growing season until temperatures drop below freezing.
11) Consider using mulch to retain moisture and suppress weeds around your plants.
12) Rotate crops annually to prevent depletion of soil nutrients and reduce pest buildup.
