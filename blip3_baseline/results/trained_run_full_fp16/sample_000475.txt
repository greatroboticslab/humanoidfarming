Task: Transforming cassava peels into animal feed
Please break it into step-by-step subtasks. Focus on safety, measurement units, and necessary tools.
------------------------------------------------------------
1) Collect cassava peels from local farms or processing plants.
2) Wash the cassava peels thoroughly to remove any dirt or residue.
3) Dry the washed cassava peels in an oven or under direct sunlight until they are completely dry.
4) Grind the dried cassava peels into a fine powder using a food processor or grinder.
5) Screen the ground cassava peels to ensure all particles are small enough for animal consumption.
6) Test the nutritional content of the ground cassava peels to ensure they meet animal feed standards.
7) Mix the ground cassava peels with other ingredients as per recommended ratios for specific animals.
8) Store the prepared cassava peels in a clean, dry container away from moisture and pests.
9) Distribute the cassava peels to livestock farmers or feed mills for use in animal feed formulations.
10) Monitor the health and performance of animals fed on cassava peels to assess their suitability as an animal feed ingredient.
