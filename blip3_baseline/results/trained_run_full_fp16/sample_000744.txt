------------------------------------------------------------
A tractor PTO is an acronym for Power Take-Off.
It allows the transmission of power from the tractor's engine to other machinery.
This feature enables tasks like mowing grass or operating drills.
The most useful feature on any tractor related to PTO is its ability to drive attachments directly.
Thus, the most useful feature on any tractor is the Power Take-Off system.
