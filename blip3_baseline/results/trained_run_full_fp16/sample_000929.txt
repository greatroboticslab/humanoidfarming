Task: Hand milking
Please break it into step-by-step subtasks. Focus on safety, measurement units, and necessary tools.
------------------------------------------------------------
1) Obtain a clean, soft towel to wrap around the udder.
2) Use warm water to gently rinse the udder and teats.
3) Place the teats in a clean, dry container for milking.
4) Sit comfortably with the cow facing you.
5) Hold the teats between your thumb and forefinger.
6) Apply gentle pressure while pulling the teat out of the cow's body.
7) Continue milking until the milk stops flowing freely.
8) Clean the teats and container thoroughly after each use.
9) Dispose of the used towels and containers safely.
10) Wash your hands thoroughly with soap and water afterwards.
