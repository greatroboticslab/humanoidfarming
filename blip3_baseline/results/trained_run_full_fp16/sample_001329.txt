Task: Harvesting and Storing Water for Crop Irrigation
Please break it into step-by-step subtasks. Focus on safety, measurement units, and necessary tools.
------------------------------------------------------------
1) Assess local water sources for potential harvesting.
2) Determine crop water needs based on climate and soil type.
3) Choose appropriate storage containers or structures.
4) Install necessary equipment like pumps and filters if needed.
5) Plan irrigation schedule to avoid overwatering or drought.
6) Construct or modify existing infrastructure for efficient water flow.
7) Educate farmers about proper water usage techniques.
8) Monitor water quality regularly to ensure safe irrigation.
9) Implement leak detection systems to prevent waste.
10) Regularly maintain all harvested and stored water systems.
11) Prepare contingency plans for unexpected events affecting water supply.
12) Collaborate with local authorities for regulatory compliance and support.
