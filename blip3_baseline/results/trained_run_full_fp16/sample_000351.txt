Milking a cow at Lake Metroparks Farmpark.
1) Wear appropriate protective gear including gloves and a mask.
2) Approach the cow gently to establish trust and comfort.
3) Position yourself behind the cow facing its rear end.
4) Use a milking machine or hand milking technique carefully.
5) Gently squeeze the teats to stimulate milk flow.
6) Ensure all teats are properly cleaned after milking.
7) Dispose of used materials safely according to farm guidelines.
8) Thank the cow for its cooperation and provide a treat if possible.
9) Clean and sanitize all equipment thoroughly before leaving.
10) Report any issues or concerns to the farm staff immediately.
