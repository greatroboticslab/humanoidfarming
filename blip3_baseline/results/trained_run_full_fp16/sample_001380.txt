Task: GROW lettuce at home | How to grow lettuce at home
Please break it into step-by-step subtasks. Include validation steps and fallback procedures.
------------------------------------------------------------
1) Choose a sunny spot for your lettuce plants.
2) Prepare the soil by adding compost or well-rotted manure.
3) Sow seeds indoors about 6 weeks before the last expected frost.
4) Transplant seedlings outdoors when temperatures stabilize.
5) Water regularly, keeping the soil moist but not waterlogged.
6) Provide support for taller varieties like butterhead lettuces.
7) Harvest leaves as needed when they reach desired size.
8) Rotate crops annually to prevent soil-borne diseases.
9) Consider using containers if space is limited indoors.
10) Monitor for pests and diseases, treating them promptly.
11) Enjoy fresh home-grown lettuce throughout the growing season.
12) Store excess lettuce in the refrigerator for later use.
