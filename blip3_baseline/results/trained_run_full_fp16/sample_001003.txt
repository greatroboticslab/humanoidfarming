Task: Furrow Irrigation System - I
Please break it into step-by-step subtasks. Include validation steps and fallback procedures.
------------------------------------------------------------
1) Identify the area needing furrow irrigation.
2) Measure the soil moisture level to determine when to irrigate.
3) Dig furrows along the length of the plants at appropriate spacing.
4) Fill the furrows with water using a sprinkler or hose.
5) Allow the water to soak into the soil gradually.
6) Monitor the irrigation system for leaks or clogs.
7) Adjust the watering schedule based on weather conditions.
8) Maintain the furrows to prevent erosion and ensure even distribution of water.
9) Clean the irrigation system after each use to prepare for future irrigation.
10) Document the irrigation process for future reference and adjustment.
