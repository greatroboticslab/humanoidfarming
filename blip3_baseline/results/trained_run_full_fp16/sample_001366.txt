Task: Person Milking a cow
Please break it into step-by-step subtasks. Include validation steps and fallback procedures.
------------------------------------------------------------
1) Approach the cow from behind to avoid startling it.
2) Place one hand under the cow's neck near the shoulder blade.
3) Use the other hand to support the cow's hindquarters.
4) Gently pull the udder towards you using your supporting hand.
5) Position the milking equipment correctly over the teat.
6) Squeeze the teat gently to release air before attaching the equipment.
7) Attach the milking equipment to the teat and begin milking.
8) Monitor the cow for signs of discomfort or distress during milking.
9) Ensure all milk is collected properly after milking is complete.
10) Clean and sanitize the equipment before storing or disposing of it.
