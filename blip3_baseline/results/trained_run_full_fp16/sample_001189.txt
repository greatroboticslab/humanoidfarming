Task: HOW TO FIX FLAT TIRES FOR 10 YEARS
Please break it into step-by-step subtasks. Provide numbered subtasks with concise justifications.
------------------------------------------------------------
1) Check if the tire is actually flat or just underinflated.
2) Use a tire pressure gauge to check the tire's current pressure.
3) Inflate the tire to the recommended pressure level for your vehicle.
4) If the tire is indeed flat, locate a safe place to change the tire.
5) Remove the lug nuts using a lug wrench.
6) Jack up the vehicle following manufacturer instructions.
7) Place the spare tire on the car and secure it with the lug nuts.
8) Lower the jack and remove the lug nuts completely.
9) Rotate the flat tire onto the spare and tighten the lug nuts.
10) Drive slowly to a mechanic or tire shop for repairs or replacement.
