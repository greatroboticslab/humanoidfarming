Task: Small Farm Irrigation - Video 1 - How to Attach a Lay Flat Header Pipe to a Garden Hose Faucet
Please break it into step-by-step subtasks. Emphasize precision, sustainability, and error checks.
------------------------------------------------------------
1) Locate the garden hose faucet on your small farm's irrigation system.
2) Identify the lay flat header pipe that needs to be attached to the faucet.
3) Turn off the water supply to the faucet to prevent accidental water flow during attachment.
4) Remove any existing connections or debris from around the faucet.
5) Place the lay flat header pipe in position over the faucet's connection port.
6) Apply soapy water to the connection points to check for leaks before tightening.
7) Use adjustable wrenches to tighten the connections securely without overtightening.
8) Turn on the water supply to test for any leaks or issues with the new connection.
9) Ensure all connections are tight and functioning properly before using the irrigation system.
10) If necessary, adjust the angle or length of the lay flat header pipe to fit the desired layout of the irrigation system.
