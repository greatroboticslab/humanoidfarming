Task: Quick And Simple Hydroponic Tomato Setup!  #hydroponics #tomato #indoorgardening
Please break it into step-by-step subtasks. Focus on safety, measurement units, and necessary tools.
------------------------------------------------------------
1) Choose a suitable indoor space for your hydroponic system.
2) Select a hydroponic setup like NFT or Drip System based on space and budget.
3) Purchase necessary components including grow lights, nutrient solution, and containers.
4) Prepare growing medium such as perlite or rockwool for tomato plants.
5) Plant tomato seeds in small pots filled with growing medium.
6) Set up water pump and tubing to deliver nutrient-rich water to plants.
7) Install grow lights providing at least 18 hours of light daily for optimal growth.
8) Fill reservoir with clean water mixed with appropriate nutrients.
9) Monitor pH levels and adjust nutrient solution as needed for healthy growth.
10) Maintain consistent temperature between 65°F and 75°F for best results.
11) Harvest tomatoes when they reach full size and color, usually about 60 days after planting.
12) Rotate plants every few weeks to ensure even growth and prevent disease.
