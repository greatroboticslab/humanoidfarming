When To Buy Or Rent Heavy Equipment
1) Assess the frequency and duration of equipment usage.
2) Evaluate the cost of buying versus renting over time.
3) Consider the equipment's condition and maintenance needs.
4) Check if there are any tax benefits for purchasing.
5) Determine if the equipment is essential for long-term projects.
6) Look at the availability of rental options in your area.
7) Compare the features and capabilities of different brands and models.
8) Consult with professionals who use similar equipment regularly.
9) Factor in potential future growth or expansion plans.
10) Review any existing inventory or assets you already own.
