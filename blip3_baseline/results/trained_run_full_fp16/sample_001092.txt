Task: Our Secrets to Growing the Best Tomato Plants

------------------------------------------------------------
1) Choose a sunny spot for your tomato plants.
2) Select disease-resistant tomato varieties suited to your climate.
3) Prepare rich soil enriched with compost and organic matter.
4) Water plants deeply but infrequently to encourage deep root growth.
5) Support plants with stakes or cages as they grow taller.
6) Prune leaves to improve air circulation and prevent fungal diseases.
7) Apply balanced fertilizer every few weeks during growing season.
8) Monitor for pests and diseases regularly, treating early if necessary.
9) Harvest tomatoes when they reach full color and firmness.
10) Rotate crops annually to reduce soil-borne diseases and pests.
