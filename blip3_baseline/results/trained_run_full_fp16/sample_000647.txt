Task: Mechanical methods of weed control
Please break it into step-by-step subtasks. Include validation steps and fallback procedures.
------------------------------------------------------------
Identify types of weeds present in the garden.
Determine the size and density of weeds to decide on appropriate control method.
Choose between manual removal, mechanical tools like hand weeder or hoe, or using mulch to prevent new growth.
Apply chosen method carefully, avoiding damage to desired plants.
Monitor garden regularly after treatment to catch and remove any new weeds promptly.
Adjust method as necessary based on effectiveness and environmental conditions.
Dispose of removed weeds properly to avoid spreading seeds or pathogens.
