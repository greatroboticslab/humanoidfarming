Task: Small Farm Irrigation - Video 2 - Backflow Prevention

------------------------------------------------------------
1) Identify potential backflow hazards in small farm irrigation systems.
2) Install appropriate backflow prevention devices at the right locations.
3) Regularly inspect and maintain backflow prevention assemblies.
4) Train farmers and staff on proper use and care of backflow prevention equipment.
5) Ensure all connections are properly sealed to prevent backflow.
6) Use check valves or other approved devices to protect against backflow.
7) Regularly test backflow prevention devices according to manufacturer guidelines.
8) Keep records of inspections, tests, and maintenance activities for compliance.
9) Educate local authorities about the importance of backflow prevention in agriculture.
10) Implement regular maintenance schedules for backflow prevention systems.
