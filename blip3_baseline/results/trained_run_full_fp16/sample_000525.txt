Task: Tractor Tire Set up
Please break it into step-by-step subtasks. Focus on safety, measurement units, and necessary tools.
------------------------------------------------------------
Set up the tractor tire by first ensuring the tractor is parked on a flat surface.
Check that all tires have proper inflation pressure according to the manufacturer's guidelines.
Inspect each tire for any signs of damage or wear before installation.
Place the new tire onto the wheel hub ensuring it aligns correctly with the other tires.
Securely tighten the lug nuts around the tire using a torque wrench following the recommended torque settings.
Rotate the tractor to ensure even wear across all tires after installation.
Verify that the tractor moves smoothly without dragging or wobbling during operation.
Clean up any debris from the work area and store tools safely when finished.
Adjust the tractor's suspension if necessary to maintain proper ride height and alignment.
Inspect the tires again after driving to check for any unusual behavior or wear patterns.
