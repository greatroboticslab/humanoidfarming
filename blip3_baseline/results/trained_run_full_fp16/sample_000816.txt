4) Implement crop rotation practices to improve soil health and reduce pest infestations.
5) Use GPS mapping to accurately mark planting areas and avoid over-sowing.

7) Monitor weather forecasts regularly to prepare for potential climate changes affecting crop growth.
8) Encourage biodiversity around the farm to attract beneficial insects that naturally control pests.
