Task: This Week in the Garden: January 21 2025 (Pre-Spring Planting)
Please break it into step-by-step subtasks. Emphasize precision, sustainability, and error checks.
------------------------------------------------------------
1) Check local weather forecasts for potential frost risks in early January.
2) Prepare garden beds by removing weeds and adding compost or well-rotted manure.
3) Plant cold-hardy vegetables such as spinach, lettuce, and radishes directly in the soil.
4) Start seeds indoors for tomatoes, peppers, and other warm-season crops.
5) Water newly planted seeds and seedlings thoroughly to settle them into the soil.
6) Apply a layer of mulch around plants to retain moisture and suppress weeds.
7) Monitor soil moisture levels regularly, especially during dry spells.
8) Consider using row covers to protect young plants from late frosts.
9) Plan next week's tasks based on plant growth and weather conditions.
10) Prepare a list of spring-flowering bulbs to plant after the last expected frost.
11) Clean tools and store them properly for safekeeping over winter.
12) Review gardening books or online resources for inspiration and tips on pre-spring planting.
