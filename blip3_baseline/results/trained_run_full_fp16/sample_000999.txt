Task: "Hay Baling 101: Essential Tips for Cutting and Storing Hay"
Please break it into step-by-step subtasks. Provide numbered subtasks with concise justifications.
------------------------------------------------------------
1) Start by choosing the right hay bales for cutting based on their age and condition.
2) Use a sharp, sturdy knife or scythe to cut the hay at the proper height.
3) Stack the cut hay in rows, leaving space between each row for air circulation.
4) Cover the stacked hay with tarps or plastic sheets to protect from rain and pests.
5) Store the hay in a dry area away from direct sunlight and sources of heat.
6) Regularly check the stored hay for signs of moisture or mold growth.
7) Rotate the stored hay periodically to ensure even drying and prevent spoilage.
8) If necessary, use a baler to compress the hay for easier storage and transportation.
9) Label each bale clearly with information about its origin and date of harvest.
10) Keep records of all hay-related activities for future reference and management purposes.
