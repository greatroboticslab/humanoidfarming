Task: Burning straw @Shorts
Please break it into step-by-step subtasks. Focus on safety, measurement units, and necessary tools.
------------------------------------------------------------
1) Gather dry straw from fields or farms for burning.
2) Choose a safe location away from buildings and forests.
3) Prepare firewood and water nearby for quick extinguishment.
4) Build a small pile of straw in the chosen area.
5) Light the center of the straw pile using a match or lighter.
6) Monitor the fire closely to prevent it from spreading.
7) Extinguish the fire completely with plenty of water when done.
8) Dispose of any remaining ashes safely in a designated area.
9) Ensure all participants wear protective gear like gloves and masks.
10) Inform neighbors about the burning activity to avoid complaints.
