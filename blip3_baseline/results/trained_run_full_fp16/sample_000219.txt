Task: Alabama Beef Systems Videos: How to Collect a Hay Sample
Please break it into step-by-step subtasks. Focus on safety, measurement units, and necessary tools.
------------------------------------------------------------
1) Obtain a clean, dry container suitable for holding the hay sample.
2) Select several areas of the hay field to ensure randomness in your sample.
3) Use a sharp knife or scissors to cut small pieces from different parts of the hay.
4) Place the cut pieces into the prepared container, making sure they are well mixed.
5) Label the container with the date, location, and type of hay collected.
6) Store the labeled container in a cool, dry place until you can send it for analysis.
7) Contact a local agricultural extension service for guidance on how to properly send the sample for testing.
