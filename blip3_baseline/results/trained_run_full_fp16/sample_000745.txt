Task: Small-Scale Irrigation Innovation Lab
Please break it into step-by-step subtasks. Include validation steps and fallback procedures.
------------------------------------------------------------
1) Identify key challenges in small-scale irrigation systems.
2) Research existing technologies and innovations for improvement.
3) Develop prototypes of new irrigation devices or methods.
4) Conduct field tests to evaluate prototype performance.
5) Gather feedback from farmers using the prototypes.
6) Refine prototypes based on collected feedback.
7) Collaborate with local agricultural experts for validation.
8) Scale up successful prototypes for wider distribution.
9) Establish partnerships with local governments and NGOs.
10) Launch marketing campaigns to promote new irrigation solutions.
11) Monitor long-term impact and sustainability of innovations.
12) Continuously update and improve irrigation technology based on data.
