Task: Whole Farm Assessment of Nutrient Extraction from Dairy Manure
Please break it into step-by-step subtasks. Emphasize precision, sustainability, and error checks.
------------------------------------------------------------
1) Identify the specific dairy farm for assessment.
2) Collect data on current nutrient levels in soil and manure.
3) Determine the amount of dairy manure applied to fields annually.
4) Analyze the nutrient content of the applied dairy manure.
5) Assess the impact of previous nutrient applications on soil health.
6) Calculate the total nutrient extraction from the soil over time.
7) Evaluate the efficiency of nutrient use in relation to crop production.
8) Identify any nutrient deficiencies or excesses in the soil.
9) Recommend adjustments to nutrient management practices based on findings.
10) Implement recommended changes and monitor their effects.
11) Repeat assessments periodically to track progress and adjust strategies as needed.
12) Document all data collected and recommendations made for future reference.
