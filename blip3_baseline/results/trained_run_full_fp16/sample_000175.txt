Task: 3-Point Middle Buster
Please break it into step-by-step subtasks. Provide numbered subtasks with concise justifications.
------------------------------------------------------------
1) Stand facing your opponent with feet shoulder-width apart.
2) Step forward with your left foot into a lunge position.
3) Punch diagonally upward with your right hand.
4) Follow through with a low kick to the opponent's midsection.
5) Quickly return to your original stance.
6) Repeat the sequence with your left leg leading.
7) Ensure your guard is up throughout the movement for protection.
