Task: How to Prepare Organic Pesticides   Year 2022
Please break it into step-by-step subtasks. Emphasize precision, sustainability, and error checks.
------------------------------------------------------------
1) Identify pests affecting crops before preparing organic pesticides.
2) Choose natural ingredients like neem oil or garlic for pest control.
3) Mix ingredients in water according to package instructions carefully.
4) Apply solution directly onto plants using a spray bottle or sprayer.
5) Ensure even coverage of all parts of the plant including undersides of leaves.
6) Avoid applying during hot midday sun to prevent burning plants.
7) Allow at least 24 hours between application and harvest if applicable.
8) Monitor treated areas regularly for effectiveness and adjust as needed.
9) Store remaining pesticide safely away from children and pets.
10) Dispose of used containers properly following local regulations.
11) Consider companion planting alongside treated crops for additional protection.
12) Practice crop rotation to reduce pest buildup over time.
