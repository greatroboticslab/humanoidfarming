Task: Pig Pen Improving Conditions for the Feeder Pigs
Please break it into step-by-step subtasks.
------------------------------------------------------------
1) Assess current living conditions of feeder pigs in the pig pen.
2) Identify areas needing improvement such as ventilation, cleanliness, and space.
3) Plan to enhance ventilation by installing fans or improving air flow.
4) Implement cleaning schedule to maintain pen hygiene regularly.
5) Allocate more space per pig to ensure adequate movement and comfort.
6) Provide proper nutrition according to pig's age and weight.
7) Monitor pigs' health closely for any signs of illness or distress.
8) Educate staff on proper pig care techniques and hygiene practices.
9) Consider adding shade structures during hot weather periods.
10) Regularly review and adjust improvements based on pig performance and health.
