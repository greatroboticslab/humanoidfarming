Task: Fundamentals of Agricultural Extension
Please break it into step-by-step subtasks.
------------------------------------------------------------
1)  Define agricultural extension as a process that disseminates knowledge and technologies to farmers.
2)  Identify key stakeholders including farmers, extension agents, researchers, and policymakers.
3)  Assess local agricultural needs through surveys and field visits.
4)  Develop educational materials tailored to farmer's literacy levels and cultural backgrounds.
5)  Organize training sessions focusing on improved farming techniques and market access strategies.
6)  Establish feedback mechanisms for continuous improvement of extension services.
7)  Monitor outcomes using performance indicators such as crop yields and income increases.
8)  Evaluate the effectiveness of extension programs periodically to ensure they meet their objectives.
