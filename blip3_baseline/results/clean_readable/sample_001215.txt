Task: The Green Thumb: Organic Growth with Cow Dung
Please break it into step-by-step subtasks.
------------------------------------------------------------
1) Identify suitable organic farming land for cow dung application.
2) Purchase or acquire healthy cows from reliable sources.
3) Ensure cows have access to fresh grass and clean water daily.
4) Regularly collect cow dung from designated areas.
5) Compost collected cow dung in well-ventilated piles.
6) Mix composted cow dung evenly with soil before planting.
7) Monitor plant growth and adjust cow dung application as needed.
8) Maintain soil health through regular composting and fertilization.
9) Educate local farmers about benefits of using cow dung in organic farming.
10) Establish partnerships with local communities for sustainable cow farming.
