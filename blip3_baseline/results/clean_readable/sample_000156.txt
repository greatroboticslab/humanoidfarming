Task: How to harvest and processing sunflower seeds | Agriculture Technology
Please break it into step-by-step subtasks.
------------------------------------------------------------
1) Identify mature sunflowers by their large, dry seed heads.
2) Cut down the harvested sunflower plants using a sickle or machete.
3) Remove the sunflower heads from the stalks carefully.
4) Spread the sunflower heads out in a well-ventilated area for about a week.
5) Allow the sunflower heads to dry completely before threshing.
6) Thresh the dried sunflower heads by beating them with sticks or a machine.
7) Sort through the threshed material to separate the seeds from chaff.
8) Clean the separated seeds by removing any remaining debris.
9) Store the cleaned sunflower seeds in a cool, dry place until ready for use.
