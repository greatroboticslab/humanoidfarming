Task: Eradicating Peste des Petits Ruminants Globally
Please break it into step-by-step subtasks.
------------------------------------------------------------
1) Conduct global surveillance for Peste des Petits Ruminants (PPR) outbreaks.
2) Develop and distribute vaccines tailored to local conditions.
3) Implement vaccination campaigns in affected areas.
4) Establish quarantine zones around infected sites.
5) Educate farmers on biosecurity measures to prevent spread.
6) Monitor livestock health through regular veterinary check-ups.
7) Collaborate with international organizations for funding and expertise.
8) Promote sustainable farming practices to reduce disease risk.
9) Strengthen national livestock health systems for long-term control.
10) Regularly review and update eradication strategies based on effectiveness.
