Task: Soil Health and Organic Grains with Miranda Sikora
Please break it into step-by-step subtasks.
------------------------------------------------------------
1) Identify key indicators of soil health for organic grain production.
2) Research methods to improve soil fertility using organic practices.
3) Analyze the impact of improved soil health on organic grain yields.
4) Develop strategies for integrating organic amendments into farming systems.
5) Evaluate the economic feasibility of implementing these strategies.
6) Collaborate with farmers to implement and monitor soil health improvements.
7) Collect data on crop performance and soil quality post-intervention.
8) Share findings and best practices through workshops and publications.
9) Advocate for policy changes supporting sustainable agricultural practices.
