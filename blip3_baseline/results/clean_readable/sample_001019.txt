Task: Pallet Garden Tool Storage
Please break it into step-by-step subtasks.
------------------------------------------------------------
1) Identify suitable locations for pallets in the garden to maximize space efficiency.
2) Clean and sanitize all pallets to prevent pests and diseases.
3) Arrange pallets in a stable configuration to support heavy storage items.
4) Securely attach pallets together using straps or nails if necessary.
5) Drill holes in pallets for drainage and water management.
6) Install shelves on pallets for better organization and accessibility.
7) Label each shelf clearly for easy identification of stored items.
8) Place heavier items at the bottom for stability and safety.
9) Stack pallets vertically only if they are securely attached and stable.
10) Regularly inspect the pallet garden tool storage system for any signs of wear or damage.
