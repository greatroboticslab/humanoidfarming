Task: Managing Nutrients on Small Farms
Please break it into step-by-step subtasks.
------------------------------------------------------------
1)  Assess soil nutrient levels through testing.
2)  Identify specific nutrient deficiencies in crops.
3)  Plan fertilization schedule based on crop needs.
4)  Choose appropriate organic or synthetic fertilizers.
5)  Apply fertilizers according to recommended rates.
6)  Monitor crop growth for nutrient uptake signs.
7)  Adjust fertilization as needed based on plant health.
8)  Maintain records of all nutrient management activities.
9)  Rotate crops to prevent depletion of soil nutrients.
10)  Consider using cover crops to replenish nutrients.
