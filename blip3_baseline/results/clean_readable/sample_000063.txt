Task: Grow to Learn - Drip Irrigation Installation Demo
Please break it into step-by-step subtasks.
------------------------------------------------------------
1) Measure the garden area requiring irrigation for accurate setup.
2) Choose appropriate drip irrigation system based on measured area.
3) Install mainline tubing along the perimeter of the garden.
4) Connect emitters at desired watering points according to plant needs.
5) Attach water source to the mainline tubing using a pressure regulator.
6) Test system for leaks or blockages before turning on water supply.
7) Turn on water supply to check if all components function correctly.
8) Adjust emitter flow rates as necessary for optimal plant hydration.
9) Monitor system performance regularly for adjustments as needed.
10) Educate gardeners on proper maintenance and troubleshooting tips.
