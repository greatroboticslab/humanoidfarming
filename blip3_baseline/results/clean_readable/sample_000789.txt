Task: Baling those OLF Windrows
Please break it into step-by-step subtasks.
------------------------------------------------------------
1) Gather necessary equipment for baling windrows.
2) Position the baler machine correctly over the windrow.
3) Adjust the baler settings according to the desired bale size.
4) Start feeding the hay into the baler.
5) Close the baler and ensure all parts are securely fastened.
6) Move the baler along the windrow as needed to complete baling.
7) Remove the bales from the baler once completed.
8) Stack the bales in a safe and organized manner.
