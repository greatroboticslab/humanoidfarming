Task: Burning straw @Shorts
Please break it into step-by-step subtasks.
------------------------------------------------------------
1)  Gather dry straw from fields or farms.
2)  Choose a safe location away from buildings and flammable materials.
3)  Prepare a small fire starter like a match or lighter.
4)  Arrange the straw in a pile to ensure even burning.
5)  Light the fire starter at the center of the straw pile.
6)  Monitor the fire closely to prevent it from spreading.
7)  Extinguish the fire completely when it is out.
