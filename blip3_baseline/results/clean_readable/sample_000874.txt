Task: HOW TO PROPERLY SIZE TRACTOR ATTACHMENTS!
Please break it into step-by-step subtasks.
------------------------------------------------------------
1) Assess the type of work required to determine the appropriate attachment size.
2) Measure the load capacity needed for the job.
3) Consider the attachment's intended use and adjust accordingly.
4) Evaluate the attachment's weight distribution for stability.
5) Check the attachment's maximum lifting height for reach.
6) Ensure the attachment fits within the tractor's cab dimensions.
7) Test the attachment on a similar task before full-scale operation.
8) Adjust the attachment's size based on operator comfort and visibility.
9) Verify that the attachment meets safety standards and regulations.
10) Plan for future expansion or replacement needs.
