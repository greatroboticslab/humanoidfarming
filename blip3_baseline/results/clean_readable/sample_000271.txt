Task: THE ONLY TOOL YOU NEED FOR GRAVEL DRIVEWAY MAINTENANCE!
Please break it into step-by-step subtasks.
------------------------------------------------------------
1) Identify the type of gravel suitable for your driveway.
2) Clear debris from the driveway before applying new gravel.
3) Use a rake to spread the gravel evenly across the surface.
4) Employ a broom or leaf blower to compact the gravel tightly.
5) Sweep away excess gravel to ensure an even layer.
6) Water the gravel lightly to settle it further and remove air pockets.
7) Apply a sealant to protect the gravel and extend its lifespan.
