Task: Billion-Dollar Chili Farms  How Chinese Farmers Grow and Harvest Chili
Please break it into step-by-step subtasks.
------------------------------------------------------------
1) Chinese farmers start by selecting high-quality chili seeds from reputable suppliers.
2) They plant these seeds in fertile soil prepared in advance for optimal growth.
3) Regular watering and fertilization ensure the chili plants receive adequate nutrients.
4) Farmers monitor the plants for pests and diseases, using natural remedies when necessary.
5) When the chilies reach maturity, they carefully harvest them by hand to preserve quality.
6) After harvesting, the chilies undergo processing such as drying or pickling for preservation.
