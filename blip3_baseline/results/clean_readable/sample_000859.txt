Task: Farm Life ~ You Never Know what You'll Be Doing
Please break it into step-by-step subtasks.
------------------------------------------------------------
1) Identify the type of farm to start, considering resources and skills available.
2) Plan crop or livestock selection based on local climate and market demand.
3) Prepare land for planting or breeding, ensuring soil quality and space.
4) Implement crop rotation or animal husbandry practices for sustainability.
5) Monitor weather conditions daily to adjust farming strategies accordingly.
6) Harvest crops or raise animals at appropriate times to maximize yield.
7) Process harvested products through drying, canning, or other preservation methods.
8) Sell produce or raise livestock through local markets or online platforms.
9) Maintain equipment and tools regularly to ensure efficient farm operations.
10) Attend workshops or read books to continuously improve farming techniques.
