Task: Farmers benefit with sustainable agricultural practices

------------------------------------------------------------
1)  Identify local sustainable agriculture resources and programs.

3)  Provide training workshops on implementing sustainable techniques.
4)  Offer incentives for adopting sustainable farming methods.
5)  Encourage collaboration among farmers to share knowledge and experiences.
6)  Monitor and evaluate the impact of sustainable practices on soil health.
7)  Advocate for policy changes that support sustainable agriculture.
8)  Promote the sale of sustainably produced crops to consumers.
9)  Support research into new sustainable agricultural technologies.
