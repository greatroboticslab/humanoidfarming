Task: Preparations before planting ginger
Please break it into step-by-step subtasks.
------------------------------------------------------------
1)  Choose a suitable location with well-draining soil and plenty of sunlight.
2)  Prepare the soil by adding compost or organic matter to improve fertility.
3)  Decide on the variety of ginger that suits your climate and purpose.
4)  Purchase healthy ginger rhizomes from a reputable source.
5)  Clean the ginger rhizomes thoroughly to remove dirt and debris.
6)  Cut the ginger rhizomes into small pieces, ensuring each piece has at least one growing point.
7)  Plant the ginger rhizome pieces about 2 inches deep in the prepared soil.
8)  Space the plants approximately 18-24 inches apart for optimal growth.
9)  Water the planted ginger regularly, maintaining consistent moisture levels.
10)  Mulch around the plants to retain soil moisture and suppress weeds.
11)  Monitor for pests and diseases, taking action as needed to protect the crop.
12)  Harvest ginger when the leaves begin to yellow and die back naturally.
