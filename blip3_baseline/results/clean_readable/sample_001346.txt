Task: Small Farm Irrigation - Video 3 - How to Plug the End of a Lay Flat Header Pipe
Please break it into step-by-step subtasks.
------------------------------------------------------------
1) Locate the end of the lay flat header pipe that needs plugging.
2) Gather appropriate plugging material such as a rubber plug or a metal cap.
3) Ensure the area around the pipe is clean and dry for better adhesion.
4) Carefully insert the plugging material into the end of the pipe.
5) Apply pressure to ensure the plugging material seals tightly against the pipe.
6) Secure the plugging material in place with tape or a clamp if necessary.
7) Check for any leaks after securing the plugging material.
