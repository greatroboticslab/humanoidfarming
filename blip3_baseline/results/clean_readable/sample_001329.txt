Task: Harvesting and Storing Water for Crop Irrigation
Please break it into step-by-step subtasks.
------------------------------------------------------------
1)  Assess local water sources including rivers, lakes, or wells.
2)  Determine irrigation needs based on crop type and climate.
3)  Install appropriate water harvesting structures like dams or tanks.
4)  Connect harvested water to irrigation systems using pipes.
5)  Regularly clean and maintain water storage facilities.
6)  Monitor water quality for safety and suitability for crops.
7)  Schedule water distribution according to crop watering requirements.
8)  Implement rainwater harvesting techniques during rainy seasons.
9)  Store excess water in underground reservoirs for dry periods.
10)  Use drip irrigation systems for efficient water use and soil moisture retention.
