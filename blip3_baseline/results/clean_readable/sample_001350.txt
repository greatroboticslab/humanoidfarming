Task: Impact of some agricultural practices on soil quality
Please break it into step-by-step subtasks.
------------------------------------------------------------
1) Identify specific agricultural practices affecting soil quality.
2) Assess how these practices alter soil structure and composition.
3) Determine changes in soil organic matter content due to practices.
4) Evaluate impacts on soil microbial activity and biodiversity.
5) Analyze effects on soil water retention and nutrient cycling.
6) Consider long-term sustainability implications of identified practices.
7) Compare benefits and drawbacks of each identified agricultural practice.
