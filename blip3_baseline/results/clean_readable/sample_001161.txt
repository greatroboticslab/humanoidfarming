Task: Soil Testing for Gardens
Please break it into step-by-step subtasks.
------------------------------------------------------------
1)  Choose a representative sample area for testing.
2)  Use a soil probe or spade to collect soil samples from depths of 6 inches and 18 inches.
3)  Mix the collected soil samples thoroughly in a clean container.
4)  Label each sample clearly with its location and depth information.
5)  Send the labeled soil samples to a local agricultural extension office or a laboratory for analysis.
6)  Request tests for pH level, nutrient content, organic matter, and texture.
7)  Wait for the results from the laboratory.
8)  Based on the test results, adjust soil amendments as needed for optimal garden health.
9)  Apply recommended amounts of fertilizers, compost, or other soil amendments.
10)  Record all test results and future soil conditions for ongoing garden management.
