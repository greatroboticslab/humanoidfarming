Task: OGH - Installing Off Grid Submersible Solar Water Pump for our Developed Spring
Please break it into step-by-step subtasks.
------------------------------------------------------------
1) Assess site conditions for optimal solar panel placement.
2) Determine water flow rate and pump capacity needs.
3) Install foundation for submersible pump in spring area.
4) Connect solar panels to charge controller.
5) Wire charge controller to battery bank.
6) Position battery bank near pump for power storage.
7) Install submersible pump in spring water.
8) Run suction hose from pump to water collection point.
9) Connect discharge hose from pump to irrigation system.
10) Test all connections for leaks and functionality.
11) Set up monitoring system for pump performance.
12) Train local operators on maintenance and operation.
