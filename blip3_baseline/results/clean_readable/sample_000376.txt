Task: Harvest & count a Turing's sunflower
Please break it into step-by-step subtasks.
------------------------------------------------------------
1) Identify a mature Turing's sunflower plant.
2) Observe the flower head for symmetry and uniformity.
3) Count the number of seeds in the flower head.
4) Measure the distance between the first two rows of seeds.
5) Count the number of spirals in each direction.
6) Calculate the ratio of these numbers to determine if it is close to the golden ratio.
7) Record all measurements and counts for analysis.
