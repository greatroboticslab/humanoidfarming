Task: Small Farm Harvests that earn $4000/week on 1/2 Acre of Land
Please break it into step-by-step subtasks.
------------------------------------------------------------
1) Identify suitable crops for high yield per acre.
2) Determine optimal planting and harvesting schedule.
3) Invest in necessary farming equipment and tools.
4) Secure reliable water supply for crop growth.
5) Implement pest control measures to protect crops.
6) Monitor soil health and apply necessary nutrients.
7) Train farm workers in efficient agricultural practices.
8) Establish market connections for selling produce.
9) Calculate weekly earnings based on current market prices.
10) Adjust strategies if weekly earnings fall below $4000.
