Task: Rice Harvesting: Field to Machine
Please break it into step-by-step subtasks.
------------------------------------------------------------
1)  Identify suitable rice varieties for the local climate.
2)  Prepare the field by plowing and harrowing to loosen soil.
3)  Apply fertilizers and pesticides as per agricultural guidelines.
4)  Plant seeds at appropriate depth and spacing in rows.
5)  Monitor crop growth and manage weeds manually or mechanically.
6)  Water the fields regularly to support plant development.
7)  Harvest when grains turn from green to golden brown.
8)  Use mechanical harvesters to cut and thresh the rice.
9)  Sort harvested rice to remove debris and damaged grains.
10)  Store the cleaned rice in dry, cool conditions to prevent spoilage.
