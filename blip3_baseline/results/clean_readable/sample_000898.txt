Task: The Bhungroo (Straw) - An Intervention for  water logging and irrigation in saline soils
Please break it into step-by-step subtasks.
------------------------------------------------------------
1) Identify areas affected by water logging and saline soil conditions.
2) Collect and prepare local straw or agricultural waste.
3) Cut the straw into small, manageable lengths.
4) Dig shallow trenches across the affected fields.
5) Insert the prepared straw into the trenches.
6) Cover the straw with a thin layer of soil to secure it.
7) Ensure adequate spacing between straw placements for proper drainage.
8) Monitor the effectiveness of the intervention periodically.
9) Adjust the straw placement as necessary based on observed results.
10) Consider integrating other sustainable farming practices alongside Bhungroo.
