Task: Simple Machines Make Life Easier for Poor Farmers
Please break it into step-by-step subtasks.
------------------------------------------------------------
1)  Identify simple machines commonly used in agriculture.
2)  Assess how these machines improve farming efficiency.
3)  Research specific examples of simple machines aiding poor farmers.
4)  Analyze the impact on crop yields and labor reduction.
5)  Develop strategies to distribute affordable simple machines to farmers.
6)  Collaborate with local agricultural organizations for implementation.
7)  Monitor and evaluate the effectiveness of distributed simple machines.
8)  Share success stories and lessons learned with other communities.
