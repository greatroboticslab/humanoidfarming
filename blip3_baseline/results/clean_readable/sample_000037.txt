Task: How do you manage pests and diseases in strawberries? This is what you need to do!
Please break it into step-by-step subtasks.
------------------------------------------------------------
1)  Identify common pests and diseases affecting strawberries.
2)  Monitor plants regularly for signs of infestation or infection.
3)  Apply organic or chemical treatments as needed based on severity.
4)  Practice crop rotation to reduce pest and disease buildup.
5)  Use physical barriers like row covers to protect plants from insects.
6)  Introduce beneficial insects to control harmful pests naturally.
7)  Maintain proper soil moisture levels to enhance plant health.
8)  Ensure adequate air circulation around plants to prevent fungal growth.
9)  Remove and destroy infected plant material to prevent spread.
10)  Implement integrated pest management strategies for long-term control.
