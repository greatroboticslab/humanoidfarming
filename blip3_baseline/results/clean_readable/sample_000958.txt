Task: How to grow Giant Ceasar Lettuce
Please break it into step-by-step subtasks.
------------------------------------------------------------
1) Start by selecting a suitable location that receives at least 6 hours of direct sunlight daily.
2) Choose seeds specifically labeled for giant lettuce varieties from reputable suppliers.
3) Prepare the soil by adding compost and ensuring good drainage.
4) Sow seeds directly in the garden or use seed trays indoors if starting early.
5) Keep soil consistently moist but not waterlogged during germination.
6) Thin seedlings when they reach about 2 inches tall, spacing them 12-18 inches apart.
7) Provide support such as stakes or cages as plants grow taller.
8) Water deeply once a week, reducing watering during dry spells.
9) Apply balanced fertilizer every two weeks to promote healthy growth.
10) Monitor for pests and diseases regularly, addressing issues promptly.
11) Harvest outer leaves when they reach 6-8 inches long for best flavor and texture.
12) Continue harvesting as needed, allowing the plant to continue producing new leaves.
