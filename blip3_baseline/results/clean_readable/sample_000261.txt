Task: Optimizing Agricultural Practices: Balancing Manure Use and Minimizing Compaction

------------------------------------------------------------

2) Plan manure application timing to avoid peak growing seasons.
3) Implement crop rotation to reduce reliance on manure nutrients.
4) Use precision agriculture tools to apply manure accurately.
5) Schedule manure application during dry weather conditions.
