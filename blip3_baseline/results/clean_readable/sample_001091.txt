Task: Cleaning Out a VSI Deicing Sprayer for Storage
Please break it into step-by-step subtasks.
------------------------------------------------------------
1) Open the sprayer's lid and remove all components.
2) Drain any remaining water from the tank and nozzles.
3) Use a soft brush to clean the interior surfaces of the tank.
4) Disassemble parts that can be removed for thorough cleaning.
5) Rinse all removable parts under clean water.
6) Scrub the spray hose and nozzle with a mild detergent solution.
7) Allow all parts to air dry completely before reassembly.
8) Store the cleaned sprayer in a dry area until needed.
