Task: Exploring Farming Practices in Canada
Please break it into step-by-step subtasks.
------------------------------------------------------------
1)  Identify key regions of Canada known for farming.
2)  Research historical and current agricultural practices.
3)  Examine modern farming techniques used in Canada.
4)  Analyze the impact of climate change on Canadian agriculture.
5)  Investigate government policies affecting Canadian farmers.
6)  Explore traditional farming methods still practiced in Canada.
7)  Understand the role of technology in Canadian agricultural production.
8)  Study the export markets for Canadian farm products.
9)  Look at challenges faced by Canadian farmers today.
10)  Compare Canadian farming practices to those in other countries.
