Task: Polydome Sustainable Agriculture System
Please break it into step-by-step subtasks.
------------------------------------------------------------
1) Assess local climate conditions for optimal polydome placement.
2) Design polydome structure with sustainable materials and energy efficiency.
3) Install hydroponic systems for nutrient-rich soilless growing.
4) Integrate solar panels or wind turbines for renewable energy sources.
5) Implement rainwater harvesting system to reduce water usage.
6) Select crops suitable for indoor cultivation and high humidity.
7) Monitor temperature and humidity levels inside the polydome regularly.
8) Use LED grow lights to ensure consistent light exposure for plants.
9) Schedule automated irrigation based on plant needs and weather forecasts.
10) Train staff in proper crop care and disease prevention techniques.
