Task: How to grow a TROPICAL JUNGLE in a small garden
Please break it into step-by-step subtasks.
------------------------------------------------------------
1) Choose a sunny spot with well-draining soil rich in organic matter.
2) Prepare the garden bed by removing weeds and adding compost.
3) Plant a variety of tropical plants including palms, ferns, and orchids.
4) Water deeply but infrequently to encourage deep root growth.
5) Mulch around plants to retain moisture and suppress weeds.
6) Provide support for climbing plants like vines and lianas.
7) Regularly prune to maintain shape and promote healthy growth.
8) Monitor for pests and diseases, treating as needed.
9) Supplement with fertilizer specifically formulated for tropical plants.
10) Encourage pollinators and beneficial insects for natural pest control.
11) Consider using hydroponics or aquaponics systems for more efficient growing.
12) Keep an eye on temperature and humidity levels to mimic tropical conditions.
