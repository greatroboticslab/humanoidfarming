Task: How regenerative farming reduces costs
Please break it into step-by-step subtasks.
------------------------------------------------------------
1) Enhances soil health through composting and crop rotation, reducing fertilizer needs.
2) Builds soil organic matter, improving water retention and nutrient cycling.
3) Supports diverse ecosystems that control pests naturally, minimizing pesticide use.
4) Increases biodiversity, attracting beneficial insects and pollinators.
5) Reduces energy consumption by utilizing renewable sources for irrigation.
6) Encourages local food production, lowering transportation costs.
7) Improves livestock health, reducing veterinary expenses and feed costs.
8) Promotes recycling of farm waste, decreasing landfill fees.
9) Strengthens community resilience, leading to fewer external support services.
10) Creates jobs in sustainable agriculture practices, stimulating local economies.
