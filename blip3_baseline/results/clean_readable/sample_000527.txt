Task: Whole Farm Assessment of Nutrient Extraction from Dairy Manure
Please break it into step-by-step subtasks.
------------------------------------------------------------
1) Identify all sources of dairy manure on the farm.
2) Collect samples from each source for nutrient analysis.
3) Determine the total volume of manure produced annually.
4) Assess the current nutrient content in the collected samples.
5) Calculate the annual nutrient output based on sample analysis.
6) Evaluate the land area available for nutrient absorption.
7) Analyze soil tests to understand current nutrient levels.
8) Develop a plan for nutrient management and application rates.
9) Implement the nutrient management plan and monitor its effectiveness.
10) Regularly update the nutrient extraction assessment based on new data.
