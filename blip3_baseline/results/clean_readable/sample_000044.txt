Task: Sunflower Harvesting Machine - How to harvest and processing sunflower seeds
Please break it into step-by-step subtasks.
------------------------------------------------------------
1) Assess the maturity of sunflowers for harvesting.
2) Gather necessary equipment including a harvester or manual tools.
3) Position the machine or tools under the sunflowers.
4) Use the machine or manually cut the heads from the stalks.
5) Remove the seeds from the flower heads using a separator.
6) Clean the separated seeds to remove debris.
7) Sort the seeds based on size and quality.
8) Dry the sorted seeds in a well-ventilated area.
9) Store the dried seeds in airtight containers.
10) Package the seeds for sale or distribution.
