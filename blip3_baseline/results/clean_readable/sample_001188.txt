------------------------------------------------------------

3)  Prepare nutrient-rich soil mix tailored to eggplants' needs.
4)  Plant eggplants in individual pots ensuring proper spacing.
5)  Water plants regularly maintaining consistent moisture levels.
6)  Provide adequate sunlight exposure for optimal growth.
