Task: It's a Warm Spring... When Can I start Planting?
Please break it into step-by-step subtasks.
------------------------------------------------------------
1) Determine your location's average last frost date.
2) Check local climate conditions for spring planting suitability.
3) Choose plants that thrive in your specific climate zone.
4) Consider the soil temperature to ensure seeds germinate properly.
5) Plan when to sow seeds indoors or directly outdoors based on plant type.
6) Prepare garden beds by adding compost and removing weeds.
7) Water the area thoroughly before planting to settle the soil.
