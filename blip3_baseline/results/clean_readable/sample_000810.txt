Task: Amazing technique of climbing coconut tree #agriculture #tendercoconut #howto #coconuttree #farming
Please break it into step-by-step subtasks.
------------------------------------------------------------
1) Start at the base of the coconut tree, facing the trunk.
2) Grip the trunk firmly with both hands, palms down.
3) Climb upward, alternating hands to maintain balance.
4) Use your legs to pull yourself up as you climb.
5) Reach for branches or vines to support your ascent.
6) Once near the top, use a vine or branch to swing higher.
7) Position yourself in a secure spot to harvest tender coconuts.
