Task: Grow More Give More: Raised Bed Soil Test
Please break it into step-by-step subtasks.
------------------------------------------------------------
1)  Select a suitable location for the raised bed that receives at least six hours of sunlight daily.
2)  Measure and record the soil pH level using a soil testing kit or service.
3)  Collect soil samples from various depths within the raised bed for testing.
4)  Mix in organic matter like compost or well-rotted manure to improve soil structure and fertility.
5)  Determine the nutrient content of the soil through laboratory analysis if needed.
6)  Adjust soil pH as necessary by adding lime for alkaline soils or sulfur for acidic soils.
7)  Based on test results, decide which amendments to add to balance soil nutrients.
8)  Mix recommended amounts of amendments thoroughly into the raised bed soil.
9)  Rake the soil surface smooth after mixing to ensure even distribution of nutrients and amendments.
10)  Plant seeds or seedlings according to the specific needs of the chosen plants.
