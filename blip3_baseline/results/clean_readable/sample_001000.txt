Task: Olive Harvesting with a Tree Shaker - Jared Gulian
Please break it into step-by-step subtasks.
------------------------------------------------------------
1) Inspect the olive trees for ripeness and health.
2) Position the tree shaker at the base of the trees.
3) Activate the shaker to vibrate the branches gently.
4) Monitor the shaking process to ensure even coverage.
5) Collect fallen olives from the ground carefully.
6) Repeat the process until all trees are harvested.
