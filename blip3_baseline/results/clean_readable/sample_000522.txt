Task: How to make Carbonized Rice Hull
Please break it into step-by-step subtasks.
------------------------------------------------------------
1)  Obtain rice hulls from a local source or purchase them online.
2)  Clean the rice hulls thoroughly to remove any dirt or debris.
3)  Spread the cleaned rice hulls evenly on a baking sheet.
4)  Preheat an oven to 300 degrees Fahrenheit (150 degrees Celsius).
5)  Place the baking sheet in the preheated oven.
6)  Bake the rice hulls for approximately 2 hours, stirring occasionally.
7)  Once done, remove the baking sheet from the oven.
8)  Allow the carbonized rice hulls to cool completely before storing.
9)  Store the carbonized rice hulls in an airtight container.
