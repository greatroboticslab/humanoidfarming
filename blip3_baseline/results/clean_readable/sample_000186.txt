Task: Winter Livestock Grazing
Please break it into step-by-step subtasks.
------------------------------------------------------------
1) Assess weather forecasts for upcoming weeks to plan grazing areas accordingly.
2) Check livestock health and nutrition needs before starting grazing season.
3) Prepare pastures by removing dead plants and applying fertilizer if necessary.
4) Establish fencing or boundaries for designated grazing areas.
5) Gradually introduce livestock to new grazing areas over several days.
6) Monitor livestock closely for signs of stress or illness during transition.
7) Adjust grazing patterns as needed based on pasture condition and animal health.
8) Provide supplemental feed if necessary to ensure adequate nutrition.
9) Clean and maintain equipment used for moving livestock between pastures.
10) Plan for winter protection measures such as shelter construction or relocation.
