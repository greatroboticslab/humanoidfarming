------------------------------------------------------------
Pest control involves understanding the ecology and environment of pests.
Identify the specific type of pest for targeted control methods.
Research natural predators or biological controls to reduce pest populations.
Implement integrated pest management strategies combining chemical and non-chemical methods.
Monitor pest populations regularly to assess effectiveness of implemented measures.
Educate farmers and community members about proper pest control practices.
Dispose of pest-infested materials safely to prevent spread of pests.
Conduct further research on pest behavior and adapt control methods accordingly.
