Task: Strawberry Harvest  #strawberryharvest #harvesting #farminnovation #agriculture #shorts
Please break it into step-by-step subtasks.
------------------------------------------------------------
1) Identify the strawberry fields ready for harvest season.
2) Gather necessary tools like pruning shears and harvesting baskets.
3) Wear protective clothing to avoid skin contact with soil.
4) Check for ripe strawberries that are firm and show bright color.
5) Use gentle force to pick strawberries without bruising them.
6) Sort picked strawberries into different quality categories.
7) Transport harvested strawberries to designated storage areas.
8) Monitor weather conditions to prevent damage during transportation.
9) Prepare for next harvest cycle by maintaining field health.
