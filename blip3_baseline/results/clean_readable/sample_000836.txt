Task: No-Tillage Farming
Please break it into step-by-step subtasks.
------------------------------------------------------------
Plant seeds directly in the previous year's crop residue.
Remove weeds manually or with minimal tillage equipment.
Apply fertilizer and other nutrients using precision methods.
Monitor soil moisture levels and adjust irrigation as needed.
Harvest crops according to their maturity stages.
Implement cover crops between growing seasons for soil health.
Rotate crops annually to prevent depletion of soil nutrients.
