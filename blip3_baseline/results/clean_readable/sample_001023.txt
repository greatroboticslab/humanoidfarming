Task: How to Make Safe Pesticide
Please break it into step-by-step subtasks.
------------------------------------------------------------
1)  Identify the type of pest requiring treatment.
2)  Research and select an appropriate pesticide suitable for the identified pest.
3)  Read and understand all label instructions carefully.
4)  Determine the correct dosage based on the pesticide's specifications.
5)  Choose the right application method for the target area.
6)  Wear protective clothing including gloves, goggles, and a mask.
7)  Apply pesticides according to the manufacturer's guidelines.
8)  Ensure proper disposal of unused pesticide containers.
9)  Avoid applying pesticides during rain or high humidity conditions.
10)  Keep children and pets away from treated areas until safe to return.
