Task: 2. IRRI Agronomy Challenge 2: Transplanting Day (20 December 2012)
Please break it into step-by-step subtasks.
------------------------------------------------------------
1)  Identify suitable planting sites based on soil quality and climate conditions.
2)  Prepare the soil by removing weeds and adding organic matter as needed.
3)  Select healthy seedlings from the nursery for transplanting.
4)  Water the seedlings thoroughly to ensure they are well-hydrated before transplanting.
5)  Choose a day with favorable weather conditions for transplanting.
6)  Train staff in proper transplanting techniques to minimize stress on the seedlings.
7)  Measure and mark the planting rows to ensure even spacing between plants.
8)  Use sharp tools to cut the seedlings from the nursery pots without damaging roots.
9)  Dig holes slightly larger than the root ball of each seedling to accommodate its growth.
10)  Place the seedlings in their respective holes and gently press soil around them.
11)  Water the transplanted seedlings immediately after planting to settle the soil.
12)  Monitor the transplanted seedlings closely for signs of stress or disease.
