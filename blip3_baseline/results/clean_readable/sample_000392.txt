Task: DIY Ultra-Powerful Natural Germicide JADAM Sulfur(JS) 10L for Gardeners. Homemade pesticide
Please break it into step-by-step subtasks.
------------------------------------------------------------
1) Gather ingredients including sulfur powder, water, and a large mixing container.
2) Measure out 1 kg of sulfur powder for every 10 liters of water.
3) Pour the sulfur powder into the water in the mixing container.
4) Stir the mixture thoroughly until the sulfur is fully dissolved.
5) Allow the solution to sit undisturbed for at least 24 hours to activate its germicidal properties.
6) Strain the solution through cheesecloth or a fine mesh to remove any undissolved sulfur particles.
7) Transfer the strained solution into a clean, labeled spray bottle.
8) Label the spray bottle clearly with the name "JADAM Sulfur(JS)" and the date made.
9) Test the effectiveness of the homemade pesticide on a small, inconspicuous area of your garden first.
10) Apply the JADAM Sulfur(JS) solution to plants as needed, focusing on undersides of leaves where pests often hide.
11) Reapply the solution every 7-10 days or as needed based on pest infestation levels.
12) Store the remaining solution in a cool, dry place away from direct sunlight.
