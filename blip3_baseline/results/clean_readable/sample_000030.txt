Task: Lesson 21  Concept of Sustainable Agriculture
Please break it into step-by-step subtasks.
------------------------------------------------------------
1) Define sustainable agriculture as practices that meet current needs without compromising future generations' ability to meet their own needs.
2) Explain how sustainable agriculture integrates ecological, economic, and social considerations.
3) Discuss the importance of biodiversity in sustainable farming systems.
4) Outline key principles of sustainable agriculture including soil health, water conservation, and crop rotation.
5) Describe the role of organic farming in promoting sustainable agricultural practices.
6) Highlight the benefits of agroforestry for enhancing sustainability in agriculture.
7) Explain the concept of permaculture and its application in sustainable agriculture.
8) Discuss the challenges faced in implementing sustainable agriculture globally.
9) Identify examples of successful sustainable agriculture initiatives worldwide.
10) Conclude by emphasizing the need for continuous education and innovation in sustainable agriculture.
