Task: Grow Your Dream: A Guide to a Productive Vegetable Garden
Please break it into step-by-step subtasks.
------------------------------------------------------------
1)  Choose a sunny spot for your garden that receives at least six hours of direct sunlight daily.
2)  Plan your garden layout based on the vegetables you wish to grow.
3)  Prepare the soil by removing weeds and adding compost or organic matter.
4)  Decide on the type of containers or raised beds you will use for your garden.
5)  Start seeds indoors four to six weeks before the last expected frost.
6)  Transplant seedlings outdoors when nighttime temperatures remain above 50°F (10°C).
7)  Water your plants deeply once or twice a week, depending on weather conditions.
8)  Mulch around plants to retain moisture and suppress weeds.
9)  Rotate crops annually to prevent soil depletion and reduce pest issues.
10)  Monitor for pests and diseases regularly and treat them promptly.
11)  Harvest vegetables when they reach maturity for the best flavor and nutrition.
12)  Enjoy the fruits of your labor and share your fresh produce with friends and family.
