Task: THE SECRET TO PLANTING GARLIC FOR PROFIT (AND SAVING YOUR SEED)
Please break it into step-by-step subtasks.
------------------------------------------------------------
1) Choose garlic varieties suitable for your climate.
2) Purchase healthy garlic cloves from a reputable source.
3) Plant cloves in well-draining soil at least 4 inches deep.
4) Space plants about 6 inches apart for optimal growth.
5) Water regularly to keep soil consistently moist.
6) Mulch around plants to retain moisture and suppress weeds.
7) Monitor for pests and diseases, treating as needed.
8) Harvest when tops begin to yellow and dry naturally.
9) Store bulbs in a cool, dry place until ready to plant again.
10) Rotate planting locations annually to prevent soil depletion.
