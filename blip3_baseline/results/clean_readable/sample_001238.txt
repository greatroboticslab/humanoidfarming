Task: How to control weeds in wheat |weeds control in wheat
Please break it into step-by-step subtasks.
------------------------------------------------------------
1) Identify common weeds that affect wheat growth.
2) Determine the best time for weed control based on local climate.
3) Apply herbicides according to product instructions and weather conditions.
4) Use mechanical methods like hoeing or cultivation for early season weed management.
5) Plant cover crops to suppress weed growth between wheat harvests.
6) Rotate crops to reduce weed populations in subsequent wheat fields.
7) Monitor fields regularly for new weed infestations and adjust control strategies accordingly.
