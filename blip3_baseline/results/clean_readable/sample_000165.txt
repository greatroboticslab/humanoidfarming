Task: Yemen: Protecting sheep and goats against disease
Please break it into step-by-step subtasks.
------------------------------------------------------------
1) Identify common diseases affecting sheep and goats in Yemen.
2) Educate local farmers on prevention methods for these diseases.
3) Implement quarantine measures to prevent disease spread.
4) Provide farmers with vaccines and treatment options.
5) Establish veterinary clinics in rural areas for immediate care.
6) Monitor livestock health regularly through check-ups and screenings.
7) Encourage farmers to report any sick animals promptly.
8) Distribute information about early signs of disease symptoms.
9) Collaborate with international organizations for additional support.
10) Promote biosecurity practices such as clean water and feed.
