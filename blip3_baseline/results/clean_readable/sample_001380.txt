Task: GROW lettuce at home | How to grow lettuce at home
Please break it into step-by-step subtasks.
------------------------------------------------------------
1) Choose a sunny spot for your lettuce plants.
2) Prepare the soil by adding compost or well-rotted manure.
3) Sow seeds indoors about 6 weeks before the last expected frost.
4) Plant seedlings outdoors after all danger of frost has passed.
5) Water regularly, keeping soil consistently moist but not soggy.
6) Provide support if growing taller varieties like butterhead lettuces.
7) Harvest outer leaves when they reach desired size for crispness.
8) Rotate crops annually to prevent soil-borne diseases.
9) Consider using containers or raised beds for easier maintenance.
10) Monitor for pests and diseases, treating as needed.
