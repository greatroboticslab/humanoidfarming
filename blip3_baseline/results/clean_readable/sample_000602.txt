Task: 5 Small Space Gardening Tips and Tricks! // Balcony Vegetable Gardening for Beginners
Please break it into step-by-step subtasks.
------------------------------------------------------------
1)  Start by selecting dwarf or compact plant varieties suitable for small spaces.
2)  Choose containers that fit your balcony size and have adequate drainage holes.
3)  Use vertical gardening techniques like hanging baskets or wall-mounted planters to maximize space.
4)  Incorporate companion planting strategies to optimize growing conditions and deter pests.
5)  Water plants regularly but ensure they don't sit in waterlogged soil to prevent root rot.
