Task: Let's begin to farm with the JADAM No-Till method.
Please break it into step-by-step subtasks.
------------------------------------------------------------
1) Identify suitable land for JADAM No-Till farming.
2) Clear existing vegetation without disturbing soil structure.
3) Plant cover crops or seeds directly into the cleared area.
4) Monitor soil moisture levels regularly.
5) Apply organic matter as needed to maintain soil health.
6) Introduce beneficial insects and microorganisms to control pests naturally.
7) Harvest crops according to their maturity stages.
8) Rotate crops annually to prevent depletion of soil nutrients.
9) Maintain a buffer zone around the farm to protect biodiversity.
10) Adapt practices based on local climate and soil conditions.
