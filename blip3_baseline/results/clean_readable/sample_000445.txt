Task: Ministry of Agriculture partners with TTM on okra cultivation techniques
Please break it into step-by-step subtasks.
------------------------------------------------------------
1) Identify key okra cultivation challenges through research.
2) Formulate new okra cultivation techniques based on findings.
3) Collaborate with TTM to develop and test these techniques.
4) Train agricultural experts in the use of new okra cultivation methods.
5) Implement the new techniques in pilot farms across the country.
6) Monitor and evaluate the success of the new okra cultivation methods.
7) Share successful results and best practices with other ministries and regions.
