Task: Daily routine as a farmer #farmer
Please break it into step-by-step subtasks.
------------------------------------------------------------
1)  Wake up early to tend to livestock and check weather forecasts.
2)  Water crops according to soil moisture levels and plant needs.
3)  Weed fields to prevent pests and maintain crop health.
4)  Harvest ripe produce using appropriate tools and techniques.
5)  Feed animals with balanced diets to ensure growth and health.
6)  Inspect crops for signs of disease or damage, treating as necessary.
7)  Prepare land for next planting season by plowing and fertilizing.
8)  Monitor water supply and irrigation systems for efficiency and maintenance.
9)  Attend to any mechanical issues with farm equipment and vehicles.
10)  Plan and organize tasks for the upcoming days based on weather and crop conditions.
