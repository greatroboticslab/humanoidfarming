Task: From technology transfer (TT) to agricultural innovation systems (AIS)
Please break it into step-by-step subtasks.
------------------------------------------------------------
1) Identify key technologies for agriculture through TT.
2) Assess local agricultural needs and conditions.
3) Develop tailored agricultural innovations based on assessment.
4) Train farmers in new techniques and technologies.
5) Establish farmer-to-farmer learning networks.
6) Monitor and evaluate innovation impact regularly.
7) Adapt and improve innovations based on feedback.
8) Integrate AIS principles into policy frameworks.
