Task: Starting a Food Forest on the Cheap!
Please break it into step-by-step subtasks.
------------------------------------------------------------
1)  Research local food forest designs and principles.
2)  Identify free or low-cost materials for construction.
3)  Choose a suitable location with good soil and sunlight.
4)  Plan the layout of trees, shrubs, and perennials.
5)  Obtain seeds or saplings from community gardens or nurseries.
6)  Start small with easy-to-grow plants like strawberries or beans.
7)  Build raised beds or use existing garden beds for planting.
8)  Install irrigation systems using rainwater harvesting methods.
9)  Integrate companion planting to enhance growth and health.
10)  Monitor plant growth regularly and adjust as needed.
