Task: Field to Fork 2019: Best Management Practices to Produce More Potatoes
Please break it into step-by-step subtasks.
------------------------------------------------------------
1) Identify key challenges in potato production.
2) Research existing best management practices.
3) Evaluate effectiveness of each practice.
4) Select practices that enhance potato yield.
5) Implement selected practices on farms.
6) Monitor implementation for improvement.
7) Collect data on increased potato production.
8) Share successful practices with farmers.
9) Advocate for policy changes supporting these practices.
10) Continue research and refine practices annually.
