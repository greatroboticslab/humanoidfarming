Task: "Hay Baling 101: Essential Tips for Cutting and Storing Hay"
Please break it into step-by-step subtasks.
------------------------------------------------------------
1)  Start by assessing the hay crop's moisture content to determine when to bale.
2)  Choose the right equipment for cutting and baling based on your farm size.
3)  Cut the hay at the correct stage of maturity for best quality.
4)  Use a baler that fits your hay type and desired bale size.
5)  Secure the bales tightly to prevent them from becoming damp or molding.
6)  Store hay in a dry, well-ventilated area to maintain its quality.
7)  Rotate stored hay regularly to ensure even drying and prevent spoilage.
8)  Cover stored hay to protect it from rain and dust.
9)  Stack bales neatly to maximize space and prevent damage during storage.
10)  Label each stack clearly with the date of baling and any other relevant information.
