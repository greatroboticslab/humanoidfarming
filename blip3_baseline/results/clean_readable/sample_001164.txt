Task: Daily Life Episode 60 Preparing the Land for Planting Season
Please break it into step-by-step subtasks.
------------------------------------------------------------
1) Assess soil quality to determine if amendments are needed.
2) Clear weeds and debris from the planting area.
3) Till the soil to improve its structure and depth.
4) Test pH levels to ensure they are suitable for desired crops.
5) Apply necessary fertilizers based on soil test results.
6) Level the ground to create a flat surface for planting.
7) Mark rows for straight planting of seeds or seedlings.
8) Water the prepared land thoroughly before planting begins.
