Task: Revolutionize Your Farm with Solar Irrigation!
Please break it into step-by-step subtasks.
------------------------------------------------------------
1)  Assess your farm's water needs and solar resource availability.
2)  Choose appropriate solar panels for energy generation.
3)  Design a solar-powered irrigation system tailored to your land.
4)  Install a pump capable of handling the required water volume.
5)  Connect the pump to the solar panels for electricity supply.
6)  Select and install a storage tank for collected water.
7)  Set up a distribution network to deliver water to different fields.
8)  Program the system to operate efficiently based on weather conditions.
9)  Integrate sensors to monitor soil moisture levels and adjust watering accordingly.
10)  Train staff on how to maintain and troubleshoot the new irrigation system.
