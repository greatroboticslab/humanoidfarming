Task: Winter Livestock Care
Please break it into step-by-step subtasks.
------------------------------------------------------------
1) Check weather forecasts for upcoming cold spells.
2) Increase bedding in livestock shelters to prevent drafts.
3) Provide extra hay or grain to ensure adequate nutrition.
4) Inspect water sources for ice formation and thaw them if necessary.
5) Clean out manure from barns to maintain hygiene.
6) Secure outdoor feeders and water troughs against strong winds.
7) Monitor livestock closely for signs of illness or distress.
8) Prepare emergency supplies including food, water, and medical kits.
9) Ensure all livestock have access to fresh, unfrozen water at all times.
10) Plan for potential power outages by storing fuel for heating systems.
