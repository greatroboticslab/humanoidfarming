Task: 10 Essential GARDENING Tips for Beginners
Please break it into step-by-step subtasks.
------------------------------------------------------------
1)  Start by choosing a suitable location for your garden that receives at least six hours of sunlight daily.
2)  Plan your garden layout considering plant growth needs and space availability.
3)  Select easy-to-grow plants suited to your climate and soil type.
4)  Prepare the soil by testing its pH level and adding necessary nutrients.
5)  Use organic mulch to retain moisture and suppress weeds.
6)  Water plants regularly but ensure not to overwater, especially during dry spells.
7)  Apply appropriate fertilizers based on your soil test results and plant needs.
8)  Practice crop rotation to prevent soil depletion and reduce pest problems.
9)  Regularly prune plants to encourage healthy growth and maintain shape.
10)  Monitor your garden for signs of pests or diseases and address them promptly.
