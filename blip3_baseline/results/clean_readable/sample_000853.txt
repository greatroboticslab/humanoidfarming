Task: Introduction to Agricultural Extension Education
Please break it into step-by-step subtasks.
------------------------------------------------------------
Agricultural extension education aims to improve agricultural productivity and rural livelihoods.
Identify the target audience for agricultural extension education programs.
Determine the specific needs of the target audience through surveys or focus groups.
Develop educational materials tailored to the identified needs.
Organize training sessions or workshops based on the developed materials.
Provide ongoing support to participants through follow-up meetings or online resources.
Evaluate the effectiveness of the educational programs periodically.
