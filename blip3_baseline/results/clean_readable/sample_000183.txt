Task: BREAKING GROUND AND STARTING A NEW GARDEN-PREPARING #garden
Please break it into step-by-step subtasks.
------------------------------------------------------------
1) Clear the area for your new garden.
2) Remove all weeds and debris from the site.
3) Prepare the soil by tilling or mixing in compost.
4) Decide on the layout of your garden beds.
5) Mark out the boundaries of each bed with stakes and string.
6) Dig trenches for raised beds if desired.
7) Install irrigation systems according to your garden's needs.
8) Plant any desired trees or shrubs as per your design.
9) Start planting flowers and vegetables in prepared soil.
10) Mulch around plants to retain moisture and suppress weeds.
