Task: Our Organic Farming Practices - No Pesticides!
Please break it into step-by-step subtasks.
------------------------------------------------------------
1)  Identify organic farming practices that do not use pesticides.
2)  Research and select natural pest control methods.
3)  Integrate crop rotation to prevent pest buildup.
4)  Use beneficial insects to naturally control pests.
5)  Apply compost and organic fertilizers for soil health.
6)  Monitor crops regularly for signs of pest infestation.
7)  Implement physical barriers like nets or screens.
8)  Educate farm workers about organic farming principles.
9)  Maintain clean harvesting and storage areas.
10)  Document all organic farming practices for certification.
