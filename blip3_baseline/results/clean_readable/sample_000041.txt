Task: New Holland LX865 Skid Steer: How to Flush Water-Contaminated Hydraulic Oil & Prevent Future Issues.
Please break it into step-by-step subtasks.
------------------------------------------------------------
1) Open the hydraulic fluid reservoir cap and drain the contaminated oil into a suitable container.
2) Replace the drained oil with clean, fresh hydraulic fluid of the same type.
3) Use a high-pressure water hose to flush out any remaining contaminants from the system.
4) Bleed the air from the hydraulic lines to ensure proper operation.
5) Re-fill the reservoir with the same amount of new hydraulic fluid as was drained.
6) Perform a thorough inspection of all hoses, seals, and fittings for signs of wear or damage.
7) Follow manufacturer's guidelines for regular maintenance schedules to prevent future contamination issues.
