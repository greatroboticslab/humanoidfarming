Task: Collecting Soil Samples | From the Ground Up
Please break it into step-by-step subtasks.
------------------------------------------------------------
1)  Choose an appropriate location based on the task's requirements.
2)  Wear protective gear including gloves and a mask.
3)  Use a trowel to dig a small hole about 6 inches deep.
4)  Carefully remove soil from the hole using a scoop or shovel.
5)  Place collected soil in a clean container for analysis.
6)  Mark the sample location with a flag or marker.
7)  Repeat process at multiple locations if necessary.
8)  Ensure all tools are cleaned before leaving the site.
