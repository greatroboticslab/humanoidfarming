When to buy or rent heavy equipment?
1)  Assess your needs and usage frequency.
2)  Consider the expected lifespan of the equipment.
3)  Evaluate the cost implications over time.
4)  Check the availability of financing options.
5)  Compare the long-term maintenance costs.
6)  Factor in potential resale value at the end of the lease or purchase term.
7)  Review the terms and conditions of rental agreements.
8)  Consult with industry experts for advice.
