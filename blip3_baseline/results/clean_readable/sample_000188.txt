Task: Q&A for Managing manure beyond the farmyard: Manure management for less-typical species and settings
Please break it into step-by-step subtasks.
------------------------------------------------------------
1) Identify the specific species requiring manure management.
2) Assess the environmental conditions suitable for manure use.
3) Determine the quantity of manure needed for the species.
4) Plan storage methods to prevent contamination and odors.
5) Decide on composting or direct application techniques.
6) Schedule regular monitoring for nutrient levels and soil health.
7) Educate local farmers about best practices for manure management.
