Task: Grain Farming Process
Please break it into step-by-step subtasks.
------------------------------------------------------------
Prepare land for cultivation.
Select appropriate seeds based on climate and soil conditions.
Plant seeds in prepared fields according to planting guidelines.
Water the newly planted seeds regularly.
Monitor plants for signs of pests or diseases.
Apply fertilizers as needed to promote healthy growth.
Harvest crops when they reach maturity.
Dispose of harvested grains properly.
Store excess grains in a dry, cool place.
