Task: 10. IRRI Agronomy Challenge: Are Insects a Problem? (2 & 6 February 2012)
Please break it into step-by-step subtasks.
------------------------------------------------------------
1) Review historical data on insect populations in rice fields.
2) Analyze current pest management practices for effectiveness.
3) Examine the impact of climate change on insect behavior.
4) Assess the economic importance of insects to rice crops.
5) Identify key insect species causing damage to rice plants.
6) Evaluate the use of biological control methods against pests.
7) Determine the resistance levels of rice varieties to insect attacks.
8) Investigate the role of natural predators in controlling insect populations.
9) Compare traditional farming techniques with modern agricultural practices.
10) Propose strategies to mitigate insect-related problems in rice cultivation.
