Task: Transforming Chicken Manure into Fertilizer: The 5-Step Magic
Please break it into step-by-step subtasks.
------------------------------------------------------------
1)  Collect chicken manure from the coop.
2)  Spread the manure evenly over a large area outdoors.
3)  Cover the spread manure with a thin layer of straw or hay.
4)  Allow the mixture to sit for at least two weeks in the sun and air.
5)  Mix the now-decomposed material thoroughly with soil before application.
