Task: Students experience agronomy through crop scouting competition
Please break it into step-by-step subtasks.
------------------------------------------------------------
1) Organize teams of students to participate in the crop scouting competition.
2) Provide each team with a plot of land for crop scouting activities.
3) Equip teams with necessary tools and resources for crop scouting.
4) Train students on proper techniques for crop scouting and data collection.
5) Set up a schedule for regular meetings to discuss findings and strategies.
6) Encourage teams to document their observations and share insights with peers.
7) Establish criteria for judging the competition based on accuracy and efficiency.
8) Announce winners at the end of the competition to recognize outstanding performance.
9) Plan follow-up sessions to analyze results and improve agronomy practices.
