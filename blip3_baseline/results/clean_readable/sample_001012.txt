Task: Organic No Till Farming For Home Gardeners and Commercial Farmers
Please break it into step-by-step subtasks.
------------------------------------------------------------
1)  Identify suitable crops for organic no-till farming.
2)  Choose seeds or seedlings that thrive in no-till conditions.
3)  Prepare the soil by adding compost and organic matter annually.
4)  Plant crops directly into the existing soil layer without tilling.
5)  Mulch around plants to retain moisture and suppress weeds.
6)  Rotate crops annually to prevent soil depletion and pest buildup.
7)  Monitor for pests and diseases using natural methods like beneficial insects.
8)  Water plants deeply but infrequently to encourage deep root growth.
9)  Harvest crops when they reach maturity to promote healthy plant growth.
10)  Maintain a diverse garden ecosystem to support beneficial organisms.
