Task: 07 - Rafter Support
Please break it into step-by-step subtasks.
------------------------------------------------------------
1) Measure the roof span to determine rafter length.
2) Calculate the rafter pitch based on roof slope.
3) Determine the rafter spacing for structural integrity.
4) Select appropriate rafters ensuring they fit within measurements.
5) Mark locations for rafter supports using chalk lines.
6) Install support brackets at marked points.
7) Secure rafters to supports with nails or screws.
8) Verify all rafters are level and plumb before proceeding.
