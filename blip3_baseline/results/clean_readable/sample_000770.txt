Task: Ras Modi Plants Corn: Embracing Sustainable Farming This Corn Season. #CornPlanting #gardeningtips
Please break it into step-by-step subtasks.
------------------------------------------------------------
1)  Choose a sustainable corn variety suitable for local climate conditions.
2)  Prepare the soil by removing weeds and adding compost or organic matter.
3)  Plant corn seeds at recommended depth and spacing according to package instructions.
4)  Water the newly planted corn regularly until seedlings emerge.
5)  Monitor for pests and diseases, using natural methods if possible.
6)  Apply mulch around corn plants to retain moisture and suppress weeds.
7)  Provide support for corn stalks as they grow taller.
8)  Harvest corn when kernels are plump and show milky juice when punctured.
9)  Rotate crops annually to prevent soil depletion and pest buildup.
10)  Compost plant residues and kitchen scraps to enrich the soil naturally.
