Task: Transplanting Tomatoes & Peppers and a Look at the Growing Area
Please break it into step-by-step subtasks.
------------------------------------------------------------
1) Assess soil conditions for both tomatoes and peppers to ensure they thrive.
2) Choose healthy tomato and pepper seedlings from a nursery or garden center.
3) Prepare the growing area by tilling the soil deeply and removing weeds.
4) Mix compost into the soil to improve fertility and drainage.
5) Plant tomato and pepper seedlings at the appropriate depth, ensuring good spacing.
6) Water the newly planted seedlings thoroughly to settle them in.
7) Apply mulch around plants to retain moisture and suppress weeds.
8) Monitor for pests and diseases regularly, taking action as needed.
9) Provide support structures like stakes or cages for taller plants like tomatoes.
10) Fertilize plants according to their needs, following package instructions carefully.
11) Keep an eye on watering needs, adjusting based on weather and plant growth.
12) Harvest tomatoes and peppers when ripe, enjoying fresh produce from your garden.
