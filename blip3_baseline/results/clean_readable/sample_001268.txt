Task: Buried sprinkler irrigation system display
Please break it into step-by-step subtasks.
------------------------------------------------------------
1) Identify components of buried sprinkler irrigation system.
2) Locate water supply line and main valve.
3) Check for any visible damage or leaks on the surface.
4) Turn off power to electrical components if applicable.
5) Remove cover plate over the irrigation system.
6) Inspect tubing and connections for integrity.
7) Test water flow through the system using a pressure gauge.
8) Adjust settings on control valves as necessary.
9) Reinstall cover plate securely.
10) Turn on water supply and monitor system operation.
