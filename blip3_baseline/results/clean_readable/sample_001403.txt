Task: Potatoes - Grading and Packing
Please break it into step-by-step subtasks.
------------------------------------------------------------
1)  Inspect each potato for size, shape, and overall condition.
2)  Sort potatoes into grades based on size and quality standards.
3)  Clean potatoes thoroughly under running water to remove dirt.
4)  Pack potatoes carefully in boxes or crates to prevent damage.
5)  Label each box clearly with grade, quantity, and date of packing.
6)  Ensure proper temperature control during transportation to maintain quality.
