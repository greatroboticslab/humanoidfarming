Task: Small Farm Irrigation - Video 2 - Backflow Prevention
Please break it into step-by-step subtasks.
------------------------------------------------------------
1) Identify all irrigation valves and connectors on the farm.
2) Install backflow preventers at each identified valve.
3) Ensure proper installation according to manufacturer guidelines.
4) Test backflow preventers for functionality after installation.
5) Train farmers on the importance of regular maintenance.
6) Schedule quarterly inspections of backflow prevention devices.
7) Keep records of all maintenance activities and test results.
