Task: Sugarcane peeling machine
Please break it into step-by-step subtasks.
------------------------------------------------------------
1)  Assess the size and capacity needed for the sugarcane peeling machine.
2)  Choose appropriate materials for construction of the machine.
3)  Design the mechanical components including rollers and blades.
4)  Assemble the main frame and support structure of the machine.
5)  Install the conveyor system to transport sugarcane through the machine.
6)  Integrate the peeling mechanism with rotating blades or discs.
7)  Connect the electrical components for operation and control.
8)  Test the machine's functionality with test sugarcane samples.
9)  Adjust settings for optimal peeling efficiency and quality.
10)  Train operators on safe use and maintenance procedures.
