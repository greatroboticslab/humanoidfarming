Task: Improving soil #health and #crop #yield through #crop rotation
Please break it into step-by-step subtasks.
------------------------------------------------------------
1)  Identify suitable crops for rotation based on local climate and soil conditions.
2)  Plan a crop rotation schedule that includes at least three different types of crops.
3)  Start with a leguminous crop to improve soil nitrogen levels.
4)  Follow legumes with a cereal grain to utilize residual nutrients.
5)  Rotate cereals with root vegetables to enhance soil structure.
6)  Introduce cover crops between main crop cycles for added benefits.
7)  Monitor soil health regularly using tests like pH and nutrient analysis.
8)  Adjust crop rotation plans annually based on soil test results and crop performance.
9)  Educate farmers about the importance of crop rotation and its benefits.
10)  Encourage community sharing of successful crop rotation practices and techniques.
