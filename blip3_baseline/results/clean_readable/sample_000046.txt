Task: Best4Soil: Crop rotation  Practical Information ET
Please break it into step-by-step subtasks.
------------------------------------------------------------
1) Identify suitable crops for the soil type.
2) Plan a sequence of crops to rotate annually.
3) Research water needs for each crop in rotation.
4) Calculate evapotranspiration rates for planning irrigation.
5) Adjust crop rotation based on local climate conditions.
6) Monitor soil health and adjust rotation as necessary.
