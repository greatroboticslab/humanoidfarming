Task: PREVENT FLAT TIRES IN TRACTORS TRAILERS & TRUCKS! CRAZY!
Please break it into step-by-step subtasks.
------------------------------------------------------------
1) Regularly inspect tires for wear, cuts, or bulges.
2) Check tire pressure according to manufacturer guidelines weekly.
3) Rotate tires every 5000 miles to ensure even wear.
4) Avoid driving on damaged roads or potholes.
5) Use proper load distribution to avoid uneven tire wear.
6) Replace worn-out tires before they become dangerous.
7) Keep a spare tire and tools in your vehicle at all times.
8) Consider using tire chains in snowy or icy conditions.
9) Monitor tire temperature; hot spots may indicate issues.
10) Have tires professionally inspected annually for safety.
