Task: Be part of CSA! - Community Supported Agriculture
Please break it into step-by-step subtasks.
------------------------------------------------------------
1) Identify local CSA programs in your area.
2) Research their availability, benefits, and costs.
3) Contact chosen CSA to inquire about membership options.
4) Review contract terms and confirm details about produce delivery.
5) Decide on the number of shares you wish to purchase.
6) Pay for your CSA share(s) according to the program's instructions.
7) Receive regular updates about upcoming harvests and available produce.
8) Pick up or arrange for delivery of your weekly or bi-weekly share.
9) Enjoy fresh, locally grown produce from your CSA farm.
