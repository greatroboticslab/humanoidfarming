Task: Winter Livestock Feeding
Please break it into step-by-step subtasks.
------------------------------------------------------------
1) Assess current livestock health and nutritional needs.
2) Adjust feeding schedules to accommodate colder weather.
3) Increase protein content in feed for better winter nutrition.
4) Provide adequate water sources to prevent freezing.
5) Clean and maintain feeding areas to avoid contamination.
6) Monitor livestock closely for signs of illness or distress.
7) Supplement feed with energy-rich foods like grains.
8) Ensure proper ventilation to prevent respiratory issues.
9) Plan for potential emergency supplies and veterinary care.
10) Prepare for possible power outages affecting heating systems.
