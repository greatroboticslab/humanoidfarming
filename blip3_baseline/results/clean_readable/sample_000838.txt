Task: URGENTE! Fungos Sumindo com Bicarbonato  Aplique Agora!
Please break it into step-by-step subtasks.
------------------------------------------------------------
1) Identify areas affected by fungi on skin or surfaces.
2) Mix one part baking soda with three parts water to create paste.
3) Apply the paste gently over the affected area in a circular motion.
4) Leave the paste on for about 10 minutes or as directed.
5) Rinse off thoroughly with warm water.
6) Dry the area completely before applying any additional treatments.
