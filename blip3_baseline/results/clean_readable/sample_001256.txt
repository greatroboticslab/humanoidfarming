Task: Tomato Farming | tomato | tomato farming techniques
Please break it into step-by-step subtasks.
------------------------------------------------------------
1) Identify suitable soil type for tomatoes.
2) Choose disease-resistant tomato varieties.
3) Prepare soil by adding compost and fertilizers.
4) Plant tomatoes at appropriate spacing for growth.
5) Water plants regularly to maintain soil moisture.
6) Apply mulch to retain soil moisture and suppress weeds.
7) Provide adequate sunlight exposure daily.
8) Monitor for pests and diseases, using natural remedies if possible.
9) Prune plants to improve air circulation and fruit quality.
10) Harvest tomatoes when they reach full color and size.
