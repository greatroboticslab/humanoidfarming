Task: Backyard Conservation
Please break it into step-by-step subtasks.
------------------------------------------------------------
1)  Identify local conservation organizations for guidance.
2)  Assess backyard space for wildlife-friendly features.
3)  Research native plants suitable for the area's climate.
4)  Plan placement of birdhouses or bat boxes.
5)  Choose composting methods to reduce waste.
6)  Install water features like birdbaths or ponds.
7)  Create a garden bed for pollinator-friendly flowers.
8)  Introduce native trees and shrubs for habitat.
9)  Set up a feeding station for birds if desired.
10)  Educate family on the importance of backyard conservation.
