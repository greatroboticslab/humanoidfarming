Task: The Art of Strawberry Cultivation: Tips for Luscious Berries
Please break it into step-by-step subtasks.
------------------------------------------------------------
1)  Choose suitable strawberry varieties based on climate and soil conditions.
2)  Prepare the planting site by tilling and adding organic matter.
3)  Plant strawberry runners in early spring or late summer.
4)  Water plants deeply but infrequently to encourage strong root growth.
5)  Mulch around plants to retain moisture and suppress weeds.
6)  Apply balanced fertilizer every two weeks during growing season.
7)  Monitor for pests and diseases regularly, using organic controls if necessary.
8)  Harvest strawberries when they are fully ripe and show their characteristic color.
9)  Encourage new runners to spread and increase your strawberry patch size.
10)  Overwinter plants by covering them with straw or leaves in colder climates.
