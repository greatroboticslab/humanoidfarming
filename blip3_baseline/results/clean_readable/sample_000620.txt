Task: How One Country Sought to Combat the Harm of Burning Sugar Cane
Please break it into step-by-step subtasks.
------------------------------------------------------------
1) Identified high levels of air pollution from burning sugar cane as a significant environmental issue.
2) Developed policies to regulate and reduce the practice of burning sugar cane fields.
3) Implemented stricter emission standards for sugar mills to decrease harmful emissions.
4) Encouraged farmers to adopt alternative methods like mechanical harvesting to minimize smoke.
5) Provided financial incentives to farmers who transitioned to sustainable farming practices.
6) Increased public awareness campaigns about the health impacts of sugar cane burning.
7) Collaborated with international organizations to share best practices in managing agricultural waste.
