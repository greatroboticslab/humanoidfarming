Task: Refilling Grain Drill with Pasture Mix/Oats/Red Clover to Reseed Old Pasture
Please break it into step-by-step subtasks.
------------------------------------------------------------
1) Assess soil conditions for optimal pasture mix growth.
2) Purchase appropriate quantities of pasture mix, oats, and red clover seeds.
3) Prepare drill equipment for even distribution of seeds.
4) Clear old pasture area of debris and weeds.
5) Broadcast seeds evenly across cleared field using the drill.
6) Adjust drill settings according to seed type and desired depth.
7) Water newly planted seeds thoroughly after drilling.
8) Apply mulch or cover crop to protect seeds from drying out.
9) Monitor growth regularly and remove any weeds that emerge.
10) Harvest new pasture once established and ready for grazing.
