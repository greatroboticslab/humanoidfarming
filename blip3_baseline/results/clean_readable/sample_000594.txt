Task: HARVESTING Okra in MY GARDEN || How to Harvest Okra  @farmguards
Please break it into step-by-step subtasks.
------------------------------------------------------------
1) Identify mature okra pods that are about 4-6 inches long.
2) Use sharp scissors or pruning shears to cut the pods near the stem.
3) Pick okra regularly to encourage new growth and prevent over-maturity.
4) Check plants daily for ripe pods as they grow quickly.
5) Remove any damaged or diseased okra pods to keep plants healthy.
6) Store harvested okra in a cool place or prepare immediately for cooking.
