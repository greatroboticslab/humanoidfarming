Task: Checking in on the Chickens in their New Coop
Please break it into step-by-step subtasks.
------------------------------------------------------------
1) Observe the new coop for structural integrity and safety features.
2) Check that all doors and windows are securely closed and locked.
3) Inspect the ventilation system to ensure proper airflow.
4) Verify water supply is functional and accessible to the chickens.
5) Examine feeders and waterers for cleanliness and adequate placement.
6) Assess nesting boxes for comfort and hygiene conditions.
7) Listen for any unusual sounds or signs of distress among the chickens.
8) Count the number of chickens present to confirm they are all accounted for.
9) Provide immediate attention if any chickens appear sick or injured.
10) Document observations for future reference and potential improvements.
