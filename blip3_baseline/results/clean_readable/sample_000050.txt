Task: How Much Does It Cost To Run A Farm? | FoodUnfolded Explains
Please break it into step-by-step subtasks.
------------------------------------------------------------
1) Assess land cost based on size and location.
2) Calculate labor expenses for farming staff.
3) Determine seed and fertilizer costs for crops.
4) Factor in machinery and equipment maintenance fees.
5) Include utilities like water and electricity bills.
6) Estimate marketing and transportation costs.
7) Add insurance premiums for farm protection.
8) Consider potential losses due to weather or pests.
9) Sum up all costs to find total annual running expense.
