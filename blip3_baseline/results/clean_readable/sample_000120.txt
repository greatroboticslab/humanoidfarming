Task: The Art of Garlic Farming: A Look at the Process from Soil to Packaging| Farming Documentary
Please break it into step-by-step subtasks.
------------------------------------------------------------
1)  Identify suitable garlic varieties for the local climate.
2)  Select high-quality seeds or cloves for planting.
3)  Prepare the soil by testing its pH level and adding necessary nutrients.
4)  Plant garlic cloves in rows spaced according to recommended guidelines.
5)  Water the planted garlic regularly to ensure proper growth.
6)  Monitor for pests and diseases, applying organic treatments if needed.
7)  Harvest garlic when the leaves turn yellow and begin to dry.
8)  Cure harvested garlic by hanging it in a well-ventilated area.
9)  Clean and sort cured garlic to remove damaged cloves.
10)  Package garlic carefully to protect it during transportation.
11)  Label packaging with information about the garlic's origin and care instructions.
12)  Distribute packaged garlic to markets or directly to consumers.
