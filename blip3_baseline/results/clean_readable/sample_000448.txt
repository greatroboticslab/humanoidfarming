Task: Grow Pear Trees from Cuttings  Best Methods & Tips! #shorts #satisfying #farming
Please break it into step-by-step subtasks.
------------------------------------------------------------
1)  Choose healthy, mature pear tree cuttings for propagation.
2)  Clean the cuttings to remove dirt and debris carefully.
3)  Plant the cuttings in well-draining soil at an angle of about 45 degrees.
4)  Water the cuttings thoroughly after planting.
5)  Cover the area with a clear plastic bag to create a mini greenhouse.
6)  Place the covered area in a warm, sunny location.
7)  Keep the soil moist but not waterlogged.
8)  After several weeks, check for roots forming on the cuttings.
9)  Once roots are visible, transplant the cuttings into larger pots or directly into the garden.
10)  Provide regular care including watering, pruning, and fertilizing.
