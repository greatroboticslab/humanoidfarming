Task: Agricultural Machinery
Please break it into step-by-step subtasks.
------------------------------------------------------------
Organize agricultural machinery inventory.
Identify maintenance needs for each piece of machinery.
Schedule regular maintenance checks for all machinery.
Train operators on proper machinery usage and safety protocols.
Assess the need for new or upgraded machinery based on current operations.
Determine the most cost-effective financing options for purchasing new machinery.
Develop a plan for distributing new machinery among available fields.
Coordinate with local suppliers to ensure timely delivery of new machinery.
Implement a system for tracking machinery usage and performance metrics.
Evaluate the effectiveness of the machinery management system periodically.
