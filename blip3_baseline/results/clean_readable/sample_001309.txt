Task: Life on a Small Farm: Growing Food Caring for Animals and Hard Work
Please break it into step-by-step subtasks.
------------------------------------------------------------
1) Assess farm size and resources for sustainable living.
2) Plan crop rotation to maintain soil health.
3) Select appropriate livestock based on available space.
4) Design shelter that meets animal needs and safety standards.
5) Prepare garden beds with compost and organic matter.
6) Plant seeds in rows or raised beds according to season.
7) Water plants regularly to ensure healthy growth.
8) Monitor crops for signs of pests or disease daily.
9) Feed animals according to their nutritional needs daily.
10) Clean barns and pens weekly to prevent illness.
11) Harvest vegetables and fruits at peak ripeness.
12) Sell produce at local markets or through community-supported agriculture.
