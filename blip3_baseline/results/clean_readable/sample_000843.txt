Task: Agricultural Drone
Please break it into step-by-step subtasks.
------------------------------------------------------------
1)  Identify specific agricultural needs such as crop monitoring, pest control, or soil analysis.
2)  Choose appropriate drone models based on payload capacity and flight duration.
3)  Install necessary sensors and cameras compatible with the chosen drone model.
4)  Program the drone to fly pre-defined routes for efficient data collection.
5)  Calibrate sensors and cameras to ensure accurate readings and images.
6)  Test the drone in controlled environments before full-scale operations.
7)  Train operators on safe and effective use of the agricultural drone system.
8)  Obtain necessary permits and insurance coverage for legal operation of the drone.
9)  Develop a maintenance schedule to keep the drone operational and reliable.
10)  Integrate drone data into existing farm management systems for better decision-making.
