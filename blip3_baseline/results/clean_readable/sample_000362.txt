Task: The Blockies Guide - Managing weeds on small farms and acres
Please break it into step-by-step subtasks.
------------------------------------------------------------
1)  Identify common weed species in your farm to understand what you're dealing with.
2)  Choose appropriate herbicides based on the identified weed types.
3)  Apply herbicides according to the manufacturer's instructions for safety.
4)  Use mulch to suppress weed growth around crops.
5)  Regularly inspect fields for new weed infestations.
6)  Rotate crops to disrupt weed life cycles.
7)  Consider using cover crops to outcompete weeds.
8)  Hand-pull weeds manually when they're young and small.
9)  Implement integrated pest management strategies including mechanical controls.
10)  Maintain good soil health through proper fertilization and irrigation.
