Task: KNOW YOUR RASPBERRIES - How to get a BUMPER crop from your homegrown raspberries
Please break it into step-by-step subtasks.
------------------------------------------------------------
1) Choose disease-resistant raspberry varieties suitable for your climate.
2) Plant raspberry canes in well-drained soil enriched with compost.
3) Provide at least six hours of direct sunlight daily for optimal growth.
4) Water plants deeply but infrequently to encourage strong root development.
5) Mulch around plants to retain moisture and suppress weeds.
6) Prune canes annually in late winter or early spring to promote new growth.
7) Apply a balanced fertilizer every two months during growing season.
8) Harvest ripe berries regularly to encourage continuous fruit production.
9) Encourage pollination by planting nearby flowers or using a bee-friendly environment.
10) Monitor for pests and diseases, treating promptly if necessary.
