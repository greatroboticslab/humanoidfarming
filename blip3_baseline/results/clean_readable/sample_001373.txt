Task: How To Start A Farm In Five Minutes
Please break it into step-by-step subtasks.
------------------------------------------------------------
1)  Choose a suitable location for your farm that receives at least six hours of sunlight daily.
2)  Purchase seeds or young plants according to the crops you wish to grow.
3)  Prepare the soil by removing weeds and adding compost or fertilizer as needed.
4)  Plant seeds or place seedlings in the prepared soil following recommended spacing guidelines.
5)  Water the newly planted area thoroughly and maintain consistent moisture levels.
6)  Apply appropriate pesticides or natural pest control methods to protect your crops.
7)  Monitor plant growth regularly and adjust care as necessary based on weather conditions and crop needs.
