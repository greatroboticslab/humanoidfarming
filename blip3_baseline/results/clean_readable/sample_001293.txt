Task: How to plant under 3 minutes |Gardening wit Konzept Garden
Please break it into step-by-step subtasks.
------------------------------------------------------------
1) Choose a suitable spot with plenty of sunlight for your plants.
2) Prepare the soil by removing weeds and adding compost.
3) Dig holes according to the recommended depth and spacing for each plant.
4) Place the plant in the hole and backfill with soil, gently pressing down.
5) Water thoroughly to settle the soil around the roots.
6) Apply mulch to retain moisture and suppress weeds.
