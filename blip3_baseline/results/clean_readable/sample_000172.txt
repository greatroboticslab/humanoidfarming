Task: Growing Food in Urban Small Spaces - Urban Gardening
Please break it into step-by-step subtasks.
------------------------------------------------------------
1)  Assess available space for gardening.
2)  Choose appropriate plants suitable for small spaces.
3)  Decide on container type or raised beds.
4)  Select soil that suits plant needs.
5)  Determine lighting conditions for optimal growth.
6)  Plan watering schedule based on plant requirements.
7)  Install necessary tools and equipment.
8)  Start planting seeds or seedlings.
9)  Monitor plants regularly for health issues.
10)  Harvest produce as it becomes ready.
