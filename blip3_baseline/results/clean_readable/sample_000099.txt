Task: Replacing the Top of a Rain Bird Valve
Please break it into step-by-step subtasks.
------------------------------------------------------------
1)  Turn off the water supply to the irrigation system.
2)  Locate the top of the Rain Bird valve.
3)  Unscrew the existing cap from the top of the valve.
4)  Remove the old cap carefully without damaging the valve body.
5)  Clean any debris or residue from the valve opening.
6)  Install a new cap over the valve opening.
7)  Replace the screw that holds the cap in place.
8)  Reconnect the water supply to the irrigation system.
9)  Test the valve to ensure proper operation.
