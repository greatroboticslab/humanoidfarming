Task: Crop Rotation   Benefits &Techniques
Please break it into step-by-step subtasks.
------------------------------------------------------------
1) Identify soil nutrient deficiencies through testing.
2) Research suitable crops for the identified soil needs.
3) Plan crop rotation schedules based on research findings.
4) Adjust planting dates to ensure staggered growth cycles.
5) Prepare soil for new crops after harvest of previous ones.
6) Use cover crops between main crop rotations for added benefits.
7) Rotate crops annually or biannually as per recommended practices.
8) Monitor crop health and adjust rotation plans accordingly.
9) Educate farmers about the importance of crop rotation techniques.
10) Implement integrated pest management strategies alongside crop rotation.
