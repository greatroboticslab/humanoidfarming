Task: African Village Life | Morning Routine In The Farm
Please break it into step-by-step subtasks.
------------------------------------------------------------
1) Farmers wake up early to tend to livestock before sunrise.
2) They prepare breakfast for the family using locally sourced ingredients.
3) Children help gather firewood and collect water from wells.
4) Men start plowing fields with oxen to prepare for planting season.
5) Women begin processing crops like millet and sorghum for storage.
6) Cattle are led to grazing areas after feeding them in the morning.
7) Chickens are gathered and fed, then allowed to roam freely.
8) Afternoon chores include checking on crops and repairing tools.
