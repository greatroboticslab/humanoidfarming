Task: My Daily Life as a Farmer A Hardworking Day
Please break it into step-by-step subtasks.
------------------------------------------------------------
1) Wake up early to tend to livestock and check weather forecasts.
2) Prepare fields for planting or harvesting crops.
3) Water crops using irrigation systems or manual watering methods.
4) Weed and fertilize plants to ensure healthy growth.
5) Harvest ripe produce carefully to avoid damage.
6) Sort and pack harvested items for market or storage.
7) Rest briefly before preparing meals and tending to personal hygiene.
8) Plan next day's tasks based on current conditions and weather predictions.
