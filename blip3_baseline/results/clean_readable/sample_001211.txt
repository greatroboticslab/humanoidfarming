Task: Ghazas maize planting technique.#MaizePlantingTips #ExpertGardening #GrowBigCorn
Please break it into step-by-step subtasks.
------------------------------------------------------------
1) Identify suitable soil for optimal growth.
2) Choose high-quality maize seeds for better yield.
3) Prepare the land by removing weeds and rocks.
4) Plant seeds at proper depth and spacing for air circulation.
5) Water regularly to keep soil moist but not waterlogged.
6) Apply balanced fertilizer to support plant development.
7) Monitor for pests and diseases, taking action promptly.
8) Support plants with stakes or trellises as needed.
9) Harvest maize when ears are fully filled and dry.
10) Store harvested corn properly to prevent spoilage.
