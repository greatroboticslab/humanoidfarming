Task: Farming Basics
Please break it into step-by-step subtasks.
------------------------------------------------------------
1)  Choose a suitable plot of land for farming.
2)  Prepare the soil by removing weeds and rocks.
3)  Test the soil to determine nutrient levels.
4)  Decide on crops or livestock based on climate and space.
5)  Plant seeds at recommended intervals and depths.
6)  Water plants regularly according to their needs.
7)  Monitor for pests and diseases, using natural remedies if possible.
8)  Harvest crops when they reach maturity.
9)  Rotate crops to maintain soil health and prevent pest buildup.
10)  Maintain livestock by providing food, water, and shelter.
