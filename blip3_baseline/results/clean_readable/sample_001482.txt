Task: Cataloging and Evaluating Dairy Manure Treatment Technologies
Please break it into step-by-step subtasks.
------------------------------------------------------------
1)  Identify different dairy manure treatment technologies available.
2)  Research each technology's principles of operation.
3)  Gather data on energy consumption for each method.
4)  Collect information on environmental impact assessments.
5)  Determine the cost implications of implementing each technology.
6)  Assess the scalability and adaptability of each technology.
7)  Evaluate the technological feasibility of each method.
8)  Analyze the maintenance requirements for each treatment system.
9)  Consider the regulatory compliance status for each technology.
10)  Compile a comparative analysis table of all evaluated technologies.
