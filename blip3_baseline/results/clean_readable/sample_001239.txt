Task: Best4Soil: Soil health  Practical Information
Please break it into step-by-step subtasks.
------------------------------------------------------------
1) Identify key indicators of soil health such as organic matter content, pH level, and microbial activity.
2) Determine soil type and its specific needs for optimal health.
3) Apply appropriate amendments like compost or lime based on soil test results.
4) Implement crop rotation and cover cropping to enhance soil structure and fertility.
5) Monitor soil moisture levels and adjust irrigation practices accordingly.
6) Use natural pest control methods to avoid chemical soil degradation.
7) Regularly assess soil health through periodic testing and analysis.
8) Educate farmers and community members about soil conservation practices.
