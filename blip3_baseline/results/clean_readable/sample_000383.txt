Task: Ancient China to Modern Agriculture: The Evolution of Plows
Please break it into step-by-step subtasks.
------------------------------------------------------------
1) Ancient Chinese plows were typically ox-drawn, using simple wooden designs.
2) Iron plows began to appear in the Han Dynasty, improving soil cultivation efficiency.
3) By Tang Dynasty, iron plows became more sophisticated, incorporating adjustable parts.
4) Song Dynasty saw advancements like the curved-plow, enhancing draft animal control.
5) Ming Dynasty introduced the two-handled plow, increasing labor efficiency.
6) Qing Dynasty continued improvements, integrating water management techniques.
7) Western-style steel plows were introduced during the late Qing and early Republican periods.
8) Today, modern agricultural implements have largely replaced traditional Chinese plows.
