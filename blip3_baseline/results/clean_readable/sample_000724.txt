Task: Inoculating Grain Spawn from Midwest Grow Kits - Bulk Growing Series (pt. 1)
Please break it into step-by-step subtasks.
------------------------------------------------------------
1)  Obtain inoculated grain spawn from Midwest grow kits.
2)  Prepare clean containers for inoculation.
3)  Sterilize containers using autoclave or boiling water.
4)  Allow containers to cool before opening.
5)  Open containers carefully to avoid contamination.
6)  Carefully place spawn into prepared containers.
7)  Ensure even distribution of spawn throughout the container.
8)  Seal containers tightly after adding spawn.
9)  Label each container with date and contents.
10)  Place sealed containers in a suitable growing environment.
