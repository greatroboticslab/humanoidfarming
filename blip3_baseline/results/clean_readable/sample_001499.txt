Task: Start a FARM in your BACKYARD | Small Scale Regenerative Farming with Nature's Always Right
Please break it into step-by-step subtasks.
------------------------------------------------------------
1)  Research small-scale regenerative farming methods suitable for backyard environments.
2)  Choose a location that receives adequate sunlight and has access to water sources.
3)  Design a layout that maximizes space efficiency and promotes biodiversity.
4)  Select native plants and crops that require minimal intervention and support local ecosystems.
5)  Incorporate composting systems to recycle organic waste and enrich soil naturally.
6)  Introduce beneficial insects and microorganisms to control pests and improve soil health.
7)  Install rainwater harvesting systems to conserve water and reduce runoff.
8)  Practice crop rotation and intercropping to enhance soil fertility and prevent pest buildup.
9)  Maintain a natural mulch layer to retain moisture and suppress weeds.
10)  Monitor plant growth and adjust management practices based on observations and feedback from nature.
