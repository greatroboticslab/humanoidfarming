Task: Drip irrigation system slowly  plastic bottle self gravity watering for the garden plants pots
Please break it into step-by-step subtasks.
------------------------------------------------------------
1) Cut off the bottom of a plastic bottle to create an opening large enough for plant roots.
2) Punch small holes around the opening for water flow control.
3) Fill the bottle with water and place it upside down in each pot.
4) Ensure the soil is moist before placing the bottle to avoid overwatering.
5) The weight of the filled bottle will slowly release water through the holes as needed.
6) Monitor plants regularly to adjust placement or frequency if necessary.
