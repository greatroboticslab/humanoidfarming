Task: NO POTTING UP METHOD FOR TOMATO SEEDLINGS  - One Cup Method for Huge Tomatoes
Please break it into step-by-step subtasks.
------------------------------------------------------------
1) Start with a clean one-cup container, preferably made of plastic or glass.
2) Fill the cup about halfway with moistened potting soil.
3) Plant one tomato seedling in the center of the soil.
4) Cover the roots gently with more soil.
5) Water the soil lightly to settle around the seedling.
6) Place the cup in a warm, sunny spot.
7) Keep the soil slightly moist but not waterlogged.
8) After about two weeks, transplant the seedling into a larger pot or garden bed.
