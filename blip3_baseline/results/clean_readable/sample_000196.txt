Task: Sowing Garlic 28th September 2024
Please break it into step-by-step subtasks.
------------------------------------------------------------
1) Check local weather forecast for 28th September 2024 to ensure suitable conditions for sowing garlic.
2) Prepare planting beds by removing weeds and adding compost or well-rotted manure.
3) Choose garlic cloves that are firm and have no signs of disease or damage.
4) Plant garlic cloves about 6 inches apart in rows spaced 18 inches apart.
5) Cover planted cloves with a thin layer of soil or mulch.
6) Water the planted garlic thoroughly to settle the soil around the cloves.
7) Monitor the garlic plants for pests and diseases, applying organic treatments if necessary.
8) Keep the planting area weed-free to prevent competition for nutrients.
9) Maintain consistent moisture levels in the soil throughout the growing season.
10) Harvest garlic when the tops begin to yellow and fall over naturally, usually late summer or early autumn.
