Task: Rain Water Collection System
Please break it into step-by-step subtasks.
------------------------------------------------------------
1)  Assess the terrain and location for optimal water collection.
2)  Design the system to maximize rainwater capture.
3)  Select appropriate materials for the collection and storage components.
4)  Install gutters and downspouts to direct rainwater to the system.
5)  Construct or install rain barrels or cisterns for water storage.
6)  Connect the gutters to the rain barrels or cisterns using pipes.
7)  Ensure proper drainage around the installation to prevent overflow.
8)  Add overflow protection devices to prevent water from spilling over.
9)  Inspect and clean the system regularly to maintain its efficiency.
10)  Educate users on how to use collected rainwater responsibly.
