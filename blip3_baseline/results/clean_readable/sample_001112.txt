Task: How to Grow Great Potatoes at Your Place
Please break it into step-by-step subtasks.
------------------------------------------------------------
1)  Choose a sunny spot for planting potatoes.
2)  Purchase certified seed potatoes from a garden center.
3)  Cut seed potatoes into pieces, ensuring each has at least one eye.
4)  Plant seed potato pieces about 8 inches deep in rows spaced 2 feet apart.
5)  Cover planted seed potatoes with soil or compost.
6)  Water regularly, keeping soil consistently moist but not waterlogged.
7)  Thin plants when they reach 6 inches tall, leaving every 12 inches between plants.
8)  Mulch around plants to retain moisture and suppress weeds.
9)  Monitor for pests and diseases, treating as needed.
10)  Harvest mature potatoes when the tops begin to yellow and die back.
11)  Dig carefully to avoid bruising the tubers.
12)  Store harvested potatoes in a cool, dry place.
