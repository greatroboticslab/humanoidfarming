Task: Tractor and agricultural machinery maintenance program
Please break it into step-by-step subtasks.
------------------------------------------------------------
1) Identify all types of tractors and machinery used on farm.
2) Schedule regular maintenance check-ups for each piece.
3) Train staff on proper maintenance procedures and safety.
4) Create a comprehensive maintenance log for each machine.
5) Stock essential tools and spare parts for quick repairs.
6) Establish a routine inspection schedule for equipment daily.
7) Conduct monthly inspections to ensure machinery is in good condition.
8) Perform annual servicing and tune-ups for major machinery.
9) Keep records of all maintenance activities and repairs done.
10) Update maintenance schedules based on usage patterns and wear.
