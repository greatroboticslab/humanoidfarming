Task: Free Containers For Gardening
Please break it into step-by-step subtasks.
------------------------------------------------------------
1) Identify unused containers in your home or garden.
2) Clean and disinfect all containers thoroughly.
3) Choose suitable plants for container gardening.
4) Select appropriate soil and potting mix for each container.
5) Plant seeds or seedlings according to container size and plant needs.
6) Water plants regularly and provide adequate sunlight.
7) Monitor plants for pests and diseases, and take necessary actions.
8) Harvest produce as needed, encouraging more growth.
9) Rotate plants to ensure even growth and prevent nutrient depletion.
10) Prepare containers for winter storage if applicable.
