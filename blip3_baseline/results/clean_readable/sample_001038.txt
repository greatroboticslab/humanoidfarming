Task: Finishing Cattle in Hoop Barns
Please break it into step-by-step subtasks.
------------------------------------------------------------
1) Assess current cattle health and condition before starting.
2) Prepare all necessary equipment and supplies for the task.
3) Ensure safety measures are in place to protect both workers and animals.
4) Open the hoop barn gates to allow cattle to enter the designated area.
5) Mark off the area where cattle will be moved using temporary fencing.
6) Use gentle handling techniques to guide cattle towards the new area.
7) Monitor cattle closely during the transition to ensure they adapt smoothly.
8) Provide water and feed as needed to maintain their hydration and nutrition.
9) Once cattle are settled, close the temporary fencing and secure the gates.
10) Document the process and any observations for future reference or improvement.
