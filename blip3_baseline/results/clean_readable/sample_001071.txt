Task: Mealworm 101 || 5 Simple Steps to Starting a Mealworm Colony!
Please break it into step-by-step subtasks.
------------------------------------------------------------
1)  Obtain mealworms from a reliable source or purchase them online.
2)  Prepare a suitable container with bedding material like shredded newspaper or coconut coir.
3)  Add a layer of moistened substrate for the mealworms to live on.
4)  Provide a shallow dish filled with bran or oatmeal as a food source.
5)  Maintain the environment at a consistent temperature between 70-80 degrees Fahrenheit and ensure proper humidity.
