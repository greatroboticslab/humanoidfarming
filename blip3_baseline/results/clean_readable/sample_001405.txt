Task: Operator Daily Maintenance of Crawler Tractors
Please break it into step-by-step subtasks.
------------------------------------------------------------
1) Inspect all hydraulic lines for leaks or damage.
2) Check tire pressure and condition on crawler tractors.
3) Lubricate moving parts according to manufacturer guidelines.
4) Clean and inspect the engine air filter.
5) Ensure coolant levels are at recommended specifications.
6) Verify that all safety devices are functioning properly.
7) Tighten loose bolts and nuts on the tractor chassis.
8) Test the tractor's steering and braking systems.
9) Inspect the electrical system for any signs of wear.
10) Refuel the tractor if necessary, ensuring fuel quality.
11) Perform a thorough visual inspection of the entire machine.
12) Document maintenance activities in the operator's logbook.
