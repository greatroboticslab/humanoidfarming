Task: Struggling with dry conditions on your farm?  Discover low-cost irrigation techniques.
Please break it into step-by-step subtasks.
------------------------------------------------------------
1)  Assess current water usage to identify areas needing improvement.
2)  Research and select appropriate low-cost irrigation methods.
3)  Install drip irrigation systems for efficient water delivery.
4)  Use rainwater harvesting tanks to collect and store water.
5)  Implement mulching practices to reduce evaporation and retain moisture.
6)  Plant drought-resistant crops suitable for the local climate.
7)  Monitor soil moisture levels regularly to avoid overwatering.
8)  Educate farm workers about proper irrigation techniques and water conservation.
