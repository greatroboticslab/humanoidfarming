Task: South Dakota Rancher Shares how Rotational Grazing Helps His Regenerative Agriculture Ranch
Please break it into step-by-step subtasks.
------------------------------------------------------------
1)  South Dakota rancher introduces rotational grazing practices to his farm.
2)  He implements these practices to enhance soil health and water retention.

4)  This method allows grasses to recover and grow back stronger.
5)  As a result, his regenerative agriculture approach improves overall ecosystem balance.
6)  The rancher observes increased biodiversity and improved pasture quality.
7)  He shares his experiences to inspire other farmers adopting sustainable farming methods.
