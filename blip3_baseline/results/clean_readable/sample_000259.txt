Task: The NEW FASTEST Way to Level Up Hourglass in Sea of Thieves
Please break it into step-by-step subtasks.
------------------------------------------------------------
1) Gather a crew of at least three players for optimal teamwork.
2) Choose a ship that can navigate through shallow waters efficiently.
3) Collect hourglass shells from the ocean floor using your ship's grappling hook.
4) Place hourglass shells on the hourglass level in the map.
5) Use the hourglass shells to fill up the hourglass containers.
6) Once filled, use the hourglass to level up your ship's speed.
7) Repeat the process daily to maintain or increase your ship's speed.
