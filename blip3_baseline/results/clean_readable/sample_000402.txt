Task: Soil-Building Practices for Healthier Crops
Please break it into step-by-step subtasks.
------------------------------------------------------------
1)  Identify soil type and nutrient deficiencies.
2)  Conduct soil tests to determine pH levels.
3)  Apply organic matter like compost or manure.
4)  Incorporate cover crops to improve soil structure.
5)  Rotate crops annually to prevent pest buildup.
6)  Use crop rotation to maintain soil fertility.
7)  Implement mulching practices to retain moisture.
8)  Practice minimal tillage to preserve soil structure.
9)  Utilize green manures to add nutrients.
10)  Monitor soil moisture levels regularly.
