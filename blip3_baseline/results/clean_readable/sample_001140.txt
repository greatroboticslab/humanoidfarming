Task: Pumpkin Growing Basics | From the Ground Up
Please break it into step-by-step subtasks.
------------------------------------------------------------
1)  Choose a suitable location with plenty of sunlight for optimal growth.
2)  Prepare the soil by adding compost to improve fertility and drainage.
3)  Plant pumpkin seeds about 1 inch deep in rows spaced 6 feet apart.
4)  Water regularly, ensuring the soil stays moist but not waterlogged.
5)  Mulch around plants to retain moisture and suppress weeds.
6)  Support vines with stakes or trellises as they grow taller.
7)  Thin seedlings to one plant every 2 feet when they reach 6 inches tall.
8)  Apply balanced fertilizer once during the growing season for best results.
9)  Monitor for pests and diseases, treating early to prevent infestations.
10)  Harvest pumpkins when the skin is hard and the color is uniform.
11)  Store properly in a cool, dry place until ready to use or carve.
