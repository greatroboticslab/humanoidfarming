Task: Top 9.5 Things Make a BETTER Worm Farmer
Please break it into step-by-step subtasks.
------------------------------------------------------------
1) Choose a suitable worm species for your environment.
2) Provide consistent moisture levels in the compost bin.
3) Maintain a temperature between 60°F to 80°F for optimal activity.
4) Supply a balanced diet of organic materials like fruit and vegetable scraps.
5) Ensure proper ventilation to prevent odors and mold growth.
6) Regularly harvest mature worms from the compost bin.
7) Rotate bins to distribute worms evenly and maintain quality compost.
8) Monitor for signs of pests or disease affecting the worm population.
9) Educate yourself on best practices for sustainable worm farming.
