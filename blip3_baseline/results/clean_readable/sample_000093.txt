Task: Home Vegetable Garden Planting Guide
Please break it into step-by-step subtasks.
------------------------------------------------------------
1)  Choose a suitable location with at least six hours of sunlight daily.
2)  Prepare the soil by removing weeds and adding compost or organic matter.
3)  Decide on the vegetables to plant based on your climate and space availability.
4)  Purchase seeds or seedlings from a local nursery or online.
5)  Follow package instructions for planting depth and spacing.
6)  Water plants deeply but infrequently to encourage strong root growth.
7)  Mulch around plants to retain moisture and suppress weeds.
8)  Monitor for pests and diseases regularly, using natural remedies if possible.
9)  Harvest vegetables when they reach maturity for best flavor and nutrition.
10)  Rotate crops annually to prevent soil depletion and reduce pest buildup.
