Task: Preparing the Spring Garden
Please break it into step-by-step subtasks.
------------------------------------------------------------
1)  Assess the current state of the garden for any dead or diseased plants.
2)  Clear weeds and debris from the garden area.
3)  Prepare the soil by adding compost and adjusting pH levels if necessary.
4)  Plan the layout of new plants and flowers based on sunlight exposure.
5)  Purchase seeds or young plants according to the planned design.
6)  Start seeds indoors if planting season is early.
7)  Dig holes for new plants, ensuring proper spacing between them.
8)  Water newly planted areas thoroughly to settle the soil.
9)  Apply mulch around plants to retain moisture and suppress weeds.
10)  Install irrigation system if not already in place.
11)  Monitor for pests and diseases regularly.
12)  Prune overgrown plants and remove dead branches as needed.
