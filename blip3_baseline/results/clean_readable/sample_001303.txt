Task: A SIMPLE WAY TO CREATE AN OUTDOOR STORAGE SPACE
Please break it into step-by-step subtasks.
------------------------------------------------------------
1)  Choose a suitable location for your outdoor storage space.
2)  Measure the area to determine dimensions of the storage space.
3)  Decide on the materials needed for construction.
4)  Purchase necessary tools and materials for building.
5)  Lay down a foundation if required, ensuring stability.
6)  Construct the walls according to chosen design.
7)  Add a roof to cover the structure.
8)  Install doors and windows as desired.
9)  Seal all joints and gaps for weatherproofing.
10)  Decorate or paint the exterior for aesthetic appeal.
11)  Secure the storage space with locks and hinges.
12)  Organize items inside for easy access and maintenance.
