Task: Modern Tomato  Farming Techniques | Tomato Growing | Tomato planting Tips | Harvesting Tomato
Please break it into step-by-step subtasks.
------------------------------------------------------------
1) Identify suitable tomato varieties for local climate conditions.
2) Prepare soil rich in organic matter and proper drainage.
3) Plant tomatoes at least 18 inches apart to allow space for growth.
4) Water plants deeply but infrequently to encourage deep root development.
5) Apply balanced fertilizer every four weeks during growing season.
6) Monitor for pests and diseases regularly, using natural remedies when possible.
7) Prune plants to improve air circulation and reduce disease spread.
8) Support plants with stakes or cages as they grow taller.
9) Begin harvesting tomatoes when they reach full color and firmness.
10) Store harvested tomatoes in a cool place to extend shelf life.
