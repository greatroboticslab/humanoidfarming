Task: Southern Illinois Agronomy Observations
Please break it into step-by-step subtasks.
------------------------------------------------------------
1) Identify specific regions within Southern Illinois for agronomy observations.
2) Collect data on soil types and their characteristics in those regions.
3) Monitor weather patterns and climate conditions affecting agricultural activities.
4) Assess crop yields from previous seasons to predict future performance.
5) Evaluate irrigation needs based on current water availability and soil moisture levels.
6) Observe pest and disease occurrences to determine necessary control measures.
7) Document changes in plant growth stages and overall health of crops.
8) Note any unusual weather events or extreme temperatures impacting agriculture.
9) Gather information on fertilizer usage and nutrient deficiencies in the soil.
10) Record livestock health and feeding practices for sustainable farming methods.
