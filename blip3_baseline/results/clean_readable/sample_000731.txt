Task: Tractor Repair  #tractorrepair  #tractor #agriculture #farming #shorts
Please break it into step-by-step subtasks.
------------------------------------------------------------
1) Identify the specific issue with the tractor.
2) Gather necessary tools and parts for repair.
3) Turn off and disconnect the tractor's power source safely.
4) Disassemble the affected area carefully if needed.
5) Clean the area thoroughly to inspect for damage or debris.
6) Replace any worn-out or damaged components.
7) Reassemble the tractor following manufacturer guidelines.
8) Test the repaired part(s) to ensure proper function.
9) Lubricate moving parts as recommended by the manual.
10) Document the repairs for future reference and maintenance.
