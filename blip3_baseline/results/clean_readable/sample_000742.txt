Task: FARM SCHOOL: How to Do Sucessfully Farming In Africa "Simple Techniques"
Please break it into step-by-step subtasks.
------------------------------------------------------------
1) Identify suitable crops for African climate and soil types.
2) Start small with easy-to-grow crops like maize or beans.
3) Use organic compost for better soil fertility.
4) Practice crop rotation to maintain soil health.
5) Water plants regularly but avoid overwatering.
6) Mulch around plants to retain moisture and suppress weeds.
7) Introduce natural pest control methods instead of chemicals.
8) Harvest crops at peak maturity for maximum yield.
9) Save seeds from best-performing plants for next season.
10) Join local farming groups for knowledge exchange and support.
