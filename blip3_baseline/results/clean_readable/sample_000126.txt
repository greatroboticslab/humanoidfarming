Task: Orchid & Houseplants Pest Control Made Easy  DIY Garlic-Infused Alcohol Solution #ninjaorchids
Please break it into step-by-step subtasks.
------------------------------------------------------------
1) Gather garlic cloves, rubbing alcohol, and a spray bottle.
2) Peel and mince several garlic cloves finely.
3) Pour minced garlic into a spray bottle filled with rubbing alcohol.
4) Shake well to combine ingredients thoroughly.
5) Label the bottle as "Garlic-Infused Alcohol Solution.".
6) Spray solution onto houseplant leaves and stems gently.
7) Allow the solution to dry naturally on plants for pest control.
