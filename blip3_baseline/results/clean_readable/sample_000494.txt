Task: Mob Grazing Sandy Smart SD
Please break it into step-by-step subtasks.
------------------------------------------------------------
1) Identify areas of sandy land suitable for mob grazing.
2) Gather livestock in large groups to graze intensively.
3) Rotate livestock through different sections of the land daily or every few days.
4) Allow grass to regrow before moving livestock to prevent overgrazing.
5) Monitor soil moisture levels and adjust watering as necessary.
6) Evaluate results after several months to assess effectiveness.
7) Adjust grazing patterns based on observed outcomes and environmental conditions.
