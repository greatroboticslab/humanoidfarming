Task: "Surviving the Drought: Innovative Farming Techniques in Nigeria's Benin City "
Please break it into step-by-step subtasks.
------------------------------------------------------------
1)  Identify areas in Benin City most affected by drought.
2)  Research innovative farming techniques globally.
3)  Engage local farmers to discuss their current practices.
4)  Select three most promising techniques for implementation.
5)  Train local farmers on selected innovative techniques.
6)  Implement the chosen techniques in pilot farms.
7)  Monitor and evaluate the effectiveness of the new methods.
8)  Share results with other farmers in Benin City.
9)  Advocate for policy changes to support sustainable agriculture.
10)  Document the entire process for future reference and learning.
