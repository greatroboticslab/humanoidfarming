------------------------------------------------------------
1) Identify key regions within Central Tablelands for seasonal update analysis.

7) Collaborate with local agricultural experts for insights.
8) Implement pilot projects to test new sustainable farming techniques.
9) Monitor and assess the effectiveness of implemented strategies.
