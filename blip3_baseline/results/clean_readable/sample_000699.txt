Task: Agribusiness as a sustainable tool to mitigate rural-urban migration of youth in Kenya
Please break it into step-by-step subtasks.
------------------------------------------------------------
1) Identify key challenges faced by rural youth in Kenya regarding agriculture.
2) Assess current agricultural practices and resources available in rural areas.
3) Develop innovative agribusiness models that improve profitability and sustainability.
4) Implement training programs for rural youth on modern farming techniques.
5) Establish partnerships between local farmers and urban markets.
6) Create access to finance and credit facilities for small-scale farmers.
7) Promote technology adoption in agriculture to enhance productivity.
8) Encourage government policies supporting rural development and agriculture.
9) Organize community-based initiatives focusing on education and job creation.
10) Monitor progress and gather feedback from participants for continuous improvement.
