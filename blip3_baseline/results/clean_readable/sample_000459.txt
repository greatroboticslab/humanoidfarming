Task: Irrigation check list plan
Please break it into step-by-step subtasks.
------------------------------------------------------------
1)  Identify all irrigation systems in the area needing maintenance.
2)  Schedule regular inspections for each system at least once a month.
3)  Check water pressure and flow rates to ensure proper functioning.
4)  Inspect pipes and valves for leaks or damage.
5)  Clean filters to prevent clogging and improve water quality.
6)  Adjust sprinkler heads to correct any misalignment or blockages.
7)  Test soil moisture levels using probes or sensors before watering.
8)  Record data on weather conditions affecting irrigation needs.
9)  Plan adjustments based on seasonal changes in climate.
10)  Prepare a detailed report of findings and recommendations for improvements.
