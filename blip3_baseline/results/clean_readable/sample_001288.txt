Task: 6 Gardening Tips and Trick for Beginners
Please break it into step-by-step subtasks.
------------------------------------------------------------
1)  Start by selecting appropriate plants suitable for your climate zone.
2)  Choose a sunny spot in your garden or balcony for optimal growth.
3)  Prepare the soil by adding compost to improve its fertility and structure.
4)  Water plants regularly but ensure not to overwater, especially during dry spells.
5)  Apply mulch around plants to retain moisture and suppress weeds.
6)  Regularly inspect plants for signs of pests or diseases and treat accordingly.
