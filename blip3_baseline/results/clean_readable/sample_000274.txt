Task: Cover crop mix. What is the management goal?
Please break it into step-by-step subtasks.
------------------------------------------------------------
1) Determine the primary soil health objectives for the cover crop mix.
2) Identify specific pests or diseases to manage through the cover crop.
3) Assess the desired biodiversity enhancement in the agricultural system.
4) Plan for nutrient cycling and soil fertility improvement.
5) Consider weed suppression benefits from the cover crop mix.
6) Evaluate water retention and erosion control needs of the land.
7) Plan for efficient use of space and time within the growing season.
8) Decide on the timing of planting and termination relative to main crops.
9) Plan for harvesting and incorporating cover crops into the soil.
10) Establish monitoring protocols to assess effectiveness of the cover crop mix.
