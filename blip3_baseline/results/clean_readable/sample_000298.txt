Task: Grow More Give More: Planting a Raised Bed Garden
Please break it into step-by-step subtasks.
------------------------------------------------------------
1)  Choose a sunny location for your raised bed garden.
2)  Measure and mark the area where you want to build your raised bed.
3)  Purchase or build wooden frames for your raised bed.
4)  Fill the frame with a mixture of soil, compost, and sand.
5)  Decide on the vegetables or flowers you wish to grow.
6)  Start seeds indoors or purchase seedlings from a nursery.
7)  Transplant seedlings into your raised bed once they have grown.
8)  Water your plants regularly, ensuring the soil stays moist but not waterlogged.
9)  Mulch around plants to retain moisture and suppress weeds.
10)  Monitor for pests and diseases, using natural methods if possible.
11)  Harvest your crops when they are ripe and ready to eat.
12)  Rotate your crops each season to maintain soil health.
