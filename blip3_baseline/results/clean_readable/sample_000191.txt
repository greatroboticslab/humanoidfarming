Task: Introduction to Land-based Rainwater Harvesting - RUVIVAL Toolbox
Please break it into step-by-step subtasks.
------------------------------------------------------------
1) Identify suitable locations for rainwater harvesting based on rainfall patterns.
2) Design systems that can collect, store, and distribute water efficiently.
3) Choose appropriate materials for construction to ensure durability and longevity.
4) Implement filtration systems to remove contaminants before water use.
