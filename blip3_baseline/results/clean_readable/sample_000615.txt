Task: Corn Processing in Northeastern China  #shorts #cornprocessing #agriculture
Please break it into step-by-step subtasks.
------------------------------------------------------------
1) Identify corn varieties suitable for northeastern climate.
2) Harvest corn at optimal moisture levels.
3) Sort and clean harvested corn kernels.
4) Grind corn into cornmeal or corn flour.
5) Ferment corn to produce ethanol or lactic acid.
6) Mill corn to extract germ oil for biodiesel.
7) Process corn into animal feed pellets.
8) Package processed corn products for distribution.
