Task: SUSTAINABLE Tea: Key FARMING Practices  #ecofriendly #sustainablefarming #shorts
Please break it into step-by-step subtasks.
------------------------------------------------------------
1) Identify organic certification standards for sustainable tea farming.
2) Implement crop rotation to enhance soil health and reduce pests.
3) Use natural pest control methods instead of chemical pesticides.
4) Introduce biodiversity by planting diverse flora around tea plants.
5) Practice water conservation techniques such as drip irrigation.
6) Employ shade-grown tea cultivation to protect wildlife habitats.
7) Encourage local communities in sustainable farming practices.
8) Monitor and manage tea plant diseases through biological means.
9) Utilize composting and green manure to improve soil fertility naturally.
10) Harvest tea leaves at optimal times to ensure quality and sustainability.
