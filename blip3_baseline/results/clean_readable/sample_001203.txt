Task: Farm Life Benefits: Fresh Maize Harvest & Roasting
Please break it into step-by-step subtasks.
------------------------------------------------------------
1) Gather necessary tools for harvesting maize.
2) Locate maize plants in the field.
3) Use tools to cut stalks of maize.
4) Remove leaves from harvested maize.
5) Bundle maize stalks for transportation.
6) Transport maize to a designated area.
7) Prepare an open fire or grill for roasting.
8) Cut maize cobs off the stalks.
9) Place roasted maize on the fire or grill.
10) Roast maize until kernels pop and become golden.
11) Serve freshly roasted maize as a snack or meal.
