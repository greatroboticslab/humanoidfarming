------------------------------------------------------------
Water scarcity is a global issue.
It occurs when there is not enough water available to meet the needs of people, ecosystems, and industries.
This can happen due to overuse, pollution, or climate change.
Regions experiencing water scarcity face challenges in maintaining agricultural productivity, supporting human populations, and sustaining natural environments.
Efforts to address water scarcity include improving water management practices, increasing access to clean water, and implementing policies that promote sustainable water use.
