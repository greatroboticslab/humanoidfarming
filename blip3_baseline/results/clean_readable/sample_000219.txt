Task: Alabama Beef Systems Videos: How to Collect a Hay Sample
Please break it into step-by-step subtasks.
------------------------------------------------------------
1) Obtain a clean, dry container for collecting the hay sample.
2) Select several representative bales from different areas of the field.
3) Cut the bale in half lengthwise and remove the outer leaves.
4) Scoop out about one cup of hay from each half-bale center.
5) Mix all scoops thoroughly before placing them in the container.
6) Label the container with the date and location information.
7) Store the sample in a cool, dry place until testing.
