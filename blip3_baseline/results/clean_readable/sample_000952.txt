Task: Trap Crops - The Basics
Please break it into step-by-step subtasks.
------------------------------------------------------------
1) Identify pests that affect crops before planning trap crops.
2) Choose plants that attract or deter specific pests but do not harm beneficial insects.
3) Plant trap crops near susceptible fields to lure pests away from valuable crops.
4) Monitor trap crops regularly for pest infestations and adjust placement if necessary.
5) Harvest and destroy trap crop plants to prevent pests from spreading to other areas.
6) Rotate trap crops annually to maintain effectiveness against changing pest populations.
7) Integrate trap crops with other pest management strategies for comprehensive protection.
