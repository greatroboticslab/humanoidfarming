Task: Improving Your Soil One Amendment at a Time | From the Ground Up
Please break it into step-by-step subtasks.
------------------------------------------------------------
1)  Identify soil type and nutrient deficiencies through testing.
2)  Research amendments suitable for your specific soil.
3)  Calculate the amount of each amendment needed based on soil test results.
4)  Choose appropriate tools and equipment for application.
5)  Prepare the soil surface by removing weeds or debris.
6)  Apply amendments evenly using a spreader or by hand.
7)  Incorporate amendments into the topsoil layer with a tiller or fork.
8)  Water the amended area thoroughly to help the amendments settle.
9)  Monitor soil health over time and adjust as necessary.
