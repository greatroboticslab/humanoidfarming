Task: Assessing and Managing Water Use and Discharges From Aquaculture Facilities
Please break it into step-by-step subtasks.
------------------------------------------------------------
1)  Identify all water use points within the aquaculture facility.
2)  Determine the volume of water used for each point.
3)  Assess the quality of water before and after use at each point.
4)  Evaluate potential sources of contamination from these points.
5)  Calculate total water discharge from the facility.
6)  Review local regulations regarding water discharge limits.
7)  Implement measures to reduce water usage where possible.
8)  Develop strategies to treat wastewater before discharge.
9)  Monitor water quality regularly post-treatment.
10)  Report any non-compliance with regulations to authorities.
