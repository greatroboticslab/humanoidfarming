Task: Anatomy Of A Sprinkler System
Please break it into step-by-step subtasks.
------------------------------------------------------------
1) Identify the main components of a sprinkler system including valves, pipes, nozzles, and controllers.
2) Locate the water source, typically a well or municipal water supply.
3) Install and connect the water supply to the system's main line.
4) Position sprinkler heads in strategic locations around the area to be irrigated.
5) Connect each sprinkler head to the main line using appropriate fittings.
6) Set up the controller to regulate watering times and schedules.
7) Test the system for leaks and ensure proper operation before use.
