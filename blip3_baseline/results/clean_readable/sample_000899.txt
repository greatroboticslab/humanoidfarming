Task: Doyogena Manual row sowing of wheat
Please break it into step-by-step subtasks.
------------------------------------------------------------
1) Assess soil conditions for optimal wheat growth.
2) Prepare seedbed by removing weeds and debris.
3) Mix seeds with fertilizer according to recommended ratios.
4) Plant seeds at appropriate depth and spacing.
5) Cover seeds with soil gently using a tool.
6) Water the newly planted seeds thoroughly.
7) Monitor plants for signs of disease or pests.
8) Apply herbicides as needed to control weeds.
9) Harvest wheat when grains are fully matured.
10) Remove harvested wheat from field for storage.
