Task: Training Video (Safe use of Pesticides)
Please break it into step-by-step subtasks.
------------------------------------------------------------
1)  Identify the type of pest to determine appropriate pesticide.
2)  Read and follow all label instructions carefully.
3)  Apply pesticides during recommended times for best results.
4)  Wear protective clothing including gloves and goggles.
5)  Avoid spraying on windy days to prevent drift.
6)  Keep children and pets away from treated areas.
7)  Dispose of empty containers according to local regulations.
8)  Store pesticides out of reach of children and pets.
9)  Monitor treated areas for signs of overuse or misuse.
10)  Seek professional advice if unsure about pesticide use.
