Task: Pesticide Storage | From the Ground Up
Please break it into step-by-step subtasks.
------------------------------------------------------------
1) Assess pesticide storage needs based on quantity and type.
2) Choose a secure, dry location away from living spaces.
3) Install sturdy, tamper-proof containers for pesticides.
4) Label all containers clearly with product names and dates.
5) Ensure adequate ventilation to prevent fumes buildup.
6) Regularly inspect stored pesticides for expiration or damage.
7) Maintain records of all pesticide purchases and usage.
8) Train staff on proper handling and disposal procedures.
9) Implement safety protocols including spill response plans.
10) Secure the area with locks and alarms if necessary.
