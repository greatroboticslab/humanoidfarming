Garlic ferments permaculture living (non-monetised)
1) Identify suitable garlic varieties for fermentation.
2) Gather necessary materials such as jars, cheesecloth, and fermentation containers.
3) Prepare the environment by ensuring proper ventilation and temperature control.
4) Clean and prepare jars thoroughly.
5) Layer garlic cloves with salt in each jar.
6) Cover with cheesecloth and secure with a rubber band.
7) Place jars in a cool, dark place to ferment.
8) Monitor progress weekly, tasting periodically to assess readiness.
9) Once ready, transfer garlic to airtight containers for storage.
