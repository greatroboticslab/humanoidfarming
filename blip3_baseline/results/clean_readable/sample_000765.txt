Task: Autumn Awakening (and Garden Graveyard!): Preparing for Cool-Season Planting
Please break it into step-by-step subtasks.
------------------------------------------------------------
1) Assess local climate conditions suitable for cool-season plants.
2) Research specific plant species that thrive in autumn.
3) Purchase seeds or young plants from trusted sources.
4) Prepare garden beds by removing weeds and adding compost.
5) Ensure beds have adequate drainage and soil nutrients.
6) Plan planting schedule based on plant hardiness zones.
7) Water newly planted areas thoroughly to settle soil.
8) Mulch around plants to retain moisture and suppress weeds.
9) Monitor for pests and diseases regularly after planting.
10) Apply appropriate organic fertilizers as needed throughout season.
