Task: Regenerative Weed Management
Please break it into step-by-step subtasks.
------------------------------------------------------------
1) Identify weed species in the garden.
2) Determine the best herbicides for targeted control.
3) Apply herbicides according to product instructions.
4) Mulch around plants to prevent new weed growth.
5) Regularly inspect garden beds for emerging weeds.
6) Hand-pull or use a hoe to remove persistent weeds.
7) Incorporate organic matter like compost into soil.
8) Rotate crops annually to disrupt weed life cycles.
9) Use shade cloth to reduce sunlight exposure on soil.
10) Introduce beneficial insects that prey on weeds.
