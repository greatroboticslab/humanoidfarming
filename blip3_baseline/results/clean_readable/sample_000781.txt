Task: Growing Potatoes Through the Year | Produce Made Simple
Please break it into step-by-step subtasks.
------------------------------------------------------------
1)  Choose a suitable location with plenty of sunlight for growing potatoes.
2)  Purchase certified seed potatoes from a trusted supplier.
3)  Prepare the soil by adding compost or manure to improve fertility.
4)  Plant the seed potatoes about 6 inches deep in rows spaced 2 feet apart.
5)  Water regularly, ensuring the soil stays moist but not waterlogged.
6)  Mulch around the plants to retain moisture and suppress weeds.
7)  Monitor for pests and diseases, applying organic treatments if necessary.
8)  Harvest small tubers when they reach 2-3 inches in size.
9)  Continue harvesting as the plants grow larger, removing any damaged tubers.
10)  Store harvested potatoes in a cool, dry place until ready to use.
