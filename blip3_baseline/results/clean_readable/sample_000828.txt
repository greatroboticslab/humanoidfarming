Task: 7 Essentials TIPS for Raising Chicks
Please break it into step-by-step subtasks.
------------------------------------------------------------
1)  Choose a suitable brooder for your chicks.
2)  Provide clean water and fresh food daily.
3)  Maintain a temperature-controlled environment for the chicks.
4)  Keep the brooder clean to prevent disease.
5)  Gradually introduce the chicks to outdoor conditions.
6)  Monitor the chicks for signs of illness or distress.
7)  Provide adequate space for each chick to move freely.
