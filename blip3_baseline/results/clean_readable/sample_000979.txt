Task: Revolutionizing Modern Agriculture The Role of Robotic Technology
Please break it into step-by-step subtasks.
------------------------------------------------------------
1) Identify key challenges in modern agriculture.
2) Research robotic technology's potential solutions.
3) Analyze current robotic applications in agriculture.
4) Develop a prototype or select an existing system for testing.
5) Conduct field trials to assess effectiveness and efficiency.
6) Gather feedback from farmers and agricultural experts.
7) Refine the robotic system based on collected data.
8) Implement the improved robotic technology in pilot farms.
9) Monitor outcomes and gather long-term performance metrics.
10) Scale up successful implementations across various regions.
