Task: Small Farm Irrigation - Video 4 - How to Patch a Lay Flat Header Pipe
Please break it into step-by-step subtasks.
------------------------------------------------------------
1) Identify the damaged section of the lay flat header pipe.
2) Drain the water from the irrigation system.
3) Remove any debris or rust around the damaged area.
4) Clean the cut edges of the pipe thoroughly.
5) Apply a layer of primer to both cut edges.
6) Apply an appropriate sealant to the cut edges.
7) Align the cut edges precisely and press them together.
8) Secure the patch in place using clamps or tape.
9) Reconnect the lay flat header pipe to the rest of the system.
10) Test the repaired section for leaks before re-pressurizing.
