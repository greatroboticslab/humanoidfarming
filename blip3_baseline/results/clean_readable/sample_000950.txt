Task: FAO Animal Health Vaccination Campaign Against PPR in Syria
Please break it into step-by-step subtasks.
------------------------------------------------------------
1) Assess current vaccination coverage rates for Peste des Petits Ruminants (PPR) in Syria.
2) Identify regions most at risk for PPR outbreaks based on historical data.
3) Collaborate with local veterinary services to establish vaccination sites.
4) Develop a detailed vaccination schedule tailored to Syrian conditions.
5) Train local veterinarians and community health workers in PPR vaccination techniques.
6) Secure funding from international organizations to support the campaign.
7) Distribute vaccines and supplies to all identified vaccination sites.
8) Launch public awareness campaigns about the importance of PPR vaccination.
9) Monitor vaccination progress and effectiveness through regular check-ups.
10) Provide post-vaccination care instructions to farmers and herders.
