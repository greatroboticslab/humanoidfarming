Task: How to Prepare Organic Pesticides   Year 2022
Please break it into step-by-step subtasks.
------------------------------------------------------------
1)  Research organic pesticides suitable for your specific plants and pests.
2)  Purchase natural ingredients like neem oil, garlic, or hot peppers from local stores.
3)  Dilute the chosen ingredients according to package instructions for safe application.
4)  Apply pesticides during early morning or late evening when bees are less active.
5)  Cover treated areas thoroughly but avoid over-application to prevent resistance.
6)  Monitor plants regularly for effectiveness and signs of pests.
7)  Dispose of used pesticides safely in accordance with local regulations.
