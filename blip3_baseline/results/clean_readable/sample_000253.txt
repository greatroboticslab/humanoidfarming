Task: Small Garden Tips:  Keep Planting   SD 480p
Please break it into step-by-step subtasks.
------------------------------------------------------------
1) Start by choosing plants suitable for small garden spaces.
2) Regularly water plants to keep them healthy and thriving.
3) Apply mulch around plants to retain moisture and suppress weeds.
4) Rotate planting areas each season to prevent soil depletion.
5) Prune plants regularly to encourage bushier growth and health.
6) Use vertical space creatively with trellises or hanging baskets.
7) Incorporate herbs and edible plants for added functionality.
8) Consider companion planting to enhance plant health naturally.
9) Add decorative elements like stones or small sculptures for interest.
10) Plan for seasonal changes to ensure year-round beauty in your garden.
