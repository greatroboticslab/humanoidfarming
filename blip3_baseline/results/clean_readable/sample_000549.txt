Task: The Profitable Pursuit of Mango Farming #agribusiness
Please break it into step-by-step subtasks.
------------------------------------------------------------
1) Identify suitable mango varieties for local climate.
2) Conduct market research to understand demand and prices.
3) Plan the farm layout considering soil quality and sunlight exposure.
4) Purchase land and obtain necessary permits for farming.
5) Invest in irrigation systems and pest control measures.
6) Plant mango saplings following recommended spacing.
7) Monitor plant health regularly and apply appropriate fertilizers.
8) Harvest mangoes at optimal ripeness to maximize yield.
9) Package and store harvested mangoes properly for sale.
10) Establish a marketing strategy to reach potential buyers.
