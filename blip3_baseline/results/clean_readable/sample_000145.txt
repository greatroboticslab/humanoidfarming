Task: Pt 1 - 3 tips to make your homesteading self-sufficient lifestyle successful while staying debt free
Please break it into step-by-step subtasks.
------------------------------------------------------------
1)  Create a detailed budget outlining all income sources and expenses.
2)  Prioritize essential expenses and reduce non-essential spending.
3)  Set financial goals and create a plan to achieve them within your means.
4)  Start small with one or two self-sufficient projects to build confidence.
5)  Invest in quality tools and equipment that will last for years of use.
6)  Learn from online resources and local workshops to enhance skills.
7)  Establish relationships with neighbors and community members for support.
8)  Plan for emergencies by setting aside an emergency fund.
9)  Monitor progress regularly and adjust plans as necessary based on reality.
10)  Consider bartering goods and services with others for additional support.
