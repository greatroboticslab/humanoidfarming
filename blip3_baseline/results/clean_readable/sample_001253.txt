Task: Bluetongue virus of ruminants
Please break it into step-by-step subtasks.
------------------------------------------------------------
1) Bluetongue virus infects ruminant animals like sheep and cattle.
2) The virus is transmitted through midges, primarily Culicoides species.
3) Symptoms include fever, swelling in the face and neck, and bluish discoloration of the tongue.
4) Infected animals may experience reproductive issues and reduced milk production.
5) Diagnosis involves clinical signs, serological tests, and molecular methods.
6) Prevention includes controlling midge populations and vaccinating susceptible animals.
7) Treatment focuses on supportive care to manage symptoms and prevent secondary infections.
