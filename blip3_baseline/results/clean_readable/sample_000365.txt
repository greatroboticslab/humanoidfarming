Task: How to Start a Permaculture Food Forest #NaturalFarming #Shorts
Please break it into step-by-step subtasks.
------------------------------------------------------------
1) Choose a sunny spot with good soil drainage for your food forest.
2) Design layers of plants from ground up to canopy height.
3) Plant nitrogen-fixing trees like fruit trees and shrubs.
4) Integrate perennial vegetables and herbs between tree rows.
5) Include groundcovers such as clover or strawberries.
6) Incorporate vertical space with trellises for climbing plants.
7) Add companion plants that deter pests naturally.
8) Establish a diverse range of edible species for year-round harvest.
9) Consider companion planting zones for efficient use of space.
10) Prepare the soil by adding compost and organic matter.
11) Water regularly during establishment until plants are established.
12) Monitor and adjust the design based on plant growth and needs.
