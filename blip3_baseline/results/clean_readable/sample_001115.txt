Task: How to Take Care of My Cows Daily Routine for Top Care
Please break it into step-by-step subtasks.
------------------------------------------------------------
1)  Feed cows fresh hay and grain at designated times each day.
2)  Provide clean water in multiple accessible troughs daily.
3)  Check for any signs of illness or injury in cows.
4)  Brush cows regularly to remove dirt and promote skin health.
5)  Clean cow stalls daily to maintain hygiene and comfort.
6)  Monitor temperature and humidity levels in barns.
7)  Trim hooves as needed to prevent infections.
8)  Collect milk from cows according to schedule.
9)  Ensure all gates and fences are secure to prevent escapes.
10)  Provide shade during hot weather periods.
