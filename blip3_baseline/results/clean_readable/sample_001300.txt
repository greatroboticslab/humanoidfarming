Task: Gardening Tips for Beginner
Please break it into step-by-step subtasks.
------------------------------------------------------------
1)  Start by choosing a suitable location that receives at least six hours of sunlight daily.
2)  Select a variety of plants suited to your local climate and soil type.
3)  Prepare the soil by testing its pH level and adding necessary nutrients.
4)  Use proper gardening tools such as trowels, pruners, and watering cans.
5)  Plant seeds or seedlings according to the instructions on their packaging.
6)  Water plants regularly, ensuring they receive about an inch of water per week.
7)  Mulch around plants to retain moisture and suppress weeds.
8)  Apply organic fertilizers to promote healthy growth without over-fertilizing.
9)  Prune plants to maintain shape and encourage new growth.
10)  Monitor for pests and diseases, and treat them promptly if found.
11)  Harvest vegetables and fruits when they are ripe to encourage continued production.
12)  Learn from mistakes and adjust your gardening practices accordingly.
