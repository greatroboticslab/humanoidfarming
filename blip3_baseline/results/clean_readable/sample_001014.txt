Task: Harvesting Sunflowers seed
Please break it into step-by-step subtasks.
------------------------------------------------------------
1) Identify mature sunflower plants ready for harvesting.
2) Cut down the stalks of the sunflowers using pruning shears or a machete.
3) Remove the seeds from the flower heads by gently squeezing them to release the seeds.
4) Clean the seeds by removing any remaining chaff or debris.
5) Dry the cleaned seeds in a well-ventilated area until they reach a moisture content of about 10%.
6) Store the dried seeds in an airtight container away from direct sunlight.
