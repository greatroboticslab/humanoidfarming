Task: Ethiopia's Agricultural Transformation
Please break it into step-by-step subtasks.
------------------------------------------------------------
1) Identify key challenges in Ethiopian agriculture such as low productivity and limited access to markets.
2) Assess current agricultural practices and technologies used by farmers.
3) Develop targeted interventions to improve crop yields and livestock health.
4) Implement extension services to educate farmers on best farming practices.
5) Establish farmer cooperatives to enhance bargaining power and collective action.
6) Invest in infrastructure like roads and irrigation systems to support farming activities.
7) Promote sustainable land management techniques to prevent soil degradation.
8) Encourage research and development of drought-resistant crops suitable for local conditions.
9) Facilitate access to credit and financial services for smallholder farmers.
10) Create policies that incentivize private sector investment in rural areas.
