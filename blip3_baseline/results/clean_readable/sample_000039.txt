Task: Helping Farm Operators Build Labor Management Know-How
Please break it into step-by-step subtasks.
------------------------------------------------------------
1) Identify key areas of labor management for farm operations.
2) Assess current labor practices and identify inefficiencies.
3) Develop strategies to improve workforce scheduling and allocation.
4) Implement training programs for employees on time management skills.
