Task: Fall Gardening Essentials  5 Crops to Plant for a Bountiful Harvest!
Please break it into step-by-step subtasks.
------------------------------------------------------------
1)  Identify suitable fall gardening locations with ample sunlight.
2)  Purchase seeds or seedlings of crops like kale, broccoli, spinach, carrots, and radishes.
3)  Prepare soil by adding compost and ensuring good drainage.
4)  Water plants regularly to prevent drying out during cooler weather.
5)  Monitor for pests and diseases, applying organic treatments if necessary.
6)  Harvest crops as they mature to ensure a bountiful harvest throughout the season.
