Task: Person Milking a cow
Please break it into step-by-step subtasks.
------------------------------------------------------------
1) Approach the cow from behind.
2) Gently pat the cow's side to calm it.
3) Position one hand on the cow's neck and the other on its shoulder.
4) Use your dominant hand to grasp the cow's udder.
5) Pull the teats gently to release milk.
6) Move to another teat if needed.
7) Continue milking until the cow stops producing milk naturally.
8) Slowly release the cow's udder.
9) Clean hands thoroughly with soap and water.
