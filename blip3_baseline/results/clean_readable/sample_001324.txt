Task: How to Prevent Soil Erosion by Planting Sweet Potatoes
Please break it into step-by-step subtasks.
------------------------------------------------------------
1)  Choose sweet potato varieties known for their deep root systems.
2)  Prepare soil by adding compost or organic matter.
3)  Plant sweet potatoes in rows spaced at least three feet apart.
4)  Cover young plants with mulch to retain moisture.
5)  Water regularly, especially during dry periods.
6)  Monitor for pests and diseases, treating as needed.
7)  Harvest sweet potatoes when vines start to yellow.
8)  After harvest, leave the roots in the ground to regenerate.
9)  Rotate planting locations annually to prevent soil depletion.
10)  Consider intercropping with other crops to enhance soil health.
