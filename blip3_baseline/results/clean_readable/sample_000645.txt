Task: Bamboo Biochar
Please break it into step-by-step subtasks.
------------------------------------------------------------
1)  Identify suitable bamboo for biochar production.
2)  Harvest bamboo in a sustainable manner.
3)  Clean and dry the harvested bamboo thoroughly.
4)  Crush or chop the bamboo into small pieces.
5)  Heat the chopped bamboo in a low-oxygen environment.
6)  Monitor temperature to ensure it remains below combustion point.
7)  Allow the biochar to cool naturally after heating process.
8)  Screen or sieve the cooled biochar to remove impurities.
9)  Store the biochar in a dry, airtight container.
10)  Test the biochar's quality for use as a soil amendment.
