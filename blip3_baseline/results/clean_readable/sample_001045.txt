Task: PENYEMPROTAN RUTIN UNTUK MENCEGAH DAN MEMBASMI KUTU PUTIH #pertanian #durian #ediduren
Please break it into step-by-step subtasks.
------------------------------------------------------------
1) Identify all durian fruits needing treatment for kutu putih infestation.
2) Prepare a mixture of 1 part dish soap to 4 parts water.
3) Apply the solution directly onto the durian fruit surfaces using a cloth or spray bottle.
4) Ensure thorough coverage of both sides of each durian fruit.
5) Allow the treated durians to dry naturally before storing or consuming.
6) Repeat application every 7 days for three consecutive weeks for best results.
7) Monitor treated durians for any signs of kutu putih reinfestation.
