Task: Organic Pesticides | From the Ground Up
Please break it into step-by-step subtasks.
------------------------------------------------------------
1) Identify natural resources suitable for organic pesticide production.
2) Research and select plants known for their pesticidal properties.
3) Extract active compounds from selected plants using appropriate methods.
4) Test extracted compounds for effectiveness against target pests.
5) Develop formulations that ensure safe application to crops.
6) Implement integrated pest management strategies incorporating organic pesticides.
7) Monitor crop health and efficacy of organic pesticides regularly.
8) Educate farmers on proper use and benefits of organic pesticides.
