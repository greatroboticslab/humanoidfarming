Task: Charcoal retort in action
Please break it into step-by-step subtasks.
------------------------------------------------------------
1) Locate a suitable container for collecting charcoal.
2) Gather dry wood or other carbonaceous materials.
3) Prepare a fire inside the container using kindling.
4) Gradually feed the wood into the fire.
5) Maintain the fire until the wood begins to smolder.
6) Once smoldering, cover the container's opening with a lid.
7) Allow the heat to continue cooking the wood without oxygen.
8) After several hours, remove the lid to allow air circulation.
9) The charcoal will ignite spontaneously when exposed to air.
10) Collect the charcoal for use as fuel or in experiments.
