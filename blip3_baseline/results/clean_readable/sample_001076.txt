Task: Biodiversity and agronomy - Introduction
Please break it into step-by-step subtasks.
------------------------------------------------------------
1) Define biodiversity as the variety of life on Earth, including genetic diversity within species, species diversity, and ecosystem diversity.
2) Explain how biodiversity supports agricultural productivity through pollination, pest control, and soil health.
3) Discuss the importance of maintaining diverse ecosystems for sustainable agriculture practices.
4) Outline the challenges in balancing biodiversity conservation with agricultural needs.
5) Highlight the role of agroecology in promoting biodiversity-friendly farming methods.
6) Introduce the concept of integrated pest management as a biodiversity-friendly approach to agriculture.
