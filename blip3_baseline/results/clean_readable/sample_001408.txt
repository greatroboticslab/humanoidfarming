Task: (Tiv) Six steps to harvesting more than 20 tons per hectare of cassava with improved weed control
Please break it into step-by-step subtasks.
------------------------------------------------------------
1)  Select high-yielding cassava varieties resistant to common pests and diseases.
2)  Implement integrated pest management strategies including biological controls.
3)  Apply precise fertilization based on soil tests to enhance nutrient uptake.
4)  Use mulching techniques to retain moisture and suppress weeds effectively.
5)  Introduce mechanical weeding tools for timely removal of weeds without damaging roots.
6)  Monitor crop health regularly and adjust irrigation schedules as needed for optimal growth.
