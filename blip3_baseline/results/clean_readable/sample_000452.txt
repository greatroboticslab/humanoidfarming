Task: How to grow soybeans
Please break it into step-by-step subtasks.
------------------------------------------------------------
Plant soybeans in well-draining soil rich in organic matter.
Choose a planting time based on local climate and weather conditions.
Sow seeds about 1 inch deep and space them 6 to 8 inches apart.
Water the soil thoroughly after planting.
Apply a balanced fertilizer at planting time according to package instructions.
Monitor for pests and diseases regularly.
Provide support for climbing varieties of soybeans.
Harvest soybeans when they turn from green to yellow and feel firm.
Dry harvested soybeans in a cool, dry place or use a dehydrator.
Store dried soybeans in an airtight container away from moisture and light.
