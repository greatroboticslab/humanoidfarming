Task: How to start your own Bison herd!
Please break it into step-by-step subtasks.
------------------------------------------------------------
1) Research local laws and regulations regarding bison herds.
2) Identify suitable land for grazing and protection from predators.
3) Purchase or breed bison from reputable sources.
4) Set up fencing and water sources for the herd.
5) Acquire necessary permits for livestock management.
6) Learn about bison nutrition and feeding schedules.
7) Establish a veterinary care plan for the herd's health.
8) Prepare for potential weather changes and natural disasters.
9) Plan for marketing and selling bison products if desired.
10) Hire or train staff for daily herd management tasks.
