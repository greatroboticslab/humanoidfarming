Task: Combine harvester in action
Please break it into step-by-step subtasks.
------------------------------------------------------------
1) Identify suitable field conditions for a combine harvester operation.
2) Load grain storage containers onto the combine harvester.
3) Connect power supply to the combine harvester.
4) Start the engine of the combine harvester.
5) Adjust cutting height and other operational settings on the combine harvester.
6) Begin moving the combine harvester across the field.
7) Monitor the combine harvester's performance and adjust as necessary.
8) Collect harvested crops from the combine harvester's discharge chute.
9) Transfer harvested crops to the grain storage containers.
10) Repeat process until the entire field is harvested.
