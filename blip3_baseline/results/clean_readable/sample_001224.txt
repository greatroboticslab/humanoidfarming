Please break it into step-by-step subtasks.
------------------------------------------------------------
1) Identify the type of fruit to be harvested.
2) Plan the harvesting time based on ripeness.
3) Gather necessary tools like baskets or crates.
4) Wear protective clothing to avoid skin irritation.
5) Select a suitable harvesting method for the crop.
6) Begin harvesting from the top of the plant downwards.
7) Sort fruits according to quality before storing.
8) Ensure all picked fruits are safely transported home.
