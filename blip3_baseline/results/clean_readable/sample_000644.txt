Task: Farmers' Guide to High-quality Seed Production
Please break it into step-by-step subtasks.
------------------------------------------------------------
1)  Identify local high-quality seed varieties suitable for the climate and soil conditions.
2)  Obtain certified seeds from reputable suppliers to ensure genetic purity.
3)  Implement crop rotation and soil testing to maintain soil health and fertility.
4)  Use appropriate irrigation techniques based on the specific needs of the crops.
5)  Apply organic or chemical fertilizers as needed to support plant growth.
6)  Monitor crops regularly for signs of pests, diseases, or nutrient deficiencies.
7)  Harvest seeds at the optimal maturity stage to preserve quality.
8)  Clean harvested seeds carefully to remove debris and impurities.
9)  Store cleaned seeds in a cool, dry place away from light to prevent deterioration.
10)  Follow recommended storage guidelines to extend seed viability.
