Task: The Ingenious Agriculture of Ancient Mesopotamia
Please break it into step-by-step subtasks.
------------------------------------------------------------
1)  Identify ancient Mesopotamian civilizations such as Sumer, Akkad, and Babylon.
2)  Recognize the Tigris-Euphrates river system's importance for agriculture.
3)  Understand the development of irrigation systems to manage water resources.
4)  Learn about the creation of flood control measures to prevent soil erosion.
5)  Study the use of terracing techniques to cultivate steep slopes.
6)  Explore the cultivation of crops like barley, wheat, and dates.
7)  Discover the construction of ziggurats and other structures for agricultural purposes.
8)  Analyze the role of domesticated animals in farming practices.
9)  Examine the development of early writing systems to record agricultural knowledge.
10)  Investigate the impact of climate change on ancient Mesopotamian agriculture.
