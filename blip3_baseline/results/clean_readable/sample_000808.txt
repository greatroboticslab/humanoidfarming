Task: Good Agricultural Practices GAP For Microgreens
Please break it into step-by-step subtasks.
------------------------------------------------------------
1) Identify suitable microgreen crops based on local climate and soil conditions.
2) Design a clean and organized growing space with proper ventilation.
3) Use organic fertilizers and compost for sustainable soil health.
4) Implement water conservation techniques to minimize waste.
5) Regularly monitor and control pests and diseases using biological methods.
6) Ensure all equipment and tools are sanitized before and after use.
7) Maintain consistent temperature and humidity levels for optimal growth.
8) Provide adequate light exposure for photosynthesis and plant development.
9) Harvest microgreens at their peak maturity for maximum flavor and nutrition.
10) Store harvested microgreens in a cool, dark place to preserve freshness.
