Task: A Poultry Farmers Guide to FSA Loans
Please break it into step-by-step subtasks.
------------------------------------------------------------
1)  Research available FSA loans for poultry farmers.
2)  Determine eligibility criteria based on income and expenses.
3)  Prepare necessary documentation including financial statements.
4)  Complete an application form accurately and thoroughly.
5)  Submit the application through the appropriate channel.
6)  Await approval from the FSA or lender.
7)  If approved, negotiate loan terms and conditions.
8)  Receive funds and begin using them for business purposes.
9)  Maintain records of all transactions related to the loan.
10)  Repay the loan according to the agreed schedule.
