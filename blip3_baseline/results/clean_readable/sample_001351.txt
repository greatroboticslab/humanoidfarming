Task: Tighten that wire!  Tighten that wire hard!
Please break it into step-by-step subtasks.
------------------------------------------------------------
1) Identify the wire to be tightened.
2) Gather appropriate tools for tightening, such as pliers or wrenches.
3) Position yourself safely near the wire.
4) Loosen any existing tighteners or clamps on the wire.
5) Grasp the wire firmly with the chosen tool.
6) Apply steady pressure to the wire in a consistent direction.
7) Continue applying pressure until the wire is as tight as desired.
8) Double-check the tension of the wire to ensure it meets the required specifications.
9) Secure any loose ends or connections with additional clamps or ties if necessary.
10) Clean up any tools and materials used.
