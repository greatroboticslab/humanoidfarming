Task: Extreme Weather: Livestock Preparation Before the Storm
Please break it into step-by-step subtasks.
------------------------------------------------------------
Prepare shelters for livestock in safe areas.
Secure all loose items that could become projectiles during the storm.
Provide ample clean water to prevent dehydration.
Ensure access to food and replenish supplies if necessary.
Monitor weather forecasts closely for early warning signs.
Educate livestock handlers on emergency procedures and evacuation routes.
Inspect fences and gates for potential damage and reinforce as needed.
Secure or move livestock to higher ground if possible.
Establish communication plans with neighbors and local authorities.
Implement a plan for veterinary care in case of injuries or illnesses caused by the storm.
