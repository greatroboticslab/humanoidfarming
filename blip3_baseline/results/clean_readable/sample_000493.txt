Task: How to peel sweet corn easily | How to remove corn kernels in 1 minute | Peeling Trick |Simple Trick
Please break it into step-by-step subtasks.
------------------------------------------------------------
1)  Start by selecting fresh ears of corn from the cob.
2)  Place the ear of corn upright on a cutting board.
3)  Hold the ear firmly with one hand.
4)  Use a sharp knife to make a shallow cut around the entire length of the cob.
5)  Twist the knife slightly as you pull it off the cob.
6)  Repeat the process for each ear of corn.
7)  Once peeled, use your fingers to gently squeeze the kernels off the cob.
8)  Alternatively, place the cob in a bowl of cold water.
9)  Gently rub the kernels off the cob into the water.
10)  The kernels will float to the top, making them easy to collect.
11)  Use a slotted spoon to scoop out the kernels.
12)  Enjoy your freshly peeled and ready-to-eat corn kernels.
