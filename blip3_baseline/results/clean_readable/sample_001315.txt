Task: Linear Irrigation Systems
Please break it into step-by-step subtasks.
------------------------------------------------------------
1) Identify the irrigation system's water source.
2) Determine the land area to be irrigated.
3) Calculate the required flow rate for the system.
4) Choose appropriate sprinklers or drip emitters based on plant needs.
5) Install necessary valves and piping according to design specifications.
6) Connect the irrigation system to the water source.
7) Test the system for proper functioning and adjust as needed.
8) Program the controller for scheduled watering times.
9) Train users on how to operate and maintain the irrigation system.
10) Monitor system performance regularly for efficiency and effectiveness.
