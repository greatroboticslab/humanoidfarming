Task: Pesticidewise: minor pesticide spill
Please break it into step-by-step subtasks.
------------------------------------------------------------
1) Identify the type of pesticide spilled.
2) Determine the extent and location of the spill.
3) Wear appropriate personal protective equipment before handling.
4) Contain the spill if possible, using absorbent materials.
5) Avoid spreading the spill by preventing further contact or movement.
6) Report the spill to your supervisor or designated safety officer immediately.
7) Follow any specific cleanup instructions provided by the pesticide manufacturer.
8) Clean up the spill according to the manufacturer's guidelines and local regulations.
9) Dispose of all contaminated materials in accordance with environmental laws and waste management procedures.
10) Document the spill incident for future reference and training purposes.
