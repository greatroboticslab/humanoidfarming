Task: Our Morning Farm Routine With Horses Goats And Other Animals
Please break it into step-by-step subtasks.
------------------------------------------------------------
1)  Check weather forecast for morning activities.
2)  Prepare breakfast for all animals.
3)  Gather necessary tools and equipment.
4)  Walk to the farm with the horses.
5)  Feed horses fresh hay and water.
6)  Walk to the goat pen.
7)  Feed goats their daily ration of grain.
8)  Walk to the chicken coop.
9)  Collect eggs from the chickens.
10)  Walk to the pigsty.
11)  Feed pigs their meal.
12)  Walk around the farm checking on other animals.
13)  Record observations in the animal care log.
14)  Return home with collected eggs and any needed supplies.
