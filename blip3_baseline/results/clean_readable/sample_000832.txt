Task: Alternative Feeding For Cattle
Please break it into step-by-step subtasks.
------------------------------------------------------------
1) Identify cattle's nutritional needs based on age, weight, and health status.
2) Research suitable alternative feed sources like hay, silage, or grains.
3) Ensure alternative feeds are free from harmful contaminants.
4) Calculate appropriate feeding amounts for each animal.
5) Prepare alternative feed in proper quantities and consistency.
6) Provide alternative feed at consistent times daily.
7) Monitor cattle's response to new feed types and adjust as necessary.
8) Maintain clean feeding areas to prevent disease transmission.
