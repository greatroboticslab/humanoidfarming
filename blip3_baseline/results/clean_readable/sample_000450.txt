Task: Larger solar setup
Please break it into step-by-step subtasks.
------------------------------------------------------------
1) Assess current energy needs and available space for installation.
2) Research suitable solar panel types and their efficiency ratings.
3) Calculate required wattage based on daily energy consumption.
4) Determine optimal orientation and tilt angle for maximum sunlight absorption.
5) Plan wiring layout to connect panels efficiently to inverter.
6) Choose appropriate battery storage capacity if needed for off-grid use.
7) Select mounting system that fits roof structure securely.
8) Obtain necessary permits and approvals from local authorities.
9) Install grounding system to ensure safety and prevent electrical hazards.
10) Assemble and install solar panels according to manufacturer guidelines.
11) Connect inverter to convert direct current to alternating current.
12) Test entire system to ensure all components function correctly together.
