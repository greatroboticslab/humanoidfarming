Task: Rice cultivation//growing rice plants
Please break it into step-by-step subtasks.
------------------------------------------------------------
1)  Choose suitable land for planting rice.
2)  Prepare the soil by removing weeds and adding organic matter.
3)  Plant seeds in rows or use transplants depending on local practices.
4)  Water the fields regularly to keep them moist but not waterlogged.
5)  Apply fertilizers as per crop needs to promote healthy growth.
6)  Monitor for pests and diseases, using natural methods if possible.
7)  Harvest when grains turn from green to golden brown.
8)  Dry harvested rice in the sun or using mechanical equipment.
9)  Store in a cool, dry place to prevent mold and insect infestation.
10)  Plan next season's crop rotation to maintain soil fertility.
