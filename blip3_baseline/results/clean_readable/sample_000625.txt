Task: Learn how to grow maize
Please break it into step-by-step subtasks.
------------------------------------------------------------
1)  Choose a suitable location with well-draining soil and plenty of sunlight.
2)  Purchase high-quality maize seeds from a reliable source.
3)  Prepare the soil by removing weeds and adding compost or fertilizer.
4)  Plant seeds about 6 inches apart in rows spaced 30 inches apart.
5)  Water the plants deeply once a week, adjusting based on weather conditions.
6)  Monitor for pests and diseases, using organic methods if possible.
7)  Support taller plants with stakes or trellises to prevent lodging.
8)  Apply mulch around the base of the plants to retain moisture and suppress weeds.
9)  Harvest when the ears are fully colored and the husks start to dry slightly.
10)  Store harvested maize in a cool, dry place until ready to use.
