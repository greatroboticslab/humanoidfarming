Task: A Day in the Life of a Farmer  Raising Cows Chickens and Pigs in the Countryside
Please break it into step-by-step subtasks.
------------------------------------------------------------
1)  Wake up early to tend to morning chores including feeding livestock and checking water sources.
2)  Muck out stalls for cows, chickens, and pigs to maintain hygiene and health.
3)  Collect eggs from chicken coops and inspect for any signs of illness or injury.
4)  Walk through pastures to check on the well-being of cattle and observe their grazing patterns.
5)  Feed pigs fresh vegetables and grains, ensuring they have access to clean water.
6)  Observe piglets and chicks for any unusual behavior that might indicate disease or distress.
7)  Check barns for structural integrity and repair any damages to prevent future issues.
8)  Prepare feed mixtures for all animals based on nutritional needs and weather conditions.
9)  Clean pens and coop areas thoroughly to remove waste and reduce odors.
10)  Plan and implement strategies for pest control to protect crops and livestock from infestations.
