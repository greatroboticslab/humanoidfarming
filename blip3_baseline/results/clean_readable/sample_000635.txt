Task: High Tunnel Water Collection with Cherry Flowers
Please break it into step-by-step subtasks.
------------------------------------------------------------
1) Assess the location for optimal sunlight and drainage.
2) Install high tunnel frames using durable materials.
3) Attach water collection troughs to the tunnel's sides.
4) Position cherry flower plants within the tunnel.
5) Connect the troughs to a centralized water storage system.
6) Ensure proper ventilation for plant health and moisture control.
7) Monitor water levels regularly to maintain consistent irrigation.
8) Adjust watering schedule based on cherry flower growth stages.
9) Clean the water collection system periodically to prevent clogging.
10) Prepare for cherry blossom season to maximize water usage efficiency.
