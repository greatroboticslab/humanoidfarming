Task: MASTER RICE Transplanting Secrets Revealed!
Please break it into step-by-step subtasks.
------------------------------------------------------------
1)  Choose a suitable rice variety for your climate.
2)  Prepare the soil by turning over and adding compost.
3)  Plant seeds at the right depth according to instructions.
4)  Water the newly planted seeds thoroughly.
5)  Cover the seedlings with a shade cloth to protect them.
6)  Monitor for pests and diseases regularly.
7)  Thin out weaker seedlings to allow others to grow strong.
8)  Apply fertilizer as recommended for optimal growth.
9)  Keep the area weed-free to prevent competition.
10)  Harvest when the grains turn from green to golden brown.
