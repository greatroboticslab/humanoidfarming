Task: DO This To Make Worm Castings FASTER- 55 Gallon Worm Bin
Please break it into step-by-step subtasks.
------------------------------------------------------------
1) Choose a 55-gallon plastic bin as your worm bin.
2) Drill several small holes in the bottom for drainage.
3) Line the bottom with a layer of shredded newspaper or cardboard.
4) Add a thin layer of bedding material like coconut coir or vermiculite.
5) Scatter a handful of red wiggler worms over the bedding.
6) Distribute kitchen scraps evenly on top of the worms.
7) Cover the scraps with a thin layer of bedding material.
8) Repeat steps 5 through 7 until the bin is nearly full.
9) Maintain a moisture level similar to a damp sponge.
10) Turn the bin every few days to aerate and redistribute worms.
11) Harvest castings from the bottom of the bin after 6 months to a year.
12) Compost any remaining materials and start a new batch.
