Task: Farmers tell their stories: Laser leveling of rice fields in Southeast Asia
Please break it into step-by-step subtasks.
------------------------------------------------------------
1) Farmers in Southeast Asia use laser leveling to create flat rice fields for better water drainage.
2) This technique involves using lasers to precisely level the land before planting.
3) The leveled fields help prevent waterlogging and ensure even distribution of nutrients.
4) It increases crop yields and reduces labor costs for farmers.
5) Laser leveling also improves soil health and sustainability practices.
6) This method is adopted widely due to its benefits in agricultural productivity.
