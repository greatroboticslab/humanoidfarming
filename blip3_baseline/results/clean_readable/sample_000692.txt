Task: Burying Weeds to Prepare for a New Planting Season | THE DUC'S PLOW
Please break it into step-by-step subtasks.
------------------------------------------------------------
1) Assess the garden area thoroughly to identify all weeds.
2) Use a hoe or hand weeder to carefully remove each weed.
3) Dispose of removed weeds away from the garden to prevent regrowth.
4) Loosen soil in the planting area to prepare for new seeds.
5) Rake the soil surface to ensure even planting conditions.
6) Water the prepared soil gently to settle it and encourage germination.
