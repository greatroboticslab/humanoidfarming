Task: UNIT 1. Recording 14. What is agribusiness
Please break it into step-by-step subtasks.
------------------------------------------------------------
1) Agribusiness involves the production, processing, distribution, and marketing of agricultural products.
2) It includes various sectors such as farming, ranching, forestry, fishing, and food processing.
3) Agribusiness also encompasses the financial services, technology, and logistics industries that support the agricultural sector.
4) This field aims to improve efficiency, sustainability, and profitability in agriculture through innovative practices and technologies.
