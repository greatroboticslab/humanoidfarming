Task: Subsurface & Sprinkler Irrigated Corn
Please break it into step-by-step subtasks.
------------------------------------------------------------
1) Identify subsurface irrigation system placement for corn fields.
2) Assess sprinkler coverage area to ensure adequate water distribution.
3) Determine optimal planting density for efficient water use.
4) Select corn varieties suitable for both irrigation methods.
5) Prepare soil for planting, ensuring proper drainage for subsurface systems.
6) Plant corn seeds according to recommended spacing and depth.
7) Monitor soil moisture levels regularly using sensors or probes.
8) Adjust sprinkler irrigation frequency based on weather conditions.
9) Apply fertilizers as per crop needs and soil test results.
10) Harvest corn at appropriate maturity stage for best quality.
