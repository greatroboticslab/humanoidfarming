Task: The Sweet Corn Cultivation Revolution: Seed to Harvest
Please break it into step-by-step subtasks.
------------------------------------------------------------
1)  Identify suitable sweet corn varieties for local climate conditions.
2)  Purchase high-quality seeds from reliable suppliers.
3)  Prepare soil by testing pH levels and adding necessary nutrients.
4)  Plant seeds at recommended depths and spacings in prepared fields.
5)  Water plants regularly to ensure adequate moisture for growth.
6)  Monitor for pests and diseases using integrated pest management techniques.
7)  Apply organic or chemical fertilizers as needed for optimal growth.
8)  Support plants with stakes or cages to prevent lodging during heavy rains.
9)  Harvest ears when kernels are plump and milky but not mushy.
10)  Clean harvested corn thoroughly before storage or processing.
