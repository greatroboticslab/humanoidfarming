Task: How to Make a Powerful JADAM Natural Pesticide by mixing JWA+JS+JHS for Gardeners. Homemade
Please break it into step-by-step subtasks.
------------------------------------------------------------
1)  Collect equal parts of JWA, JS, and JHS.
2)  Mix them thoroughly in a large container.
3)  Add one tablespoon of dish soap for better absorption.
4)  Pour the mixture into a spray bottle.
5)  Shake well before each use.
6)  Spray on plants, focusing on undersides of leaves.
7)  Apply every 7 to 10 days or as needed.
8)  Store unused portion in a cool, dry place.
