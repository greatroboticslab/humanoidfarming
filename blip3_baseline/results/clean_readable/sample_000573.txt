Task: garden weeding tips and tricks #beginner #gardening #tips #tricks #weeds
Please break it into step-by-step subtasks.
------------------------------------------------------------
1) Start early in the morning or late afternoon to avoid direct sunlight.
2) Wear gloves to protect your hands from thorns and irritants.
3) Use a hoe to dig out small weeds before they spread.
4) Pull larger weeds by hand for easier removal.
5) Apply mulch around plants to prevent weed growth.
6) Regularly check garden beds for new weed sprouts.
7) Consider using organic weed killers if necessary.
8) Water plants deeply but less frequently to discourage weed growth.
9) Dispose of weeds properly to prevent spreading seeds.
10) Learn about common local weeds to identify them quickly.
