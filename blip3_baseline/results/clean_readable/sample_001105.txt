Task: Small Farm Seed Production: Tips from Driftless Seed Suppl
Please break it into step-by-step subtasks.
------------------------------------------------------------
1) Identify local climate and soil conditions suitable for seed production.
2) Choose high-quality seeds from reputable suppliers.
3) Implement crop rotation to maintain soil health.
4) Use organic fertilizers and natural pest control methods.
5) Monitor crops regularly for signs of disease or pests.
6) Harvest seeds at optimal maturity for best quality.
7) Store harvested seeds in cool, dry places away from light.
8) Follow local regulations regarding seed production and distribution.
9) Educate farmers on proper seed handling and planting techniques.
10) Consider starting a community seed bank for future generations.
