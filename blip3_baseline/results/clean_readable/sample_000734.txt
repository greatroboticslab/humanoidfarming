Task: 10: Hygiene in the shed? Let the cows tell you! Scoring system
Please break it into step-by-step subtasks.
------------------------------------------------------------
1) Determine the criteria for hygiene in a shed based on common practices for animal husbandry.
2) Identify the most suitable method to gather information from cows regarding shed hygiene.
3) Develop a scoring system that reflects the level of hygiene based on the responses from the cows.
4) Implement the chosen method to collect data from the cows about the shed's hygiene.
5) Analyze the data collected from the cows to determine their perception of the shed's hygiene.
6) Assign scores to the shed's hygiene based on the analysis of the cow responses.
7) Present the results of the scoring system to stakeholders or relevant parties.
