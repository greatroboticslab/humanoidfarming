Task: Sustainable Agriculture - science challenge
Please break it into step-by-step subtasks.
------------------------------------------------------------
1)  Identify key sustainability challenges in current agricultural practices.
2)  Research existing technologies and methods addressing these challenges.
3)  Analyze the environmental impact of different agricultural practices.
4)  Develop a prototype for an innovative sustainable farming system.
5)  Test the prototype under various climatic conditions and soil types.
6)  Collaborate with local farmers to implement and evaluate the system's effectiveness.
7)  Collect data on crop yields, water usage, and biodiversity improvements.
8)  Share findings and best practices through workshops and publications.
9)  Continuously refine the system based on feedback and new scientific discoveries.
10)  Advocate for policy changes supporting sustainable agriculture practices globally.
