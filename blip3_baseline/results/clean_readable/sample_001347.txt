Task: Grow your best Crops yet!  PRODUCT REVIEW: Soil test kit
Please break it into step-by-step subtasks.
------------------------------------------------------------
1)  Select a soil test kit suitable for local climate and crop type.
2)  Follow the kit's instructions carefully to collect soil samples.
3)  Mix the collected soil samples according to the kit's guidelines.
4)  Place the mixed soil sample in the designated area of the testing kit.
5)  Wait for the testing kit to indicate when results are ready.
6)  Interpret the results based on the kit's interpretation guide.
7)  Use the information to adjust soil pH and nutrient levels as needed.
8)  Apply recommended amendments to improve soil health for better crop growth.
