Task: Tomato Seedling Potting: How to Repot for Thriving Growth!
Please break it into step-by-step subtasks.
------------------------------------------------------------
1) Choose a pot with adequate drainage holes.
2) Fill the bottom of the pot with small rocks or gravel.
3) Add a layer of potting soil on top of the rocks.
4) Gently remove the tomato seedling from its current pot.
5) Loosen the roots if they are tightly packed.
6) Place the seedling in the new pot, ensuring it sits at the same depth as before.
7) Fill around the seedling with fresh potting soil.
8) Tamp down the soil gently to secure the plant.
9) Water the newly potted tomato seedling thoroughly.
10) Place the pot in a sunny location, ideally receiving at least 6 hours of direct sunlight daily.
11) Monitor soil moisture and water when needed, avoiding overwatering.
12) Apply a balanced fertilizer every few weeks during growing season.
