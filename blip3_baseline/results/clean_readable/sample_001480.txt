Task: Trenching Pipes & Sprinklers
Please break it into step-by-step subtasks.
------------------------------------------------------------
1) Assess the site for trenching pipes and sprinklers, considering soil conditions and utilities.
2) Plan the route of pipes and sprinkler lines, ensuring they meet design specifications.
3) Mark the trench location accurately using stakes or spray paint.
4) Dig the trench following the marked path, maintaining depth and width as specified.
5) Lay out the pipes and sprinkler lines in the trench, ensuring proper alignment.
6) Connect sprinkler heads to the lines, securing them tightly against the trench walls.
7) Backfill the trench gradually, compacting soil to prevent settling.
8) Test the system for leaks and functionality before finalizing installation.
