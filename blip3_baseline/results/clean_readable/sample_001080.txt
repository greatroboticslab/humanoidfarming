Task: HOW TO make a Gravity Fed Water Supply | What You Need.
Please break it into step-by-step subtasks.
------------------------------------------------------------
1)  Gather materials including a water tank, pipes, valves, and a gravity source like a rooftop.
2)  Install the water tank at a higher elevation than where you need the water supply.
3)  Connect the tank to the pipes using appropriate fittings.
4)  Position the pipes to direct water to the desired location.
5)  Add valves for controlling water flow and safety.
6)  Ensure all connections are secure and leak-free.
7)  Test the system by filling the tank and observing water flow.
