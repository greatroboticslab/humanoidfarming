Task: Cotton Farming 2020
Please break it into step-by-step subtasks.
------------------------------------------------------------
1)  Identify cotton varieties suitable for 2020 climate conditions.
2)  Plan crop rotation to prevent soil depletion.
3)  Prepare land by plowing and removing weeds.
4)  Apply organic fertilizers to enhance soil fertility.
5)  Install irrigation systems to ensure consistent water supply.
6)  Plant cotton seeds at optimal planting dates.
7)  Monitor cotton plants for signs of pests or diseases.
8)  Harvest cotton when fibers reach desired maturity.
9)  Sort harvested cotton to remove impurities.
10)  Package sorted cotton for storage or sale.
