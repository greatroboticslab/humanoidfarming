Task: Flood Irrigation
Please break it into step-by-step subtasks.
------------------------------------------------------------
1)  Assess the area to be irrigated for its size and shape.
2)  Determine the water source and calculate the required volume of water.
3)  Connect the irrigation system to the water source.
4)  Open the valves to start the irrigation process.
5)  Monitor the flow rate and adjust as necessary.
6)  Adjust the sprinklers or hoses to cover all areas evenly.
7)  Turn off the valves when the irrigation is complete.
8)  Clean and maintain the irrigation system for future use.
