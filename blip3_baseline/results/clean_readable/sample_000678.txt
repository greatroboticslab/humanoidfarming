Task: Harvesting Mini Bok Choy - Cabbage
Please break it into step-by-step subtasks.
------------------------------------------------------------
1) Assess the growth stage of the mini bok choy cabbage to determine when to harvest.
2) Check for firmness in the leaves, indicating maturity.
3) Gently pull the plant from the soil if it comes out easily.
4) Trim off any yellow or damaged leaves.
5) Cut the main stem about an inch above the ground.
6) Wash the harvested mini bok choy cabbage thoroughly under running water.
7) Dry the leaves on a clean towel before storing or preparing.
