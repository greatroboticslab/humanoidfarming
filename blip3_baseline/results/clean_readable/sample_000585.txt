Task: May Garden Prep Guide: Essential Tips for Preparing Your Garden for Planting
Please break it into step-by-step subtasks.
------------------------------------------------------------
1)  Assess your garden's soil type and nutrient levels.
2)  Remove any weeds or debris from the planting area.
3)  Test the soil pH to determine if amendments are needed.
4)  Add compost or organic matter to improve soil structure.
5)  Rake the soil surface to ensure even distribution of amendments.
6)  Decide on the plants you wish to grow and their planting dates.
7)  Prepare containers or raised beds according to plant needs.
8)  Water the soil thoroughly before planting to settle in amendments.
9)  Plan irrigation systems based on plant water requirements.
10)  Apply mulch around plants to retain moisture and suppress weeds.
