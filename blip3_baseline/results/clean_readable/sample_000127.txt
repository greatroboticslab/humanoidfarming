Task: Harvesting Sweet Potatoes at Don's Produce
Please break it into step-by-step subtasks.
------------------------------------------------------------
1)  Wear appropriate protective gear including gloves and sturdy shoes.
2)  Check the sweet potato fields for ripe crops.
3)  Use a digging fork to carefully extract the sweet potatoes from the soil.
4)  Sort sweet potatoes by size and quality for market display.
5)  Pack sorted sweet potatoes into boxes or crates for transportation.
6)  Ensure all harvested sweet potatoes are free of dirt and debris.
7)  Label each box clearly with the contents and date of harvest.
8)  Load the boxes onto a transport vehicle for delivery to the market.
