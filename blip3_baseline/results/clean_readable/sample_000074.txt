Task: How to Buy a Cheap Used Tractor
Please break it into step-by-step subtasks.
------------------------------------------------------------
1) Research local classifieds and tractor auctions for available models.
2) Inspect tractors in person to check mechanical condition and overall appearance.
3) Negotiate price with seller based on inspection findings and market value.
4) Arrange financing or payment plan if needed for the purchase.
5) Schedule delivery or arrange transportation of the tractor.
6) Have the tractor inspected by a professional mechanic before use.
7) Register the tractor according to local laws and regulations.
