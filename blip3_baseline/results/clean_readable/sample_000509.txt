Task: Hurricane - Livestock Preparation Before the Storm
Please break it into step-by-step subtasks.
------------------------------------------------------------
Prepare shelters for livestock.
Secure all loose items outside that could become projectiles during the storm.
Feed livestock adequately before the storm to ensure they have enough energy.
Move livestock to higher ground if possible and safe.
Ensure all necessary medications and veterinary supplies are readily available.
Close and secure all doors and windows in livestock areas.
Disconnect electrical appliances from power sources to prevent electrocution risks.
Monitor weather forecasts closely and be prepared to act quickly if conditions worsen.
Have a plan in place for emergency evacuation of livestock if necessary.
Inform neighbors or local authorities about your livestock situation and needs.
Clean and disinfect livestock areas after the storm to prevent disease spread.
