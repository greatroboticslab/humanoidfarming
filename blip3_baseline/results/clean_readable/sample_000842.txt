Task: Mechanical and Machinery hazards | Contact With Moving Parts of Equipment
Please break it into step-by-step subtasks.
------------------------------------------------------------
1) Identify all moving parts in the machinery or equipment.
2) Assess the potential for contact with these moving parts.
3) Determine appropriate protective measures to prevent contact.
4) Train operators on safe handling procedures involving moving parts.
5) Install guards or barriers around moving parts as necessary.
6) Regularly inspect and maintain equipment to ensure safety features are operational.
7) Educate employees about the risks associated with contact with moving parts.
