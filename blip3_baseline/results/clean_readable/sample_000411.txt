Task: Shiro Plum  My First Fruit & How You Can Grow One Too #shorts
Please break it into step-by-step subtasks.
------------------------------------------------------------
1) Identify Shiro Plum trees in local nurseries or online.
2) Choose a suitable planting location with plenty of sunlight.
3) Dig holes twice as wide but the same depth as the tree's root ball.
4) Plant the tree at the same depth it was in the nursery pot.
5) Water thoroughly after planting to settle soil around roots.
6) Mulch around the base to retain moisture and suppress weeds.
7) Provide regular watering during dry spells for the first year.
8) Apply balanced fertilizer once a month from spring through summer.
9) Prune branches lightly each winter to encourage healthy growth.
10) Monitor for pests and diseases, treating promptly if needed.
