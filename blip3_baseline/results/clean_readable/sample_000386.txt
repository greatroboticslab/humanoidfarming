Task: Brown spots on leaves? No problem!!
Please break it into step-by-step subtasks.
------------------------------------------------------------
1) Identify the type of plant to determine suitable treatment.
2) Check for environmental factors like humidity or sunlight exposure.
3) Inspect for pests or diseases that may cause brown spots.
4) Remove affected leaves to prevent spread.
5) Apply appropriate fungicides or insecticides as needed.
6) Increase air circulation around plants to improve conditions.
7) Water plants at soil level to avoid wetting foliage directly.
8) Ensure proper drainage to prevent waterlogged soil.
9) Use mulch to retain moisture and suppress weeds.
10) Monitor plants regularly for signs of improvement or new issues.
