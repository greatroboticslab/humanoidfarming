 Are you worried about low rice yields? Let's fix that with top transplanting tips. Rice is a staple crop and transplanting it right can make all the difference. Here's what you need to know. First, timing is crucial. Transplant your seedlings 25, 35 days after sowing for the best results. Next, prepping your paddy field. Level it well and add organic manure to enrich the soil. When it's transplant time, you have two options, manual or machine transplanting. For manual, space seedlings 20 by 20 centimeters apart. For machine, follow the manufacturer's guidelines. Post-transplant care, keep your field flooded for the first 7, 10 days to help seedlings establish. Also, watch out for pests and weeds, address them promptly. Proper transplanting leads to stronger plants and better yields. Follow these tips and watch your rice fields thrive. If you found this guide helpful, like and share it with fellow farmers.
