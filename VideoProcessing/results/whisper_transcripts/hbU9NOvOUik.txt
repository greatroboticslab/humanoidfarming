 Here's five gardening tips from some farmers. Strawberry edition. Number one, clip your runners to increase fruit production. Number two, plant your strawberries in well-drained soil. Number three, strawberries are pretty heavy feeders. So just like us, you want to make sure you feed them often. Number four, space your strawberry plants 12 to 18 inches apart. Number five, harvest frequently to maintain consistent fruit production.
