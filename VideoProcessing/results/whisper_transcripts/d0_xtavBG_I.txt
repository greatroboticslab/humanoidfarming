Baking Soda as a Fungicide and Insecticide - Natural Plant Protection

Howto & Style
 Yes, baking soda is a fungicide as well as an insecticide. It can be used to treat powdery mildew, a plant fungi. Sprinkling baking soda on the base of your plants will prevent powdery mildew from forming and stop roaches and ants from climbing your plants.
