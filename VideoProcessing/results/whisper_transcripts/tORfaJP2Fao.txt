 Accounting in agriculture refers to the process of recording, classifying and reporting financial information related to agricultural activities. It involves tracking and managing the financial aspects of farming such as income, expenses, assets, liabilities. By applying accounting principles to agricultural activities, farmers and agricultural businesses can optimize their financial performances, reduce risks and achieve long-term sustainability as well as maximize profits.
