 Aquaponic greenhouses are an innovative and sustainable method of food production that combines aquaculture and hydroponics in a closed-loop system. In this system, fish and plants are grown together in a symbiotic relationship where the fish waste provides nutrients for the plants, while the plants filter and purify the water for the fish.
