 Crave Brothers Farmstead Classics are a variety of delicious, award-winning cheeses. These Kraft cheeses have been recognized as best in class in local, national, and even international competitions. Crave Brothers Fresh Mozzarella recently swept the U.S. Championship Cheese Contest with best of class, second and third place awards. Their chocolate mascarpone was recently awarded Grand Champion Cheese at the World Dairy Expo Championship Dairy Products Contest. Crave Brothers mascarpone and chocolate mascarpone have rich and sweet cream flavors with a smooth texture. Thought of as a dessert cheese, it also blends perfectly in soups and sauces, and pairs well with strawberries, chocolate, and sparkling wine. Crave Brothers Fresh Mozzarella has a delicate, milky flavor with a firm texture. This Mediterranean classic comes packed in water or in marinade. It can be used in countless recipes, such as caprese salads, pasta salads, paninis, or as a pizza topping. Fresh Mozzarella is delicious paired with fresh tomatoes and fruit, or wrapped with prosciutto. Crave Brothers, Farmer's Rope, and Oaxaca Cheeses are a fun-to-eat variety of mozzarella. Great for snacks, these cheeses are also perfect on pizza, quesadillas, and nachos. They pair well with sliced apples, a nice pilsner, or sparkling apple cider. The award-winning cheeses from Crave Brothers are sold nationwide in grocery stores, delis, specialty markets, and fine restaurants. And for those who appreciate locally sourced food, Crave Cheese can even be found at farmers' markets. Handcrafted with a passion for cheese, the taste of family pride.
