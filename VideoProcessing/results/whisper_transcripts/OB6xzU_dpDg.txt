 In recent years, smart farming technologies have emerged as game changers in agriculture. Drones equipped with advanced sensors are now being used to monitor crop health and optimize resource use. Additionally, IoT devices are collecting real-time data on soil conditions, which enables farmers to make informed decisions. A remarkable innovation is the use of AI-driven analytics, allowing for predictive maintenance of equipment and pest control. This integration of technology not only boosts yields but also promotes sustainable farming practices, paving the way for a greener future in agriculture.
