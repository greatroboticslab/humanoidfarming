 While many farmers globally are relying on groundwater and other pumped resources to properly water their crops, the environmental effects of water waste are a big problem in agriculture. Install better watering systems. Fluttering with drip irrigation save up to 80% more water and water gets to the plant's roots. Choose more drought-tolerant crops. If plants are native to your region, it will be more likely to succeed in natural weather conditions such as periods of drought. Store rainwater.
