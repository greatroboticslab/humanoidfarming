Gaming
 Stop shooting small deer. In the Hunter Call of the Wild, when you shoot an animal, it will eventually respawn into something of similar size. So when you shoot low level animals, you will respawn more low level animals. Instead, try shooting only higher estimates of your target species. This will lead to smaller respawns, effectively shooting down your population. After you shoot down the population on your map, the game will try to balance itself, leading to consistently higher level respawns, meaning more diamonds and, if you're lucky, maybe even a great one, which is why you should...
