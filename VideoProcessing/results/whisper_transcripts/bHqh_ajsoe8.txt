 Is climate change killing our crops, droughts, heat waves, floods, climate change is hitting our farms hard? Crop yields are shrinking, soil is degrading, and extreme weather is becoming the new normal. Maize, wheat, barley, staples of our diet are at risk. Each degree of warming could wipe out up to 10% of global harvests. This means less food on shelves and higher prices for everyone. But there's hope. Growing food indoors, where the climate can be controlled and adjusted, and smart monitoring of crops can help ensure greater crop yields and quality produce.
