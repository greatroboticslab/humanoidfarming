 Ever seen a lone thorny tree in a field like this? That's a hawthorn, one of the most sacred trees in Celtic and Irish folklore. Known as the fairy tree, it guards portals to the otherworld. People say if you cut one down, something bad follows. In Ireland, entire roads have been rerouted to avoid harming a single hawthorn. But it's also a tree of love, fertility, and healing, especially on Beltane May Day. Why do we still fear this tree? Find out in the full video on our channel and stay enchanted.
