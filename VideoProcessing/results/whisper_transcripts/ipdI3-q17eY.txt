 To shop the MMPA Farm Supply Store online, visit mimilk.com in your preferred web browser. After the page loads, select Farm Supply Store at the top center of the page. On the home page of the MMPA Farm Supply Store, you can browse relevant product categories. To view products, select the appropriate category, or, if you know exactly what product you're searching for, you can use the search bar that appears above the product or category listings. Select a product to view its photo, price, and description. Some products have multiple options, such as different sizes, colors, or combinations, so you can select the drop-down menu to choose what you're looking for. To purchase a product, select Add to Cart. At this point, if you want to continue shopping, you can select the MMPA Farm Supply Store logo in the top left, or select from a category on the left-hand side of the screen. To proceed with checking out, select View Cart, and then, at the bottom of the screen, select Proceed to Checkout. Next, you can add your billing and shipping information and file for tax-exempt status if appropriate. At the bottom of the screen, enter your payment information. Members and haulers, you can pay via check deduction by entering your hauler or producer number.
