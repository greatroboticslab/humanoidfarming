 Explorers of the Unknown, welcome back. Let's expand our horizons with today's question. Organic alternatives to chemical fertilizers include using compost, manure, bone meal, and fish emulsion. Compost is rich in nutrients and helps improve soil structure. Manure adds organic matter and enhances soil fertility. Bone meal provides phosphorus, which is essential for root development, while fish emulsion offers a quick nutrient boost for plants.
