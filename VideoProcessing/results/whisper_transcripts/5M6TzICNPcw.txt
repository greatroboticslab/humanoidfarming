 It may look like crude oil, but it's actually the secret ingredient behind the milk you love. This is molasses, a natural, energy-rich syrup that cows can't resist. It's made from sugar cane, and instead of being discarded, farmers turn it into a livestock supplement. But for cows, it's more than just a treat. It also improves digestion, increases milk production, and makes their boring food more palatable. The result? Healthier and happier cattle. A simple byproduct with a big impact. The result?
