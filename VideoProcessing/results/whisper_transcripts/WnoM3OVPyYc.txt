5 Vegetables to Grow in the Shade
https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=WnoM3OVPyYc
Howto & Style
 Five vegetables that grow well in the shade. Spinach is a cool season crop and it doesn't like the hot summer months. You can actually extend your harvest by planting it in the shade by several weeks. Pok Choy is another vegetable that can easily be grown in the shade. Celery grows nice leafy greens as well as a decent stalk you can juice, eat, or put in foods. Swiss chard adapts quickly to growing in the shade and it comes in several colors. Pinks, reds, whites, greens, and oranges. Leaf lettuces also do well in the early spring and into early summer. It doesn't like the heat so planting it in the shade can extend your harvest.
