 Farmers should always ask suppliers for EBC certified biochar if they plan to apply it to fields. The EBC differentiates between various types of certified biochar EBC agrobio suitable for agricultural applications including composting and direct soil use EBC feed approved for use as feed additive and livestock nutrition EBC construction approved for use in materials like concrete and asphalt If you're a farmer EBC-AgroBio is the label to look for It guarantees that the biochar has been tested for all relevant parameters pH, heavy metals, pH, surface area and stability And is safe to use on your fields
