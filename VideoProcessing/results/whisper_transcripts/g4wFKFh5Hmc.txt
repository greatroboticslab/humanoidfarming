 Join me on a charming journey to Marker Miller Farm Market, nestled in the picturesque Shenandoah Valley in Frederick County, Virginia. From the sprawling orchards where you can pick your own apples and peaches, to the tempting aroma of fresh apple cider donuts, this family-friendly destination is a treat for all senses. Discover their homemade pies, delicious fudge, and the crisp taste of freshly made donut holes. But there's more. Explore the wonderful selection of local Virginia wines, perfect for taking a piece of the valley home with you. Spend some time outdoors in the expansive play area, perfect for kids, or relax in the scenic picnic area as you soak in the beautiful surroundings. Whether you're looking for a fun family outing or a sweet escape into the countryside, Marker Miller Farm Market promises a memorable day full of flavors and smiles.
