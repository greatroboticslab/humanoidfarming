 Welcome to Update Bowling Green. In today's edition, we'll discuss the 2020 Community Garden Rentals. Community garden plots will be available for rent March 11th at 8.30am at Bowling Green Parks and Recreation's Administrative Office at 225 East 3rd Avenue. There are 26 large plots with a limit of one plot per renter and 22 small plots with a limit of two plots per renter. Garden plot rentals are first come, first served. All garden plots are located at Cariagas Park at 1220 Fairview Avenue. A large plot is 30 by 60 with a cost of $20 and a small plot is 30 by 30 with a cost of $15. For more information, please visit www.bgky.org or call 270-393-3642. You're watching Update Bowling Green.
