 Want amazing compost? Here are the four key ingredients. First up, carbon. Microbes are what cause decomposition and carbon provides the fuel microbes need to thrive. Second is nitrogen. It's like a protein-packed superfood that powers microbial growth. Third is water. It keeps the microbes hydrated. Fourth is oxygen. Mixing the pile adds air for the microbes to breathe.
