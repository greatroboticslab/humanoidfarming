World's largest indoor farm machinery show returns to Ky. Expo Center this month
https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=530kCBCpNnY
News & Politics
 The biggest indoor farm show is set to return to the Kentucky Expo Center soon. The 59th National Farm Machinery Show is set for February 12th through the 15th. Attendees will be able to explore the newest equipment for every part of the agriculture industry. The trade show uses every part of the Expo Center's 1.2 million square feet. Over 800 exhibitors will be there showcasing brands including Case, John Deere, Kubata and New Holland. The show is expected to make more than a $27 million economic impact.
