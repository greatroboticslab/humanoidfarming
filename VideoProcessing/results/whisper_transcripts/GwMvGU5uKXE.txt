How China Clean Millions Of Solar Panels In Desert?
https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=GwMvGU5uKXE
Science & Technology
 Ever wondered how China keeps millions of solar panels clean in the middle of a desert? They use genius little robots, powered by the sun itself. Each one has its own tiny solar panel on top, which charges it while it cleans the others. And when it's time to clean itself, easy, it's just one small panel that need one guy to clean it under one minute.
