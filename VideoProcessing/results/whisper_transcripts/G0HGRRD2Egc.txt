 Why is nobody talking about this? One in four adults has a disability. So if you need gardening to be more accessible because of chronic pain like I do, here's five of my favorite tools that make gardening easier. This travel takes pressure off your hand with a vertical handle that doubles as a dibber. I know, the jokes make themselves. A vertical planter keeps you from having to bend or worry about weeding. Self-watering pots like these OYAs make it so you never have to spend hours on your feet watering. A foam pad and kneeler that flips over to make a seat allows you to change positions often so your joints don't lock up. And with a retractable hose reel, you can surface water your plants and skip the exhausting work of coiling it up when you're done.
