 AgroSilver Pastoral System is a sustainable and integrated land-use management approach that combines agriculture, forestry, and livestock farming within the same land area. This system is designed to optimize the ecological, economic, and social benefits derived from these three components. The term AgroSilver Pastoral is derived from Agro for Agriculture, Silvo for Forestry, and Pastoral for Livestock Farming. Components of AgroSilver Pastoral Systems Agriculture Agro In the AgroSilver Pastoral System, traditional crops or cash crops are cultivated alongside trees and pastures. This integrated approach helps to improve soil fertility, reduce erosion, and enhance overall agricultural productivity. Crop residues can also be used as fodder for livestock. AgroSilver Pastoral Systems represent a holistic and sustainable approach to land management, leveraging the synergies among agriculture, forestry, and livestock farming. These systems offer a range of environmental, economic, and social benefits, addressing the challenges of food security, climate change, and biodiversity loss.
