 Silvopasture is simply a combination of a trees and livestock where both benefit from each other and produce its own products. Silvopasture system can be divided according to the way trees are planted which is closely related to their use and benefits. Each system is also suitable for different types of trees and livestock. These systems are commonly used to reduce strong wind or make a boundary of land. Its product can be firewood, biomass or fruits. In these systems trees has a lot of space where livestock is more likely to be a supplement. It can be fruit or timber trees. This system is mostly used in home gardens or by small farmers. Mostly large forests with a high diversity livestock, cows, deers and other where trees are used to provide timber or biomass. Properly set and managed Silvopasture system can create both economic and environmental benefits. However trees are long-term resource which means that it takes some time to make actual economic benefit as well as once reached its constant in both economic and ecological side of benefits. Although there is one immediate benefit. Trees are shelter for animals. Trees provide shade, reduce wind and cold exposure and encourage natural behavior in animals. That leads to improved animal welfare and possible increased productivity. With trees come additional costs associated with establishment and management especially at the beginning. Trees need to be protected by shelters from being bitten and debarked by animals which adds additional costs and they need to be pruned regularly.
