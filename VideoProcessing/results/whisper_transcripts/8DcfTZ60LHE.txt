 Agronomy is the science of soil management and crop production. It has evolved over centuries, focusing on maximizing agricultural productivity. Key principles include soil fertility, crop rotation, and pest management. Agronomists play a crucial role in ensuring sustainable and high-yield crop production. Soil management and fertility are vital for healthy plant growth and yield. Modern agronomy integrates technology like precision farming and drones for efficient practices. Challenges include climate change and the need for more sustainable practices. Research in agronomy leads to innovations like drought-resistant crops for sustainable agriculture.
