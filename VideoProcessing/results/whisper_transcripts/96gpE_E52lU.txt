 By organizing a short-term operating plan, you can keep your farm running if a crisis strikes. Hi, I'm Katie Nooner, Agricultural Business Field Specialist, with this University of Missouri Extension Farm Management Minute. A short-term operating plan helps families to continue managing the farm business, even if the primary operator can't make day-to-day decisions due to illness, injury, or other difficulties. In your plan, include the details your family would need to prevent interruptions in the farm's operations for a two-week to six-month period. MU Extension has a short-term operating plan template available to print or save online.
