 I was two-day years old when I learned that you can use iron for cleaning purposes. Iron cleans spilled piece wax. Iron the damaged material on high setting between two paper towels. Iron will suck out piece wax into the towel, then throw into the washing machine and wash with hot water. Read important nuances in captions.
