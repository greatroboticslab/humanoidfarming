 Whether you're in agriculture or research, Biora by Minarch Systems has a growth chamber that will fit to your specific needs. Our growth chambers have been specifically designed to allow for the development, refinement, and repetition of specific growth conditions within the biotechnology, agriculture, and science fields. The modular and transportable nature of our rooms allow companies to scale up facilities when lab space isn't readily available without compromising on safety and functionality.
