Before starting, make sure that the all levers are in neutral positions. Depress the clutch with your left foot until it hits the floorboard. Choose your speed, either high or low speed. With the clutch depressed with your foot, push in on the key and turn it until the tractor begins to turn over. Move the shifter. Move the transmission forward. Move the gear. Move the gear.
