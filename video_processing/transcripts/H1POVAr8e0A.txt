Science & Technology
 FarmBot Genesis is an all-in-one farming machine designed for individuals who want to grow their own crops. Operating autonomously, it can perform various tasks, from seeding to weeding, using a web-based interface. It identifies and eliminates weeds using a camera, making home farming more accessible and efficient.
