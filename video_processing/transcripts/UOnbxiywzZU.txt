 From hand-plowed soil to AI-powered harvests, agriculture has transformed our world. Let's explore farming's incredible journey. 10,000 years ago, humans began farming in the Fertile Crescent, cultivating wheat, barley, and lentils. Ancient innovations like irrigation, crop rotation, and plows boosted yields. The 18th century's seed drill and mechanized threshers sparked the agricultural revolution. The Green Revolution fed billions with high-yield crops and new technology. Today, GPS GMOs and smart irrigation maximize efficiency and conserve resources. AI, robotics, and sustainability are shaping the farms of tomorrow. From ancient fields to smart farms, agriculture's journey is greener than ever.
