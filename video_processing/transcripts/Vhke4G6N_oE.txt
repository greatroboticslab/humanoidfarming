And also, in African soil, we have high diversity of benefits soil microorganisms that can be used to boost soil fertility in Africa. From our project, we come out with a product known as Rhizobia Mycorrhiza-Biofetilizer. So this product is the combination of treated urban and rural waste combined with benefits soil mycorrhiza, microorganisms. For example, Rhizobia, which is known to fix nitrogen from the atmosphere. And the mycorrhiza, which is known to mobilize soil phosphorus. The technology behind the product is the techniques of waste management, but also techniques for isolating and conditioning benefits soil microorganisms. The work starts from collection of waste and isolation of soil microorganisms. So, waste are processed in the laboratory. And we have apparatus, we have equipment that can allow us to process safely and in big quantity fertilizers. And after that, we isolate microorganisms from soil, treat it, and then combine it with the waste to produce our fertilizer.
