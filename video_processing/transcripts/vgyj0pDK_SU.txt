How to Prevent Soil Erosion

Howto & Style
 For a quick erosion fix, add mulch or rocks to weigh your soil down. If you have a steep slope, try mulch mat for a secure option. Prevent long-term soil erosion by covering the bare ground with turf, ornamental grass, or spreading shrubs. Avoid walking over your soil, which can lead to further erosion.
