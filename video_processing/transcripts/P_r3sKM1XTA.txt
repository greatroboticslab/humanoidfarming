 First in the list are the shovel. A shovel is an essential agricultural and gardening hand tool. It is a multi-purpose tool, serving various jobs at once. Shovel tool helps with digging and transplanting soil, making shallow trenches. Second is the knapsack sprayer. Knapsack sprayers are indispensable agricultural tools. Understanding how to use them is essential for the successful application of agricultural chemicals, especially selective post-emergence herbicide. Third is Bolo. The main use of the Bolo is clearing vegetation, whether for agriculture or during trailblazing. Bolo is used for cutting big grasses and weeds and chopping branches of trees. Fourth equipment is axe. Axe is a multi-purpose cutting tool used for felling and delimbing of trees, splitting of logs for firewood and dressing of logs for timber conversion. Small axes are also used for clearing of bushes. Fifth and the last home agricultural equipment is sudsad or scrapper. A Filipino weeding knife with a very short and wide dull blade with a perpendicular blunt end. Sodsad, or small hand bolo is the effective use to clear vegetation, also at a traditional use of Filipino farmers on digging and mixing soil.
