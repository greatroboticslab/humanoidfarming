 Hello Friends! आज मैं आपको differentiate between subsistence farming and commercial farming बता है। Substence farming में done for domestic need, done on small lands, done by primitive methods, done by manual tools or less produced. Commercial farming done for market profit, done on large lands, done by modern methods, done by commercial machines and more produce.
