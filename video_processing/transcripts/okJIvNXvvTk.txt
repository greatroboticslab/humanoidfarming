Science & Technology
 Soil conservation and fertility management. Soil, the foundation of life. Yet, soil erosion and nutrient depletion threaten global food security. Conservation tillage and cover cropping reduce soil disturbance, preserving its structure and fertility. Crop rotation and intercropping promote soil biota, increasing nutrient cycling and availability. Organic amendments like compost and manure enrich soil, supporting beneficial microorganisms. Soil testing and analysis inform nutrient management planning, ensuring optimal fertilizer application. By adopting these practices, farmers can conserve soil, boost fertility, and cultivate a resilient agricultural ecosystem. Healthy soil, healthy planet, healthy future. Don't forget to subscribe.
