Growing Apple Trees from Store-Bought Apples
https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=5hQ12br3tVs
Howto & Style
 First, take a good quality apple. Take a piece of aloe vera leaf and rub it on the back of the apple. Use an egg as a root booster for more roots and add small pieces of banana into the egg. Take soil in a pot, pour the mixture of banana and egg into the soil, then plant the apple in it. Give water and cover the pot, then keep the pot in safe zone. After 12 days, you will see that the roots have grown very beautifully. Now, it can be planted in the garden. Let it lightens the Voldemort on one side. Now let's use a plant to blend in the garden.
