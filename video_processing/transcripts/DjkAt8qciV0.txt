When it comes to animal rest, that's something we've forgotten about, we don't focus on, and we really have to keep in mind that if animals are in muddy conditions, they're going to be standing for many more hours than normal. Thirty to thirty-five cows and their fall calves spend the winter months in this compost bedded pack barn.
