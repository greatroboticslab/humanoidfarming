 Popular vegan influencer Jana Samsonova who ate only exotic fruits has died of starvation at age 39. The Russian national attracted millions of views after sharing her controversial diet online. Samsonova claimed she ate a completely raw vegan diet for the last four years consuming just fruits, sunflower seeds, sprouts, fruit smoothies and juices. The 39 year old shared a chilling message on Instagram just days before she allegedly died of starvation. She shared this caption over a photo of herself.
