The benefits of using compost in commercial farming
https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=9e8nu7cRJJY
Science & Technology
 Compost is a very useful addition to soil. The main benefits are that it supplies the biology in the soil. So the biology is really absolutely critical to the health of the crop. It supplies nutrients, it protects the crop against stress and also ensures much faster growth. So one of the other advantages though is that it improves the physical properties of the soil. So you can get changes in water holding capacity. You can also improve the drainage rate of the soil. And so it affects the plant with its biology and also with its access to nutrients and to water.
