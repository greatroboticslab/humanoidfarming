 Did you know that while you're clicking next looking for a base out in multiplayer battles, if you wait until at least 28 seconds you'll instantly find a base? Let's test that theory real fast. When we click next at 30 seconds, it will cause the cloud option search for opponents to populate for a few seconds. But if we wait to 28 seconds, the base instantly pops up.
