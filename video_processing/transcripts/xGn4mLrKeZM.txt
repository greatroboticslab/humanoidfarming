 Rooting agent doesn't need to be bought with money. You can make it at home simple and practical. Remember to like and bookmark that is in 500 milliliters of warm water. Add a spoon of red sugar and a spoon of yeast powder. After stirring, evenly seal and store for 24 hours. The homemade rooting water is completed.
