 The agricultural dealers traditionally have been equipment sales. Some do rental, but probably not as many take advantage of what rental can do for them. They might use some of the used equipment they already have sitting on their lot. Utilizing those assets can increase additional profit. It brings customers into the dealership that we might have been ignoring because we didn't offer that particular choice to the customers. Rental can also be a way of introducing some customers to product that they haven't tried before. New technology and new models of equipment. If we rent it for a while, they might like it so much they want to buy it. New technology and new models of equipment.
