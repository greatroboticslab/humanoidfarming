 Agricultural Consultant is under MLTSSL and the skills assessing body for this role is VETASSESS. So what do they do? Agricultural Consultant advise farmers, agricultural businesses, rural industries and government on the production, processing and distribution of farm products. Specializations can be an Agricultural Extension Officer, Land Care Officer, Horticultural Consultant or Agribusiness Consultant. So to get a positive skills assessment from VETASSESS, you should satisfy two things. The first is the qualification. You should have a bachelor's degree. That's related to the nominated occupation. The second thing that you need to satisfy is that you have to have work experience in that field with at least 12 months. 20 hours per week is acceptable and you should be performing the tasks of an Agricultural Consultant. The full list of those tasks are available at the VETASSESS website. They actually have an info sheet for this.
