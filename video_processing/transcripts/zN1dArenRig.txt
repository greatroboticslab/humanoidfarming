 Dridegger Farms Market. Mouthwatering BC Farm Fresh Berries. Bring nutrition home with fresh berries from our market. You pick or we pick. Nothing tastes fresher. Our market is open from 8am to 6pm daily during the season. We have plenty of parking for everyone. Did we mention fresh? Eat them as is. Make your favourite jam, pie or fruit smoothie. The choice is yours. Bring the whole family. Kids, school tours and grandparents, we all have a great time. You can visit Dridegger Farms at 23823 72nd Avenue in Langley, British Columbia. That's just off the intersection of 72nd Avenue and the Trans-Canada Highway in Langley. Thank you for buying BC.
