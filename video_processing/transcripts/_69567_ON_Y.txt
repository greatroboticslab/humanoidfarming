Considering this and taking into consideration the poor content of organic substances detected from the soils, the introduction of the greening practice has started following the suggestion of the CNR and the Fondazione Archeologia Arborea. The three main goals of this practice are, firstly, the use of cover crops in the field that are easy to manage and also able to give productive return. Secondly, the creation of a better soil structure, increasing the organic substances and the water holding capacity, having therefore an impact on the soil humidity level. And thirdly, the clear reduction or even the drastically cut off of the use of herbicides. The water quality of the soil is also affected by the soil humidity.
