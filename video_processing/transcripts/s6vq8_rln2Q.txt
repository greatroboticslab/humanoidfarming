 Even with technological advancements from seed to plate and the highly efficient food value chains, there are still challenges to solving the hunger crisis. Agricultural Economics and Agribusiness Management looks to address these issues by appointing extraordinary agricultural economists and agribusiness managers to work on solutions in our food and agricultural value chains. If you enjoy working in an office environment with business operations but love the outdoors and understanding the science behind agriculture, then a BSc Agricultural Economics and Agribusiness Management degree is worth considering. This degree is ideal for students passionate and competent in both science and business subjects. Our program provides you with an overview of the global food and agricultural sector and cultivates problem solvers with unique skill sets that are able to help feed and clothe the world. There is a famous quote by Brenda Shook, Once in your life, you need a doctor, a lawyer, a policeman and a preacher. But every day, three times a day, you need a farmer. In today's fast-changing world, opportunities and careers extend far beyond the farm. These include careers in banking and finance, policy design and implementation, research and consultation, food processing and manufacturing, and commodity trading and logistics, to name a few. All of these aligned to address the growing problem of feeding the world. Never in our lifetime has food and agriculture been as significant as it is today, making this degree a highly valuable option.
