 11. Agriculture. In agriculture, AI is increasing efficiency and sustainability. Drones and sensors monitor crop health and soil conditions, providing real-time insights to farmers. Precision agriculture tools analyze this data to optimize irrigation, fertilization, and pest control, boosting crop yields while reducing waste. AI is helping farmers feed the world more sustainably.
