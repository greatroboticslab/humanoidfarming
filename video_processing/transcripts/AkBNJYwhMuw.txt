 To grow tomatoes in hydroponics, the easiest method is the Kratky system. Start by drilling holes into the lids of black buckets, then add a drainage hole to the side. Prep your seedlings, add some lecker to net pots, insert your tomatoes, then fill to the top with some more lecker. Mix hydroponic NPK and magnesium sulfate fertilizers and add this to the buckets, then add dissolved calcium nitrate.
