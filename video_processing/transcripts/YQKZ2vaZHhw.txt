75. Adoption of science on farms and behaviour change
https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=YQKZ2vaZHhw
People & Blogs
 In the heart of rural India, a transformation is taking place. Science, once seen as distant and complex, is now a trusted ally on the farms. A shift is occurring from traditional methods to scientific ones. Seeds genetically modified for resilience and productivity are replacing old variants. Drones monitor crop health, while precision agriculture ensures optimal use of resources. The result? A sustainable, productive farm that feeds more than just a village. This isn't just about technology. It's about changing behaviours, embracing the future and empowering our farmers. Because when science thrives, we all thrive.
