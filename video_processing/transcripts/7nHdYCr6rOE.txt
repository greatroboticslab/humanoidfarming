Education
 Step one, put a sweet potato in a box or a container. Step number two, cover the sweet potato. Step three, water it in and wait two to three weeks. Step four, cut the slip. Step five, place it in a jar of water and wait till the roots form. Step six, it's now time to plant. Look how nice that look. Never buy sweet potato slips again.
