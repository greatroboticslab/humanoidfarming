 How to build a hydroponic system in 30 seconds. Get a mason jar and fill it up with water. Add hydroponic plant food to the water. Place a hydroponic basket in the mason jar. Pull out a seedling with all of its roots. Clean off any excess dirt. Insert the seedling into a cloning collar. Place the cloning collar into the hydroponic basket. Move the system under natural or artificial light. Boom! Now you have your own hydroponic system.
