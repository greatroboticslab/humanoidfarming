 The Role of Agricultural Economics in Sustainable Farming Practices Agricultural economics is a field that applies economic principles and analysis to study the production, distribution, and consumption of agricultural goods and services. It focuses on understanding the economic factors, policies, and market dynamics that shape the agricultural industry. Agricultural economics explores the intersection of agriculture and economics, examining the economic aspects of farming, including farm management, production efficiency, market behavior, and the impacts of government policies on agricultural markets.
