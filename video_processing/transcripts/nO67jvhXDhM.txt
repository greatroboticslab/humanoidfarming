 Let's address the biggest question an aspiring cattle farmer should ask themselves. What will I do with the cattle I raise? If you want to raise beef cattle on a small scale, there are two main ways to make money. First, you can sell the beef from your steers. Sell it directly to consumers, wholesale to a store, or through a farmer's cooperative. The second way to make money with beef cattle is by raising steers. Then sell the cattle to a finishing farm, often a feedlot farm.
