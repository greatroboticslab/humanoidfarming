 Let's talk about strawberries really quick. If you want a strong, healthy plant, pick those flowers so that your plant will develop deep roots. I like to flip the unripe fruit up out of the soil so the bugs don't get it. And strawberries love to be watered well, so keep them well mulched to retain moisture in the soil.
