 Here's how you can take control of bugs and insects around your farmyard. First, there is a BT specifically designed for mosquitoes and black flies. You can put these tablets in standing water around the farm to kill these pests. Second, use dry imidacloprid. That's the active ingredient from gaucho seed treatment and spread it on your lawn once in the spring and once in the fall. Immediately water it in with an inch of rain or irrigation. Imedacloprid is from the nicotine family and is relatively safe to humans and animals. Tempo and Talstar are synthetic reproductions of the insect killing portion of the chrysanthemum flower.
