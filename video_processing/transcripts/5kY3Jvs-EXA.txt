Howto & Style
 How to grow hydroponic lettuce. Spray sponge cubes. Sow seeds under grow light. Seven to 14 days later, place cubes in nutrient solution. Move to windowsill, optional. Seven to 10 days later, tiny roots emerging. Thin seedlings to one or two per cube. Three weeks later, feel free to harvest full plants or three leaves at a time and let them regrow.
